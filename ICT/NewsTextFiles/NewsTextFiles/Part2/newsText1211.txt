
Tourism cabinet secretary Phyllis Kandie will visit china next week to woo more Chinese nationals to visit Kenya. The visit follows travel advisories against Kenya by Britain, the US and two other countries that traditionally originate the bulk of foreign tourists. It comes just a day after President Uhuru Kenyatta said the government will seek alternative tourists markets to mitigate the advisories’ effect.  Bella Genga reports.
