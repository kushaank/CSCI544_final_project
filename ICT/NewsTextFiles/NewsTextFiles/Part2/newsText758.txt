A man has been arrested in connection with two sexual assaults in St Andrews last month.

Women in the Fife town were advised not to walk alone at night after a 27-year-old was attacked on Wednesday, April 9.

The next day there was another indecent assault on an 18-year-old student on the town's university campus.

Police appealed for information in the wake of the incidents and released CCTV footage of a man they wanted to trace.

A 30-year-old man has now been arrested in connection with the attacks and is due to appear at Cupar Sheriff Court on Tuesday.

A police spokesman said: "Police Scotland can confirm that a 30-year-old man is in custody in connection with two serious sexual assaults which took place in St Andrews last month.
