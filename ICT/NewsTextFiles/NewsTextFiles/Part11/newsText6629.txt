
LAHORE: Federal Railway Minister Khawaja Saad Rafique warned on Monday that democracy would not last if people were not given relief this time.

If people dont get any relief this time, neither democracy will last nor any general will come, he said, while speaking to media persons here.

Rafique said that baton-wielding people from Katchi Abidis would come out and spare no one. Instead of criticizing each other, politicians should focus on the betterment of people, he opined.

The railway minister said that former military ruler Pervez Musharraf would not be able to escape courts. I had sought Rs50 billion in the next budget. But I will get only Rs31 billion, he added.
