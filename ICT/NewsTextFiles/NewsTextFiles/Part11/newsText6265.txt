
North Korea's nuclear and missile programs will also make up the bulk of talks in Singapore later this month between the defense chiefs of South Korea, the U.S. and Japan.

South Korea's defense ministry says Kim Kwan-jin, Chuck Hagel and Itsunori Onodera will meet on May 31st, on the sidelines of the 13th Asia Security Summit.

Although details will be finalized next week, the threat posed by North Korea is expected to lead discussions.

The sharing of military intelligence will also likely be on the table, as Seoul has been pushing for the three sides to sign an MOU on the matter.
