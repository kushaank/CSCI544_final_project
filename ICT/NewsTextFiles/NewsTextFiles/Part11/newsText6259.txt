
Shanghai, May 18 / Kabar /. Kyrgyz President Almazbek Atambayev met with President of the People's Republic of China Xi Jinping.

The official welcoming ceremony is held in Xijiao state residence.The ceremony was also attended by spouses two leaders - Raisa Atambayeva and Peng Liyuan.

Following the meeting, the sides signed a number of bilateral documents.
