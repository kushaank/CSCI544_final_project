This is stated in the Declaration on further deepening of the strategic partnership jointly signed today between President of the Kyrgyz Republic Almazbek Atambayev and President of China Xi Jingping.

Today in Shanghai Xi Jinping and Almazbek Atambayev reached broad understanding on bilateral relations as well as international and regional issues of mutual interest.

Two leaders appreciated the significant achievements since the establishment of the strategic partnership between the two countries and expressed opinion that political trust and strategic cooperation, practical cooperation and humanitarian ties are at their highest historical level. The sides stressed that further deepening of the Kyrgyz- Chinese strategic partnership is in the fundamental interests of the two countries and their peoples foster peace, stability and development in the region.
