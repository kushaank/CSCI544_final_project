
Bharat Petroleum Corporation Ltd ( BPCL ) has signed a deal with ADNOC, national oil company of the United Arab Emirates , to import 60,000 barrels per day (bpd) oil in 2014-15.

Also BPCL , along with its subsidiary Bharat Oman Refineries Ltd (BORL), has also signed a deal with Kuwati Petroleum Company to buy 84,000 bpd oil in the fiscal year to March 31, 2015.
