
New Delhi: The Aam Aadmi Party (AAP) has ruled out the  possibility of it forming government in Delhi again, even as Congress  said that it would prefer fresh polls to supporting AAP.

Lieutenant Governor Najeeb Jung had not favoured dissolution of the 70-member Delhi Assembly as recommended by the Council of Ministers and kept the House in suspended animation.
