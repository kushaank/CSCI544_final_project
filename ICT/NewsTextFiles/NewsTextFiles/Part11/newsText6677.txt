
Ghana meets Naija returns in its fourth year, with an even bigger and more exciting package for music fans in both Ghana and Nigeria and the world at large. This year’s edition slated for Saturday 24th May 2014 at the Accra International Conference Centre is set to feature performances from seasoned musicians, including Sarkodie, Shatta Wale, Castro, Guru, AK Songstress, Davido, Kcee, Wizboyy and Selebobo.

Having been undoubtedly one of the biggest musical concerts in Ghana over the last three years, organizers have assured that this year’s episode will be bigger, even more spectacular and is sure to give music fans an experience truly to relish.

The ball will be set rolling on that night with Chris Attoh as the Master of Ceremonies (MC).

Chris Attoh is a television show host, actor and meteoric MC. He is a multi- talented TV personality who has steadily risen into stardom to become one of the promising actors Africa can boast of.

Among Chris’ CV are TV productions: ‘Stars of the Future’ and ‘Rhythms’, Tinsel (a Mnet’s production), which is the first multi camera soap to be done in West Africa, specifically Nigeria.
