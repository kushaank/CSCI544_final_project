A 62-year-old man from Manhattan is suing thousands of people, organizations and business for more money than exists on the planet, according to video from buzz: 60.

In a 22-page, handwritten lawsuit, Anton Purisima accuses of variety of businesses and people of wrongdoings, including Kmart,  a Latina whose dog bit him, NYC Transit and two local hospitals.

According to the New York Post , it is unclear why some defendants are being sued, but he does specify an incident in which a "Chinese couple" took unauthorized photos of him of after he was bitten by a dog.

Purisima, who is representing himself, seeks two undecillion dollars - a 2 followed by 36 zeros, buzz:60 says.
