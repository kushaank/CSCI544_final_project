
ATLANTA — A new dean has been chosen to lead Emory University’s business school.

Officials said Monday that Erika Hayes James was chosen to lead the Goizueta Business School after a national search, and will begin her term July 15.

James comes to the Atlanta campus from the University of Virginia Darden School of Business, where in 2012 she was named senior associate dean for executive education.

In a statement, James says she hopes to strengthen Emory’s connections with Atlanta’s business and nonprofit communities, and with other universities.
