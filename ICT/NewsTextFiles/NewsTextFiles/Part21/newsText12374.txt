
Suzanne Baxter has quit as Company Secretary immediately, although she will continue as Group Finance Director.

‘We are delighted to announce the appointment of Kyla Mullins as General Counsel and Company Secretary,’ Mitie added in a statement.

The news came a few hours after the group announced the sale of a loss-making business as it reported higher annual profit and revenue.

The share price was 2.25% higher at 318p by 13:30.
