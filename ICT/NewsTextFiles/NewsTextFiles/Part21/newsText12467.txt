
(MENAFN - Qatar News Agency) Ijing may 18 (qna) - china southern airlines the country's largest airline by fleet size has signed contracts with aircraft manufacturer airbus for the purchase of 80 a320 planes airbus china said.

the order includes 30 of the existing models of the a320 planes with price between 85.8 million us dollars and 110.1 million us dollars each and 50 of the more energy-efficient a320neo model with price between 94.4 million us dollars and 120 million us dollars airbus china said.

the planes will be delivered to china southern airlines from 2016 to 2020 according to china's (xinhua) news agency.
