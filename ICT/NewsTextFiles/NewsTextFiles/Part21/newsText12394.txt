
Two Chinese workers from a Myanmar mine have been kidnapped by activists, the company and villagers said Monday. According to the Wanbao Mining Copper Ltd., the two 23-year-old workers were abducted Sunday while conducting a land survey. The activists reportedly belong to Yangon Public Service Network and have demanded a "total halting" of the project to secure the miners' release.
