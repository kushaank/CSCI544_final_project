
HOUSTON, May 19, 2014 /PRNewswire/ -- TexCom, Inc. (OTC Pink: TEXC)(the "Company" or "TexCom"), an environmental services company serving the oil and gas industry, today announced that it will hold its 2014 Annual Meeting of Shareholders on Thursday, June 26, 2014. The Meeting will be held at 10:00 a.m. local time at the Hilton Houston Post Oak, 2001 Post Oak Blvd., Houston, Texas  77056.  Notice and Proxy materials will be sent out within the next two weeks to shareholders of record as of the close of business on May 13, 2014.

About TexCom, Inc.

TexCom, headquartered in Houston, Texas, is a growth-oriented environmental services company with a primary focus on the disposal of nonhazardous wastes generated by the oil & gas industry. For more information, please visit www.texcomresources.com .
