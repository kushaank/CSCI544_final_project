
Marking the 20th anniversary of Biblio Globus, the company honoured its international partners at an event attended by 500 participants.

Royal Cliff Beach Hotel was awarded the Gold Standard Hotel (among luxury hotels) 2013 by Biblio Globus’ president, Alexander Tugolukov.

Executive Director of Royal Cliff Hotels Group, Mr Vitanart Vathanakul (left) with the Gold Standard Luxury Hotel Award together with Miss Maria Gequillana (right) Royal Cliff Hotels Group, PR and Marketing Communications Manager.

“To be admitted as a Gold Standard Hotel by …Biblio Globus, our very close business partner is an honor,” said, Royal Cliff Hotels Group director of sales and marketing, Victor Kriventsov.
