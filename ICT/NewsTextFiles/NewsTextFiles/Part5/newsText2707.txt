
Indian Border Security Force (BSF) allegedly beat to death a Bangladeshi cattle trader on Putkhali border under Benapole Port Police Station early yesterday.

The dead, Sirajul Islam, 25, was son of Siddikur Rahman of Kayemkola under Jhikargachha upazila in Jessore.

Lt Col Mostafijur Rahman, commanding officer of 23 Border Guard Bangladesh, said the BSF members of Angrail camp picked up Sirajul around 4:30am. He along with a group of cattle traders was returning home with cattle from India through the border.

The troops then beat Sirajul to death, and kept the body along the frontier, he added.
