
To commemorate the arrival of a pair of giant pandas from China, MASkargo Sdn Bhd (MASkargo) today unveiled a special livery on its A330-200 freighter ahead of the pair’s arrival on Wednesday.

MASKargo in a statement said the flight bearing registration number 9M-MUD had a new design featuring the giant pandas and “Malaysia welcomes Fu Wa & Feng Yi”.

MASKargo is the official carrier of the pandas which are travelling from Chengdu to Kuala Lumpur.

Earlier, Bernama reported Natural Resources and Environment Minister Datuk Seri G. Palanivel as saying that Fu Wa and Feng Yi would arrive at the MASKargo Complex, KL International Airport (KLIA), Sepang, on Wednesday.
