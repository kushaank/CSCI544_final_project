
Shanghai, May 19 / Kabar /. Kyrgyzstan and China continue to promote cooperation in humanitarian sphere, says in the Declaration on the further deepening of the strategic partnership signed between President of the Kyrgyz Republic Almazbek Atambayev and President Xi Jinping.

Under the document, the parties declared that they would continue all-round promotion of cooperation in culture, education, health, science and technology, sports, tourism, environmental protection and other humanitarian fields.

Kyrgyz and Chinese side will actively send their delegations to participate in cultural activities conducted by the other Party, provide cultural workers more opportunities for direct communication and cooperation. The Parties shall encourage the establishment of contacts between universities and research institutions of the two countries as well as cooperation in the exchange of scientific, technical and academic information.
