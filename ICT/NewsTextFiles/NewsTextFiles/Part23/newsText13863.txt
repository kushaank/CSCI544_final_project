
The external turnover of Kyrgyzstan and China comprised about $1.5 billion in 2013, the President's Office of the KR reported.

Note, that the President Almazbek Atambayev has arrived on a state visit to China at the invitation of its President Xi Jinping. The parties exchanged views and, as the President's Office of the KR explained, reached broad understanding on bilateral relations and regional issues of mutual interest on May 18.

"China is one of the main trade partners of Kyrgyzstan, which ranks second after Russia. The external turnover in 2013 amounted to $1,488.6 billion. Exports increased by 2.4 percent and imports grew by 31.2 percent. According to the Chinese Ministry of Commerce, last year the trade volume totaled $5,138 billion, a decrease of 0.5 percent compared with the same period of 2012. Thus, imports from China to Kyrgyzstan amounted to $5.75 billion, exports - $63 million. Currently, the number of joint economic projects are in various stages of implementation," the President's Office of the KR informed.
