
Shanghai, May 19 / Kabar /. Kyrgyzstan and China will speed up the rapid signing of the Agreement between the Government of the KR and the Government of the PRC on the mode of the Kyrgyz- Chinese border, says in the Declaration on the further deepening of the strategic partnership signed in Shanghai by President of the Kyrgyz Republic Almazbek Atambayev and President Xi Jinping.

Under the document , the parties will continue to work on implementation of projects due to financial support of the Chinese Government, aimed at improving the welfare of the population of Kyrgyzstan.
