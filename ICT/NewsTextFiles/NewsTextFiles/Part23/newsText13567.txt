Flight MH370 carrying 239 people disappeared from radar screens when flying in the skies over the South China Sea on March 8 after take off from KL International Airport, Sepang at 12.41 midnight.

The Boeing 777-200ER aircraft was supposed to land in Beijing at 6.30am on the same day.

A search that involved several countries was launched to trace the aircraft, initially in the South China Sea, and then in the Indian Ocean when it was said to deviate from its original path.
