
TALENTED Southampton school pupils will be taking to the stage in aid of charity.

Youngsters from St George Catholic College will be staging their own St George’s Got Talent event on June 3, to raise money for pancreatic cancer research.

The event, which is open to the public, starts at 7pm and will feature a host of acts, including magicians, dancers and bands.

Tickets, priced at £2.50 for adults and £2 for children, can be booked by calling the school in Leaside Way on 023 8032 2603 or will be available on the night.
