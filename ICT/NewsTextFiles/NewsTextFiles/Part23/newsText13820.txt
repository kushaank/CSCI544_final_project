
More than 3,500 Chinese citizens evacuated Vietnam via several passenger ships headed for Haikou, China. According to AFP, 2 Chinese were killed and 140 injured after mobs of Vietnamese expressed their wrath last week toward China for erecting an oil drilling rig in waters claimed by both nations. Many factories were actually owned by Taiwanese, S. Korean and Japanese companies.
