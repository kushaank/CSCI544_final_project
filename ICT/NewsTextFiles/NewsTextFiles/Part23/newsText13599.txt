
DERA ISMAIL Khan - A Chinese tourist travelling from Lahore to Balochistan via Khyber Paktunkhwa was kidnapped from Darban area of Dera Ismail Khan, official sources revealed on Monday.

The sources said the Chinese tourist, Hong Xu Dong, was travelling from Lahore to Quetta when he went missing near Garah Mehmood area of Darban in DI Khan.

The security forces recovered a cycle from Garah Mehmood and launched a search operation in the area.

A high-ranking police official, on condition of anonymity, said the incident occurred at about 2:30pm while his belongings have been recovered from the spot.
