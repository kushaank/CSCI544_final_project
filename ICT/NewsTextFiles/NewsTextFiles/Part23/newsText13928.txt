
Frank Wood went to his eternal home on Nov. 28, 2013 in Albuquerque, New Mexico. He was born March 12, 1938 in Bonne Terre, Missouri, to Herbert Vincent and Gladys Ann (McCauley) Wood.

He was an air recon pilot in the U.S. Navy for 21 years and served during the Vietnam War. After retiring from the Navy he worked for New Haven Home in Vista, California, Diversified Sciences Corporation in Palmdale, California and Lockheed Martin in Albuquerque, New Mexico. He was a member of the Knights of Columbus and the Catholic War Veterans. He was active in ACTS Retreat Ministry.

Frank is survived by his wife of 49 years, Carol Lowney Wood; his son, Michael and wife Kimba; his daughter Sarah and Jim and grandson, Bryce Barrett. He is also survived by six brothers and four sisters.
