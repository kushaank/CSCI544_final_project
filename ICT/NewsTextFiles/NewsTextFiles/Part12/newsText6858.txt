
A 19-year-old soldier from Pine Valley was shot to death while serving in Jordan, according to the Pentagon and the Army.

Spec. Adrian M. Perkins, 19, died May 17, in Amman, Jordan. An Army official at Fort Carson, Colo., said Perkins was shot and died of his injuries. No Jordanians were involved in the shooting, the official said, but she would not disclose any more details.

Perkins was assigned to 1st Battalion, 67th Armor Regiment, 2nd Brigade Combat Team, 4th Infantry Division, based in Fort Carson.

An infantryman, Perkins joined the Army in August 2012. The deployment to Jordan was his first.

He had received the Army Achievement Medal, National Defense Service Medal, Global War on Terrorism Expeditionary Medal, Global War on Terrorism Service Medal and the Army Service Ribbon.
