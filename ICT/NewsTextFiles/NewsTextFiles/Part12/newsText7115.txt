
PETALING JAYA: MMC Corporation Bhd says its subsidiary, MMC Engineering Services Sdn Bhd, along with Siemens Aktiengesellschaft and Siemens Malaysia Sdn Bhd, has landed a RM300mil contract for the Rapid Project in Pengerang, Johor.

The contract involves engineering, constructing and commissioning a co-generation plant at the Pengerang refinery and petrochemical development complex.

MMC did not specify when work was set to begin, but said completion was expected to be by the middle of 2017.
