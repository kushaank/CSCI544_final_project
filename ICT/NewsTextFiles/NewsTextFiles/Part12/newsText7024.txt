
PLYMOUTH (WWJ) - If you’re a military veteran looking for a job — you’re in luck.

Comcast is hosting a job fair and open house for military veterans at its Plymouth headquarters on Monday, May 19 from 9 a.m. until noon.

Veterans will be able to apply for 240 open positions in Michigan, with an emphasis on filling roles for direct sales representatives, sales consultants and customer service opportunities. Veterans can also learn about career opportunities at the company and chances for advancement.

Comcast Headquarters is located at 41112 Concept Drive in Plymouth.

For more information, visit www.comcast-jobs.com/military .
