
OMAN Road Safety Association (ORSA) has attended the meeting held by Legislative and Legal Committee of As’shura Council to discuss the draft amendments of some provisions of the Traffic law. The meeting was chaired by Said bin Ghanem al Muqbali in the presence of the members of the Committee. Amor bin Nasser al Matani, a board member of the association, and Shaima bint Murtadha al Lawati, CEO of the Association, attended the meeting representing the Oman Road Safety Association.

For example, the use of child seats and seat belts in the rear seat and helmet for cyclists and the presence of a points system for offenders are important. The legislation must keep pace with the rapid developments that occur in the community”.
