The Salvation Army’s local relief workers continue to assist those impacted by floods in Penn Yan, Yates County and those working to restore and clean up the damage done by recent flooding.

Since Wednesday, May 14, Rochester and Elmira Corps officers along with community volunteers served 2,131 meals, provided 3,900 bottles of water and Gatorade and distributed 154 clean up kits.

At this point, in-kind donations are not being accepted.  However, these gifts are vitally important in supporting the day-to-day work of your local Salvation Army.

Please consider giving these items to your local Salvation Army Family Store or schedule a pick up at 1-800-SA-TRUCK  (1-800-728-7825 ).
