SEEKONK - Kathy Coyle now has a home to call her own.

Coyle, a veteran employee of the town's finance department, was joined by friends, family members and volunteers from Old Colony Habitat for Humanity on Sunday as she cut the ribbon to her house at 4 Gude St.
