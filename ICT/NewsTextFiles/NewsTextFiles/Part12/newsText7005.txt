
(MENAFN) Sources have reported that Siemens is currently working on an asset-swap offer for Alstom's power business at the beginning of this week, The Peninsula Qatar reported.

Alstom is already in talks with US conglomerate General Electric over a USD16.9 billion bid for its power arm. However, it has opened its books to Siemens so it can propose its own deal if it wants to, due to strong political pressure.
