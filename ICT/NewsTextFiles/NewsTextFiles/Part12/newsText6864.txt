
•Watkins Paint, a locally owned Benjamin Moore paint dealer, donated $5,000 worth of paint to The Mission Continues to repaint their facility in Orlando. The Mission Continues is an organization that empowers and supports returning veterans as they transition out of their military career.

•HOPE Helps Inc. in Oviedo received a $20,000 grant from the Orlando Sentinel Family Fund, a McCormick Foundation fund, which it will use for the Kids of HOPE program. It feeds children from low-income families during the school breaks throughout the year.
