
Climate change will benefit sea turtles in the short term new research has found.

The warming climate will create hotter incubation conditions that will raise females in turtle populations.

Linda Mottram spoke to Professor Graeme Hays, Chair of Marine Science at Deakin University who is one of the authors of this new research.
