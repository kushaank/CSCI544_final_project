
Courtesy Meet & Greet for Cordova Voters.

May 21st from 12:00 pm to 1:30 pm at Cordova Chamber & Visitor Center 404 1st St..

Carol Austerman of Kodiak is running for State Representative in the 32nd district as a Republican.  She has held a seat on the Kodiak Island Borough Assembly since 2011.  Austerman will be here to meet  community members and learn more about the issues that are important to them.
