
At a special meeting Monday, County Manager Chuck Abernathy gave the County Commission a proposed $38.4 million budget for 2014-2015 that doesn’t call for a property tax rate hike.

The proposed county budget for next fiscal year contains more money for the school system and McDowell Technical Community College. And like in previous years, the proposed budget contains a 2.5 percent pay increase for county employees.
