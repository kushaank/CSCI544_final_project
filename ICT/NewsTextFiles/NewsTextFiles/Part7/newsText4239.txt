
Students and staff have been allowed to returned to Spring High School after a bomb scare late Monday morning at the campus in north Harris County.

The bomb threat was received about 11:25 a.m. at the school at 19428 North Freeway, according to officials with Spring Independent School District.

Students and staff were evacuated from the buildings while district police officers searched the campus. The campus was deemed safe and students and staff were allowed to returned to their classrooms at about noon.

No information was available about how the threat was received.
