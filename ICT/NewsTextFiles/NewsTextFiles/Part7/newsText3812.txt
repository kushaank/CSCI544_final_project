
A group of Border Trade Alliance (BTA) members, including  BTA Vice-Chairman Russ Jones, recently traveled to Washington, D.C., to speak to Yuma's Congressional delegation on border issues.

A group of about 20 stakeholders in the community met recently to learn more about Joint Technical Education Districts and to discuss what the next steps are for forming one in Yuma County.

Educators as well as businesspeople representing various parts of the county heard a first-hand presentation on the start up process and the day-to-day operations of the Western Maricopa Education Center (West-MEC) JTED in Glendale, Arizona. West-MEC superintendent Greg Donovan explained the wide range of programs that they offer in their service area, which covers 46 schools in 12 high school districts in Maricopa County.
