
The Seniors Ministry of Immaculate Conception/St. Joseph Parish will hold its regular monthly meeting at 11:30 a.m. Friday, May 23, in the parish hall, 732 U.S. Route 20.

This month, the ministry welcomes Sister Anne Bryan Smollin, CSJ, a noted humorist and a psychotherapist. Her topic will be "Making every day count."

All interested community members are invited to attend the program and lunch at no charge. Information: Mary Linton at (518) 733-0469.
