
The relevant committee in three readings approved a number of agreements on Kyrgyzstan's health care support, the decision was made today at session of the parliamentary committee on international relations.

Earlier it was reported that the International Development Association is providing $11 million grant: a part of the funds will be used to renovate 22 territorial hospitals on measures for protection of motherhood and childhood.

Under the second agreement, the World Bank provides $13.5 million - money will be spent on the construction of two perinatal centers in the south and the purchase of medical equipment.
