A Guatemalan man was sentenced to more than three years in federal prison for a tax fraud scheme in Nashville.

Juan Castro-Castro, 37, of Shelbyville, Ken., was sentenced May 13 in federal court in Nashville to serve 37 months in prison, according to the U.S. Attorney’s Office. They said he’d probably be deported as well, because is in the United States illegally from Guatemala.

Authorities said Castro-Castro stole about $649,000 by filing more than 100 fake tax returns between 2012 and 2013 in Nashville.
