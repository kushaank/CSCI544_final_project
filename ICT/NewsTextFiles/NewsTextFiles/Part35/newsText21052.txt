
Saint Michael's Orthodox Church in Binghamton was part of this year's Sacred Sites Open House. The event featured religious structures throughout the community that have had a historical impact. Saint Michael's was founded in 1904 and paid special tribute to its Austro-Hungary roots.

Saint Michael's is best known for its annual Carpathian Family Day that happens every fall.
