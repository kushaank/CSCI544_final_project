
VIENNA, 16 May 2014 - OSCE Secretary General Lamberto Zannier authorized a 30.000 euro donation today from the Organisation's Charity Fund for immediate help to the most affected in response to severe flooding across Bosnia and Herzegovina.

The financial assistance will be delivered through the OSCE Mission in BiH.

The OSCE Mission to BiH will continue to engage and help as the situation develops, working closely with other humanitarian organizations, EUFOR and Ministry of Security of BiH.
