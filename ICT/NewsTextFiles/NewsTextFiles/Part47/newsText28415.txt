
HUNTSVILLE, Ala. - Huntsville police said a woman riding a motorcycle was killed on south Memorial Parkway Monday morning.

Police identified the victim as Christina Renee Brazier, 23, of Huntsville.

The wreck happened northbound near the Vermont Road overpass around 9:15 a.m. Police said Brazier apparently lost control of the motorcycle and struck the inside barrier and a light pole. She was pronounced dead at the scene.

Traffic was routed to the access road off the overpass for about an hour while police worked on the scene.

This is the second fatal motorcycle accident in less than a week. On Friday two people on a motorcycle were killed in a wreck on Highway 431 in New Hope.
