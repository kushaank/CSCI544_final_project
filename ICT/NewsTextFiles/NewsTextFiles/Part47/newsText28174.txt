HUNTSVILLE, Alabama-- Police say a man is in critical condition after he shot into his ex-girlfriend's home on Patton Road and then had a wreck Sunday night.

Police responded to a shooting call around 11 p.m. The victim told police when she arrived at her home in the 3600 block she was approached by her ex-boyfriend who wanted to talk.

According to police, the victim ran towards her apartment. When she got into her front door she heard gun shots.

There were four bullet holes in the front window of her apartment police say.

Officers say the offender then left the area in a vehicle and had an accident on Belvoir Drive.

The offender was taken to Huntsville Hospital with life threating injuries.

No arrests have been made.
