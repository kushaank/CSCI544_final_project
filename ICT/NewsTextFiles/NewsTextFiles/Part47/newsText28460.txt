
Today is the second annual Hepatitis Testing Day, and the Indiana Department of Health encourages residents to learn more about hepatitis and how to protect themselves.

According to the Centers for Disease Control and Prevention, viral hepatitis is the leading cause of liver cancer and the most common reason for liver transplantation. Most individuals who have viral hepatitis are not aware of itHealth officials recommend a one-time hepatitis blood test for hepatitis C for everyone between ages 49 and 69.
