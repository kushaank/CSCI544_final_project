GLASSBORO — A 26-year-old woman told police she was pushed to the ground and robbed Sunday afternoon.

The victim reported she was walking on Grove Street near Delsea Drive at about 2:25 p.m. when two males on bikes rode up, pushed her to the ground and took her wallet from her purse, police said.

Both were wearing jean shorts and riding BMX-style bicycles, said police.

The men fled on the bikes east on Grove Street and were last seen turning left onto South Academy Street, police said.
