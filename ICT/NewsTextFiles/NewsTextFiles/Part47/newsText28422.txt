
Police identified a 36-year-old man who was fatally shot Saturday night in Southeast Washington.

Just before midnight Saturday, police received a report of a shooting in the 2700 block of Langston Place SE, just off Suitland Parkway. When officers arrived, they found Simwone Keith Milstead of Southeast. He was taken to a local hospital, where he died.

Police are continuing to investigate the incident and are offering a reward of up to $25,000. Anyone with information can call 202-727-9099.
