
A 40-year-old man was allegedly stabbed during a fight with his roommate on Saturday night.

The victim received a serious laceration to his head around 9:44 p.m. on the 300 block of Pennsylvania Street, according to a press release by police Sgt. Eric Montalbo. Police believe the victim may have been cut with a razor blade outside the home, Montalbo said. The man was taken to an area hospital for treatment of his life-threatening injuries.

Matthew Hendrix, 24, who had reportedly fled the scene on foot, was arrested on Main Street on suspicion of assault with a deadly weapon and mayhem, based on the injuries' severity.

Anyone with information about the case is asked to call the Watsonville Police Department's Investigations Division at 831-768-3352 or the crime tip line, at 831-768-3544 or by texting WATSPD to 888777.
