
A 28-year-old Broomfield woman was killed Sunday when the scooter she was driving  crashed into a Chevy Suburban.

Her name has not been released, pending notification of her family.

The accident happened near Colorado 93 and West 82nd Avenue at about 3:40 p.m., said Trooper Josh Lewis.

The crash was still under investigation Sunday evening, but Lewis said, "The initial indications are that her actions (were) the causal factor."

The driver of the suburban was not injured. The highway was closed south of Rocky Flats for more than three and half hours for the investigation. It reopened at about 7:20 p.m., according to the Colorado Department of Transportation.
