
LOUISVILLE, Ky. (WDRB) - Shively Police arrested a man who they say became involved in a verbal altercation that then became physical.

According to an arrest report, Christopher Mcintosh was arrested Saturday afternoon on Dixie Highway, near Wathen Lane, after a police officer was flagged down by a passing motorist who reported seeing two people fighting a block away.

Police records state an officer arrived in the area and saw Mcintosh loudly arguing with his brother. Police records say Mcintosh was intoxicated and he admitted that he had been drinking.

An arrest report also states that Mcintosh smelled strongly of alcohol and that his speech was slurred.

Police say that while an officer was attempting to resolve the incident, Mcintosh allegedly struck his brother in the mouth with a closed fist, while in front of the officer.

Mcintosh was arrested and charged with public intoxication and disorderly conduct.
