
Indian Border Security Force (BSF) picked up a Bangladeshi farmer from Patgram upazila in Lalmonirhat yesterday.

BSF members picked up Lablu Mia, 27, of Jhalangi village in the upazila from Burimari border while he was harvesting paddy on his land along the border.

Lt Col Shafiul Alam Khan, commanding officer (CO) of BGB 15 Battalion in Lalmonirhat, said a BSF patrol team picked up Lablu from the bordering area at around 3:00pm.

BGB sent a protest letter to its Indian counterpart. In reply, BSF assured them of returning the detainee through a flag meeting, added the CO.
