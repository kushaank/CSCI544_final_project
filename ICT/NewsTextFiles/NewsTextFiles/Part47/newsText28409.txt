Two men were shot, and another was stabbed following a large dispute in a Brooklyn park Sunday evening.

The incident occurred in Highland Park near Jamaica Avenue and Cleveland Street in Cypress Hills around 5:45 p.m.

Police say a 20-year-old Hispanic man was shot in the torso and taken to Brookdale Hospital. A 19-year-old man who was slashed in the arm was also taken to Brookdale Hospital. A 17-year-old boy was also shot in the hip. He walked into Jamaica Hospital.

The investigation is ongoing.
