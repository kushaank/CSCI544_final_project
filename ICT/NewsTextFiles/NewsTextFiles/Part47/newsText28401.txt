
BOONE COUNTY (WIFR) -- 31-year-old Francisco Mejia of Oklahoma City has been sentenced to 7 years in prison for delivering 3.4 grams of cocaine in Belvidere.

Mejia was charged in 2011 but then fled the area before he was later captured in Oklahoma City, Oklahoma.

Mejia was also ordered to pay for the transportation costs to return to Boone County which was previously paid out of the bond he previously posted.

There are currently no active polls at this time.
