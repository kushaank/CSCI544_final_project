If you drink and drive there will be a price to pay.

Starting Friday May, 23 through Saturday, May 24 the Bossier Parish Sheriff's Office will conduct a sobriety checkpoint at an undisclosed location.

Deputies will be on the lookout for impaired drivers during Memorial Day weekend.

The Bossier City Police Department and Louisiana State Police will also assist in the operation.

The sobriety checkpoint is being conducted as part of a $14,040 grant awarded to the Bossier Sheriff's Office from the Louisiana Highway Safety Commission for DWI Enforcement.
