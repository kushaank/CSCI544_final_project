ST. LOUIS • A supermarket security guard shot and wounded a suspected shoplifter Sunday afternoon, police said.

The shooting occurred in the Aldi store at 702 North Kingshighway about 1:30 p.m. Police said the guard confronted a suspected shoplifter and shot him in the foot.

The wounded man was taken to a hospital. His condition was not available, but he was stable, according to police.
