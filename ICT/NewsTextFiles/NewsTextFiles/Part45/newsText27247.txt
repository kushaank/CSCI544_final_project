
Changes are underway for the CARE Foundation of Western North Carolina, formerly known as the Broughton Hospital Foundation, whose name was officially changed after a recent meeting of its board of directors. The organization is expanding its scope of services, and — now independent from Broughton Hospital — it can serve more people than ever before.

For more than two decades, the nonprofit has been working under its original name to provide necessities for patients and meet the needs of the hospital that were outside of the realm of available state funding.
