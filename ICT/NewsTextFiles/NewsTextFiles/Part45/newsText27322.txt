
The Northeast Alabama Adult Education Program, which helps students prepare for the GED test and offers classes in adult literacy and English as a Second Language, is also reaching out to help those students who have completed high school course requirements, but have not passed all of the Alabama High School Graduation Exam.

“The grad exam is no longer a requirement for earning a high school diploma, but it remains a requirement for those who completed school prior to 2014,” said Chad Gorham, director of adult education for Northeast Alabama.
