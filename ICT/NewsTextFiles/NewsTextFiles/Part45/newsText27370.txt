
STATE TO RESPOND TO BLIND TRUST SUIT: The state is required to submit a response to the Florida Supreme Court by noon Monday in a case challenging a 2013 law that allows blind trusts for public officials. The case, filed by Jim Apthorp, a former chief of staff to the late Gov. Reubin Askew, contends that blind trusts violate the open-government Sunshine Amendment.
