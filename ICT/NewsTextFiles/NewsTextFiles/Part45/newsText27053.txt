
LANHAM, Md. (WNEW) — Maryland police are asking for the public’s help with finding a missing man who was last seen in Bethesda Saturday.

Kenneth McCurdy, 72, lives on the 1300 block of New York Avenue in D.C. He is described as a 6-foot-1 black male weighing approximately 160 pounds. He has black hair, brown eyes and a gray beard.

He was last seen on May 17 at the Bethesda Health and Rehabilitation Center located at 5721 Grosvenor Lane.

Police and family are concerned for McCurdy’s mental well-being. Anyone with information regarding his whereabouts is asked to contact the Montgomery County Police non-emergency number at 301-279-8000.
