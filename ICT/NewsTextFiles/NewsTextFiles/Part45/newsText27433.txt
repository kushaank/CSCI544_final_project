The Montgomery County Veterans Service Organization is hosting its annual Memorial Day Ceremony at 10 a.m. Monday, May 26, at Veterans Memorial Park.

City and county officials, as well as MCVSO staff will give remarks, with the keynote address given by Col. Karen M. Darnell, U.S. Air Force, who serves as the state inspector general for the Tennessee National Guard, according to a county news release.
