KENTWOOD, Mich. (WZZM) -- Firefighters are in clean-up mode after an early morning oven fire at a Kentwood bakery.

It happened around 2:40 a.m. Monday at Roskam Baking on Broadmoor Ave. near 52nd St. SE.

Crews found a fire in one of the commercial ovens at the facility. There was a haze in the air because of the fire when they first arrived.

Evacuated employees outside the Roskam Baking facility on Broadmoor Ave. following an oven fire.

Employees of the bakery were evacuated as a precaution. No one was injured.

Firefighters had the oven fire out about an hour after they arrived.

Roskam makes croutons and other baked goods for Rothbury Farms .
