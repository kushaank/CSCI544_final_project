
Hassan Khadangi said that based on the statistics, Iran's farms under cultivation of Saffron stand at 85,000 hectares.

He further added that Saffron production in last Iranian year was 311 tons, registering a 20 percent rise compared to the figure for its previous year.

Khadangi noted that Iran produces 94 percent of the world's total output, while the remaining countries produce only 20 tons of saffron.

He said that some 137 tons of saffron valued at $200 mln were exported to the international markets last year.
