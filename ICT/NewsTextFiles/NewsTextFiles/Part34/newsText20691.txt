Under the mandate of the Personal Representative of the OSCE Chairman-in-Office, the contact line on the part of Terter-Aghdere highway passing through Terter region was monitored on March 19. Press service of the Defense Ministry told APA that no incident occurred during the monitoring.

The monitoring was conducted by field assistants of Personal Representative of the OSCE Chairman-in-Office Jiri Aberle and Peter Swedberg on the Azerbaijani side and field assistants of Personal Representative of the OSCE Chairman-in-Office Evgeny Sharov and Hristo Hristov on the opposite side of the contact line.
