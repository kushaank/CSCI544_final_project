
Riyadh, Rajab 20, 1435, May 19, 2014, SPA -- A visiting Portuguese delegation discussed with the Chief Executive of the Saudi Food and Drug Authority (SFDA), Dr. Mohammed bin Abdulrahman Al-Mashaal here today investment opportunities in the sector of medicine and medical devices and products.

It is worth mentioning that the Portuguese delegation is currently on a visit to the Kingdom on the sidelines of Saudi Health Conference and Exhibition 2014.

The delegation is being headed by the Minister of Health, Dr. Fernando ad Costa of Portugal.
