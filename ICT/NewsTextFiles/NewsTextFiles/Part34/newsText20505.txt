
A 58-year-old woman who worked as a nurse at MedStar Washington Hospital Center was found dead at her home in Virginia, and authorities said they are investigating the matter as a homicide.

Nancy Mercer, of Spotsylvania County, Va., was found late Sunday night on the floor of her home on Eastridge Way, the county sheriff’s office said.

They said authorities were called about 11:40 p.m. Sunday to check on her after she had not reported to work.

The sheriff’s office said that details on the cause of death were being withheld until an antopsy could be completed.
