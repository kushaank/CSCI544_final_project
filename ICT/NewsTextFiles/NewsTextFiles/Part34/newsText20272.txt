
YEREVAN, MAY 19, ARMENPRESS. By the new program of the Government of the Republic of Armenia, priority will be given to the investments in the most intense, as well as vital parts of the road network.  This is mentioned in the program of the new government for 2014-2017, which was approved on May 19 at the special session held by the Executive Body.

As reports “Armenpress”, the construction of the road "North- South"- the first high-speed road of the regional significance will be continued in accordance with the international standards, with a particular attention to the solutions of the schemes of the southern part. In the result of state-private sector partnership, the technical-economic feasibility and design estimate documents of the "Southern Railway" project will be worked out. The length of the railway will be about 300 km, it will consist of bridges and 60 tunnels, and will have 27 stations. The construction of the railway is initially estimated at 3.2 billion dollars. It is expected that the construction will last for up to 6 years. According to the project, the construction may start in 2016 and end in 2022.

Continuous efforts will be exerted to restore the railway to the Russian Federation.
