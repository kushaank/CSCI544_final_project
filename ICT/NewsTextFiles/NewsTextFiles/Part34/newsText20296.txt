
Riyadh, Rajab 20, 1435, May 19, 2014, SPA -- Majlis Al-Shura held here today its 39th session under the chairmanship of its Speaker Sheikh Dr. Abdullah bin Mohammed bin Ibrahim Al Al-Sheikh.

The Council demanded that the Civil Aviation Authority apply the concept of the overall management of the operation of airports so that work at an airport shall be one as an independent unit, also stressing the obliging of global airlines to employ Saudis at airports and their main and branch offices in the Kingdom.

The Council approved the draft agreement between the Government of the Kingdom of Saudi Arabia and the Government of the Republic of Poland on cooperation in the field of defense.
