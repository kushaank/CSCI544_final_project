CALLING all young actors the Youth Venue is holding auditions for its new production Romeo and Julie on Wednesday and Thursday June 4 and 5.

To organise an audition contact Reina Stalker on 6571 4687 she can also provide an audition pack (with all the information needed for the audition) and the rehearsal dates and performance dates.
