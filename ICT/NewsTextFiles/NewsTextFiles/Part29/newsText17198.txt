
KOLKATA: The results of both regular and external candidates of Madhyamik Pariksha 2014 will be announced at 9am on May 22.

The heads of the affiliated institutions or their authorized representatives will be able to collect the mark sheets and certificates of the examination from the selected camp offices from 10am onwards on the same day. "I have requested the schools to deliver the mark sheets to the candidates on the same day," said administrator of West Bengal Board of Secondary Education, Kalyanmoy Ganguly.
