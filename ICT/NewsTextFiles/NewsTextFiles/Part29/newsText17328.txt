Owosso Public Schools Director of Building and Grounds Dan Hock, right, was honored by Owosso Mayor Ben Frederick during Arbor Day festivities held May 10. Frederick noted Hock’s leadership and care for the centuries-old oak grove on the Emerson Elementary property led to a 100 percent survival rate for the more than 50 trees planted there in 2013. This year’s Arbor Day planting added 16 trees.

OWOSSO — After about 40 years of service — 15 at Owosso Public Schools — Director of Operations Dan Hock is looking forward to moving on to sunny Florida for retirement.

Hock, 56, began his career with South Lyon schools in 1975 as a custodian. After four years, he moved into the grounds and maintenance department. Finally, in 1999, he came to Owosso Public Schools where the maintenance department was operated privately through ServiceMaster.
