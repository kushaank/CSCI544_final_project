
Missouri State Treasurer Clint Zweifel visits a Joplin school to highlight saving for college.

JOPLIN, MO.--- The Missouri state treasurer visits a Joplin school to highlight saving for college. Clint Zweifel is encouraging parents to invest in the 529 College Savings Plan, also called "MOST." His outreach effort includes giving away $5,290 dollars in contributions to one lucky family at the end of the month. To qualify for the giveaway, you must be a missouri resident and at least 18 years old who enters the contest for a child 17 or under.
