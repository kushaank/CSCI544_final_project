
Dyce Kimura hopes that Fort Lauderdale guitar lessons will create more eager worship leaders. Whether or not students seek his services for Christian music, Dyce Kimura remains dedicated to teaching local aspiring guitarists. He is known in the community for being a great resource for Skype guitar lessons . He uses the communications technology to work individually with students, or teach in group settings. Dyce Kimura finds Skype to be an efficient way to meet with students, teach guitar, provide specific feedback, and help students interact and practice with others who have similar levels of talent. Using Skype also allows him to understand his students and what inspires them to learn.

Guitar teacher Dyce Kimura teaches students around the Fort Lauderdale area in person and via Skype. His students vary in skill level and guitar style.
