VERGENNES TOWNSHIP, MI - An 18-year old Lowell High School senior was pronounced dead at the scene of a car crash on Saturday morning.

Around 7:30 a.m. on Saturday, May 17, Lowell resident Alexander Dlouhy was driving a Volkswagen Jetta north on Lincoln Lake Ave., south of Three Mile Rd. when the car crossed the center line, according to the Kent County Sheriff's Department.

The car collided with a southbound Ford Explorer. Dlouhy, who was not wearing a seat belt, died at the scene. The driver of the SUV, 60-year-old John Lewis of Sheridan, was taken to Greenville's hospital with injuries not considered to be life-threatening.

According to Lowell district officials, Dlouhy was set to graduate next month and was a member of the track and field team.
