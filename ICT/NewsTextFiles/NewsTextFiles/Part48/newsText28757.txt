
A MBTA bus hangs over the Massachusetts Turnpike after crashing on Centre Street on Sunday.

The MBTA bus driver who lost control of her Route 57 bus, crashed through a guardrail and ended up hanging off the edge of a Newton Corner overpass above the Massachusetts Turnpike below was charged today by transit police with obstruction of justice, according to the transit agency.
