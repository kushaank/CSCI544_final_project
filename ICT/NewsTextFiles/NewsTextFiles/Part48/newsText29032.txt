FLINT TOWNSHIP, MI – Police are searching for two suspects involved in the attempted unarmed robbery at a jewelry store on Friday.

Around 6:15 p.m. Friday, three men walked into the Medawar Jewelers at 3093 S. Linden Road and started smashing the jewelry display cases, according to police. The men were confronted by an armed security guard and they fled the scene without taking anything.

Two men were arrested after police pulled over the suspect vehicle but two others remain at large.

Anyone with information is asked to call Flint Township police Detective Sabo at 810-600-3250.
