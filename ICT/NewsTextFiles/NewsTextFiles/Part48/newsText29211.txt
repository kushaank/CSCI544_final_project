
A man was arrested Friday, May 16 for setting fire to a residence in Huntington, WV.

According to a criminal complaint, 33-year-old Timothy Raymond Whitten was charged with first degree arson.

According to several neighbors, the residence was believed to be unoccupied.

No injuries were reported.

Whitten was arraigned Saturday, May 17, at the Cabell County Magistrate Court, and is being held in the Western Regional Jail on a $50,000 bond.
