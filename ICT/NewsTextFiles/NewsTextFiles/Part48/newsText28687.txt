
EVANSVILLE, IN (WFIE) – Columbia Street in Evansville will be closed due to an accident involving a vehicle that hit a building.

According to Evansville Police, a three-car accident led to one of the vehicles hitting Lanhuck's Monday morning.  Sgt. Josh Wittmer told 14 News one woman was taken to the hospital with minor injuries.

Officers said Columbia will be closed from Governor to Lafayette until the building receives the appropriate repairs to stabilize it.

14 News is continuing to follow these breaking developments, and we will have the latest on air, online at  14News.com  and on mobile with the  14 News mobile app .
