
France's Publicis Groupe has won a digital marketing deal with Facebook worth about $500 million including spending,Ad Age reported Monday, citing an executive familiar with the matter. The deal will be managed by all the group's agencies in North America followed by a global roll out, Publicis-owned Starcom MediaVest Group's spokeswoman Anita Mcgorty told Reuters.
