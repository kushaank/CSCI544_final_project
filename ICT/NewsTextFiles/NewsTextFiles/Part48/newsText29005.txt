
At least six Austin-area companies reported receiving investment capital last week.

Home improvement retailer TreeHouse Inc. reported raising nearly $500,000 of a planned $700,000 financing from 19 investors.

Stealth mode technology startup Dropoff Inc. raised $1.5 million of a planned $1.6 million financing from 31 investors.
