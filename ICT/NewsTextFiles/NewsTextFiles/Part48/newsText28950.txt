
Jurors in the New York trial against British hate preacher Abu Hamza have found him guilty of kidnapping and terrorism offences.

The former imam of London mosque Finsbury Park faces a possible life sentence.

He was accused of involvement in the 1998 abduction of 16 Western tourists in Yemen, of trying to set up a terrorist training camp in the United States in 1999, and of promoting "violent jihad" on a global scale.
