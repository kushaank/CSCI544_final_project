
Also due in court today are two men charged with wounding former Ming Pao chief editor Kevin Lau Chun-to. Yip Kim-wah and Wong Chi-wah, both 37-year-old plumbers, will appear before magistrates at Eastern Court charged with one count of wounding with intent and one of theft. They are said to have "unlawfully and maliciously" wounded Lau and to have stolen a motorcycle.
