
FORESTRY AS a sector contributes a measly amount to the gross domestic product (GDP) of the Philippines. This is understandable because production forests in the Philippines are almost non-existent. The little left of our forests is basically for watershed areas and those declared as national integrated protected areas systems (NIPAS). The latter include those areas that are important biodiversity habitats, have unique land features, or are home to endangered plant and animal species.
