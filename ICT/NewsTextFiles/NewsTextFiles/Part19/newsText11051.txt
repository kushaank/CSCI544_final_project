
BANDAR SERI BEGAWAN: His Majesty Sultan Haji Hassanal Bolkiah Muizzaddien Waddaulah, the Sultan and Yang Di-Pertuan of Brunei Darussalam has consented to send a condolence message to His Excellency Thongsing Thammavong, Prime Minister of The Lao People’s Democratic Republic, following the recent tragic military plane crash in northern Laos, involving the Deputy Prime Minister and Minister of National Defence and other senior members of the government of the Lao People’s Democratic Republic, ©BRUDIRECT.COM reported.

In His Majesty’s message to His Excellency Thongsing Thammavong, Prime Minister of The Lao People’s Democratic Republic, His Majesty was deeply saddened to learn of the tragic plane crash in northern Laos.

His Majesty as well as His Majesty’s Government and the people of Brunei Darussalam expressed their heartfelt condolences to His Excellency and to the government as well as to the people of Laos, especially to the families of those affected by the tragedy.
