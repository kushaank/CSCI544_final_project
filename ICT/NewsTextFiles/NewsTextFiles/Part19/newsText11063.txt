
Mahanayaka of the Sri Lanka Ramagna Maha Nikaya Most Ven. Napane Pemasiri thero says that it can not be accepted that the Buddhist monks engage in politics. He expresses regrets on the media reports that some Buddhist monks are going to engage in politics. The Maha Sangha took the front to protect the country, nation, religion, culture and the language in the past. Meanwhile, Sangha Nayaka of New Zealand Ven. Walpola Munindrawansa thero said that there is a high reputation for Sri Lankan Buddhist monks internationally.
