Graebel Companies, Inc. has been honored with the 2013 UnitedHealthcare Well Deserved Award. Graebel was one of only 12 recipients in the U.S. to receive the esteemed recognition for its top-rated employee health and wellness program.

Graebel employs a multifaceted well-being program launched by senior vice president of human resources, Mary Stoik Dymond, who was recently named as one of the top five Human Resource Executives for 2013 by the Human Resource Executive publication. The innovative program incorporates everything from weight-loss and healthy eating habits to smoking cessation to stress management. For the past three years, new elements, including incentive contests, have been rolled out to keep the program exciting which has significantly increased employee interest and participation and ultimately the overall well-being of the people of the Graebel Companies, Inc.

UnitedHealthcare is dedicated to helping people nationwide live healthier lives by simplifying the health care experience, meeting consumer health and wellness needs, and sustaining trusted relationships with care providers. The company offers the full spectrum of health benefit programs for individuals, employers and Medicare and Medicaid beneficiaries, and contracts directly with more than 800,000 physicians and care professionals, and 6,000 hospitals and other care facilities nationwide. Globally, UnitedHealthcare serves more than 45 million people in health benefits and is one of the businesses of UnitedHealth Group, a diversified Fortune 50 health and well-being company.
