FORD HEIGHTS | Someone cooking on a wood-burning stove may have caused a fire Sunday morning that destroyed an abandoned home at 1528 Congress Lane, authorities said.

Firefighters responded about 8:40 a.m. to the bi-level home in Ford Heights, an official said. No one was found inside the home, which had no electricity.

The Illinois state fire marshal has determined the fire was accidental, the official said.

No injuries were reported.
