
Republicans hoping to ride their Benghazi investigation to a November election sweep have entrusted a seven-member team with "getting to the truth," in the words of House Speaker John A. Boehner, about whether the Obama administration misled Americans about the deadly 2012 attack in Libya.

They insist the investigation isn't political. But several GOP members of the House Select Committee on Benghazi have made claims about administration wrongdoing. Some have participated in previous investigations into how the U.S. ambassador to Libya and three other Americans were killed.

Democrats have yet to say if they'll participate in what will be the eighth probe into the attack on the U.S. mission.
