
The Greater Austin Asian Chamber of Commerce serves the considerable Asian business community in Central Texas. SXSW is excited to be one of the honorees at the annual awards banquet for this organization, which is scheduled on the evening of Wednesday, May 21 at the Renaissance Austin Hotel (9721 Arboretum Blvd).

Along with HEB and Seton, SXSW will be honored as one of Austin’s powerhouse economic drivers. Others that will be recognized at the May 21 event include Manoj Saxena (Lifetime Achievement Award), Tokyo Electron (Business of the Year) and Mayor Lee Leffingwell (Leadership).

Accepting the award on behalf of SXSW will be Technical & Production Coordinator Wei Yeh .
