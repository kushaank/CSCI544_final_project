
Alternative Opportunities operates two Alternative Learning Experiences Centers. The centers are named for Alex Levin Goss, who died from a drug overdose in 2011. The centers are in Bentonville and Desloge, Mo. The Walton Family Foundation provided a $200,000 grant to help fund the Bentonville center.
