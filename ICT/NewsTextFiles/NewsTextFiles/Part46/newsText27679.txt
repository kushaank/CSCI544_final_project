
Rockland Cultural Council needs new members. There are several spots available. The Council distributes the funds raised through the lottery for the arts. The Massachusetts Cultural Council decides the amount that is awarded to each town, and administers the funds. Rockland received $6,100 for fiscal 2014-2015. Those grant funds have been awarded.
