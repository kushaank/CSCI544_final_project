
A Monday morning search warrant executed by the Russell County Sheriff's Office and the Kansas Bureau of Investigation in Dorrance resulted in the arrest of a woman after methamphetamine was found in her residence.

According to a press release from the Sheriff's Office, at 9:47 AM Monday, officers from the Sheriff's Office and KBI executed a search warrant at 916 North Lincoln Ave in Dorrance.

Brandy Clevenger was placed under arrest for possession of methamphetamine with intent to distribute and possession of drug paraphernalia. She was transported to the Russell County Jail.

During the search, several items were located that appeared to be stolen.

The Russell Police Department was called over to look at the items. The PD executed a separate search warrant at the same location and seized several items.

All information was forwarded to the Russell County Attorney.
