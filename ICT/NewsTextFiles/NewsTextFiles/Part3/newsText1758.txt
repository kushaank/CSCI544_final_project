
Uttaradit Senator Peerasak Porchit, the acting second Senate deputy speaker, and appointed Senator Dejudom Krairit, held talks with Mr Niwattumrong and caretaker Justice Minister Chaikasem Nitisiri yesterday at an undisclosed location to find a way out of the current political crisis. The two senators went to the talks on behalf of acting Senate Speaker Surachai Liangboonlertchai, who was said to have been taken abruptly ill.
