
On the first day of early voting for the May 27 runoff election, Lt. Gov. David Dewhurst cast a ballot for himself Monday for re-election as the candidate with the experience and character for the job, he said.

But Dewhurst said he didn’t know whether a raft of Republican rival Dan Patrick’s psychiatric records from the 1980s that were released last week suggest that the Houston state senator was in any way unfit to serve.
