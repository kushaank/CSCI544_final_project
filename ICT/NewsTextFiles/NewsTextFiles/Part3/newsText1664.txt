
Police have called for the public’s help to find a woman who has been missing since last week.

Pamela Joy Tozer was last seen on May 15 at her Mosman Park house.

She has not been in contact with her family since 9pm last Thursday and there are concerns for the 55-year-old’s safety and welfare.

Ms Tozer is fair skinned, of medium build with green eyes and grey hair.

Police said Ms Tozer may have been driving her red 1999 Lexus IS200, registration 1AOO 680.

Anyone who has seen the missing woman or her car should call 131 444.
