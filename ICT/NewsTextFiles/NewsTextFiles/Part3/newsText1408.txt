
The United Nations human rights representative in Ukraine, Ivan Šimonović, warned about the prospect of holding presidential elections in easterm Ukraine.

The UN’s human rights representative in Ukraine has warned that a dark shadow looms over the prospect of holding next weekend’s presidential election in eastern Ukraine. After visiting Donetsk in the east and Odessa in the south, UN Assistant Secretary-General for Human Rights Ivan Šimonović said the situation had deteriorated so much that the region could collapse.
