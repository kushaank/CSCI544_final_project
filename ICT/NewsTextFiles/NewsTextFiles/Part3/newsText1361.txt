
'Sri Lanka gaining a name as a medical tourism brand'

Industry and Commerce Minister Rishad Bathiudeen said Sri Lanka’s Ayurveda healthcare is garnering the attention of high end tourist market.

Lately, the Japanese travel sector has shown stronger interest in Sri Lanka’s Ayurveda tourism.

The Minister added that last week, a Japanese Member of Parliament touring Sri Lanka also has expressed of Japan’s interest in Sri Lanka’s Ayurvedic care for Japanese medical tourists.

Minister was addressing the simultaneous launch event of AROGYA 2014 and INTRAD 2014 at BMICH held on Friday.
