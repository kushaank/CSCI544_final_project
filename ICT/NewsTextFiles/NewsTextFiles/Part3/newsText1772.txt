
DOHA: The Emir H H Sheikh Tamim bin Hamad Al Thani,  also Chairman of the Supreme Council For Economic Affairs And Investment, yesterday presided over the third meeting of (SCEAI) in 2014 at the Emiri Diwan.

It was decided to develop Al Karanaa Industrial Area and fully dedicate it to small and medium enterprises (SMEs).

The Economic Zones Company affiliated to Qatar Authority for the Development of SMEs is to develop Al Karanaa Industrial Area, Qatar News Agency reported yesterday.

The meeting was attended by the Prime Minister and Interior Minister H E Sheikh Abdullah bin Nasser bin Khalifa Al Thani, also Deputy Chairman, SCEAI.

He added that the agenda also included latest developments related to the industry and petrochemicals affairs and projects of Qatar Petroleum International (QPI).

The council is generally concerned with economic and energy affairs and the investment of the country’s surplus financial resources.
