
A Framingham High School freshman passed away in a recent “sudden accidental death,” the school’s principal announced this morning. No other details about the death were immediately available, the Milford Daily News reported.

The student, Evlyn Rodriguez Tobar, had enrolled at Framingham High in late October after coming over from her native Honduras, principal Mike Welch said.

Welch said counselors and social workers would be available at the school to help students grieving the loss.

Funeral arrangements had not yet been set as of this morning, he said.
