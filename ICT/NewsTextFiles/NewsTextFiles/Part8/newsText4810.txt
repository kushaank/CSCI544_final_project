
LONDON, May 19 (Xinhua) -- A murder enquiry is underway after a woman was found dead and four children were injured in Huddersfield, central Britain, local authorities said on Monday.

A 37-year-old woman had been found with a serious stab injury at her house on Sunday and later died in hospital, the West Yorkshire Police said in the statement published on its website.

A nine-year-old boy was also found outside the house with stab wounds to his arm and was sent to hospital for treatment.

Another two boys, aged 9 and 11 respectively, and a baby girl of six-month-old were also found on the scene and were treated for minor injuries, police said.

"A 39-year-old man was detained at the scene and is being treated in hospital for serious injuries. We are not looking for anyone else in connection with this incident," said Ady Taylor, detective chief inspector.

"Enquiries are ongoing to establish the full circumstances surrounding the incident and neighbourhood policing team officers are patrolling the area, offering support and reassurance to local residents," he added.
