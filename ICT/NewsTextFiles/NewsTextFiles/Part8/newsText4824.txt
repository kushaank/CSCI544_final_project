
The Queen's Baton Relay toured the Channel Islands as part of the first leg of its trip round the British Isles.

Tom Daley brought the baton ashore in Jersey on Sunday, where it was welcomed by people from across the island.

It then travelled to Guernsey, Alderney, Sark and Herm.

The Queen's Baton Relay continues its journey to the Isle of Man, ahead of the 2014 Commonwealth Games.
