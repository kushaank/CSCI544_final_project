
The Institute of Documentary Film is looking for creative documentary projects from Central and Eastern Europe to participate in the  Ex Oriente Film  2014 training programme.

The core of the Ex Oriente Film training is based on three week-long residential workshops held over the year. Each year IDF selects up to 14 projects that receive assistance from a wide range of film experts, with each workshop focusing on a different segment of development.

The deadline for application is 1 June 2014. Apply with your documentary project through the  online entry form .
