
30 Year old Paul Davidson III was originally from Lemont.

He was on his motorcycle in Urbana, when he was hit by a car and killed Friday night.

His mother tells the Chicago Tribune that he was going for a haircut.

Davidson was set to recieve his degree in architecture from the University of Illinois, the next morning.

He was not wearing a helmet at the time of the crash.

Police reports indicate that Paul Davidson was not at fault in the accident.

A visitation will be held Thursday afternoon and evening at Calvary Church in Lemont, followed by a full military service at the church Friday at 10 a.m.
