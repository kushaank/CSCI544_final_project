FLINT, MI – A 23-year-old man accused of a March slaying at a Flint apartment complex is in custody, according to Flint police.

Darryl R. Taylor, 20, was arrested Sunday by Burton police who were responding to a 911 call.

Taylor was wanted on first-degree murder warrant in the March 30 shooting death of Dravion C. Kemp who was shot to death at Atherton East apartments near the intersection of Chambers Street and Stonegate Drive.

A second man, Darius A. Gilbert, 23, of Flint, was previously charged with first-degree murder in the case. He is in custody. Both men are also charged with carrying a concealed weapon and felony firearms.

Anyone with information on the case is asked CrimeStoppers at 1-800-SPEAK-UP or Sgt. Alfino Donastorg at 810-237-6919.
