
The man charged with a series of shootings on Kansas City-area highways pleaded not guilty to 20 felony counts on Monday.

Mohammed Whitaker is accused of shooting at 10 vehicles in Kansas City, Blue Springs and Lee's Summit.

Whitaker is charged with unlawful use of a weapon and armed criminal action in each of the 10 cases.

Three people were injured in the shootings.
