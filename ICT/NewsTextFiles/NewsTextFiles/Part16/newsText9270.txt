GULFPORT, Mississippi -- A 28-year-old Hattiesburg woman was arrested in Gulfport Friday and charged with possession of marijuana with intent to distribute.

Roxanne Michelle Gale was arrested as a result of a traffic stop, Gulfport Police Chief Leonard Papania said in a news release.

Gale was found with an undisclosed amount of marijuana and charged.

She was transported to the Harrison County Adult Detention Facility on $10,000 in bail.
