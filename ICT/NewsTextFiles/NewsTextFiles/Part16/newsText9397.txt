Fire damage inside the La Quinta Inn on S. Colorado Blvd.

DENVER - A fire at the La Quinta Inn on South Colorado Boulevard forced guests to evacuate early Monday morning.

About 42 firefighters attacked the fire that started just before 3:30 a.m. at 1975 S. Colorado Blvd., the Denver Fire Department confirmed.

The fire started in a ground floor laundry room then spread to the roof, crews told 7NEWS Reporter Lindsay Watts .

The fire appeared to be out at 4:15 a.m., Watts reported.

Officials say 18 rooms were evacuated. Six rooms are now uninhabitable.

The woman who initially called 911 had water damage in her unit but officials say guests' belongings were not damaged.
