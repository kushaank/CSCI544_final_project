MARLTON — Two men were charged and several guns were seized as part of an ongoing investigation into marijuana sales.

Evesham Police Department and Burlington County Prosecutor's Office Narcotics Task Force, through an on-going investigation into the sale of marijuana, executed a search warrant at a home in the unit block of Denver Road, Marlton, on Saturday.

-- Alex Battipaglia, 23, of Marlton, was charged with third-degree resisting arrest, fourth-degree obstruction of justice and a disorderly persons offense for possession of marijuana. He was released after posting bail.
