
Danville Police arrested two people Sunday for a shooting that left a man seriously hurt.

During their investigation, Danville officers developed 26-year-old Donavon Wade Jr. of Concord, North Carolina and 32-year-old Kimberly Carney of Lynchburg as suspects. Virginia State Police Officers and Pittsylvania County Sheriff's Deputies soon tracked them down and arrested them on Route 29 north of the city.

Wade and Carney are charged with aggravated assault.
