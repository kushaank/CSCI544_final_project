
A 21-year-old man was shot to death last night in a Pine Hills shopping plaza after he intervened in a fight, according to Orlando police.

The victim was Clausell A. Stevens Jr.

Police have released little information about what happened, except that there was a fight about 8:20 p.m. in the 4500 block of North Pine Hills Road, that Stevens tried to intervene and was killed.

This morning, police report that they have no suspects. They're asking anyone with information about the shooting to contact Crimeline at 1-800-423-TIPS (8477).
