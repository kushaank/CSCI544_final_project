
MCINTIRE | A 19-year-old from Riceville was killed Monday in a two-vehicle crash east of McIntire, in Mitchell County.

Ryan Gaffey died in the crash, confirmed Mitchell County Sheriff Greg Beaver.

Beaver said the crash happened at 12:30 p.m. on 430th Street, one mile east of McIntire. Gaffey was traveling north on Windfall Avenue when he collided with a semitrailer truck which was traveling east. The name of the semi driver has not been released. He was not injured in the crash.

Beaver said it appeared Gaffey, who was driving a pickup truck, failed to stop at a stop sign and pulled out in front of the semi. Gaffey was alone in the vehicle.

Gaffey was transported to Mitchell County Regional Health Care Center by Riceville Ambulance, where he was pronounced dead.

Riceville and LeRoy, Minn., fire departments were on the scene. Iowa State Patrol also assisted at the scene.

Beaver said the investigation continues.
