
A Sunday fire guts an abandoned home on Twenty Mile Creek in West Lincoln.

West Lincoln firefighters and emergency crews responded to a large structure fire in West Lincoln Sunday night.

Crews from both town stations and seven trucks were called to the abandoned home at 6617 Twenty Mile Rd. at about 11:20 p.m.
