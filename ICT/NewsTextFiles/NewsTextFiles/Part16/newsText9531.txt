
In what amounts to a preview of his environmental posture should he run for president, Gov. Rick Perry lashed out at President Barack Obama’s federal energy policy in a letter to the White House released Monday by the governor’s office.

The letter Friday accuses Obama of “waging a war on coal” and of “procrastination” on a controversial oil pipeline, and it takes a swipe at federal regulators.
