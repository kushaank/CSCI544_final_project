HUNTSVILLE, Alabama-- A Madison County Sheriff's Office detention officer was arrested Sunday evening while on duty at the county jail.

The sheriff's office says that Christopher Anthony Causey, 34 of Hazel Green, was arrested in relation to an alleged fight between him and his spouse.

He is charged with third-degree domestic violence. After a mandatory 12-hour holding period in the county jail, Causey will be released on a $300 cash bond.

Causey is currently on administrative leave without pay pending the outcome of the charges.

Third-degree domestic violence is considered a Class A misdemeanor and has a maximum punishment of up to one year in the county jail.
