
The interior ministry of Afghanistan following a statement said Mullah Abdullah was arrested from Pul-e-Alam city early Monday morning.

The statement further added that Mullah Abdullah was a key Taliban leader in Logar province and he was arrested during an operation by Afghan national army and Afghan national police forces.

Interior ministry said Mullah Abdullah was involved in major terrorist attacks including roadside bombings in Pul-e-Alam city.

The interior ministry in a separate statement said at least three Taliban militants were killed and two others were injured during counter-terrorism operations in the past 24 hours.

The operations were conducted by Afghan national army, Afghan national police and Afghan intelligence forces in Faryab, Zabul, Logar, Herat and Farah provinces of Afghanistan, interior ministry said, adding that Afghan security forces also confiscated various types of heavy and light weapons along with the explosives during the operations.
