
A Chinese government working group, led by Assistant Foreign Minister Liu Jianchao, is checking on Chinese workers in Vietnam. Liu told staff at a badly-hit iron and steel complex in Ha Tinh Province that the government would be evacuating them all today. Earlier, the working group held talks with Vietnamese authorities to negotiate the evacuation details.
