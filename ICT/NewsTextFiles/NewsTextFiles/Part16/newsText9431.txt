VIRGINIA BEACH, Va. (WAVY) — Virginia Beach fire crews battled flames at a home in the Croatan section of the city Monday evening.

Battalion Chief Tracey Freeman with Virginia Beach Fire-Rescue said units responded to the 500 block of Vanderbilt Avenue for a single-family house fire.

Freeman said the fire started on the third-floor deck and one person was inside at the time. However, no one was injured.

This happened around 6:20 p.m.

The cause of the fire is still under investigation. Stay with WAVY.com for updates on this developing story.
