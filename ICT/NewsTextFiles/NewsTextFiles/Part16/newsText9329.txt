
The University of Alabama softball team won the Tuscaloosa Regional final by winning three key battles at Rhoads Stadium on Sunday.

The Crimson Tide, the No. 2 overall seed in the NCAA Tournament, defeated 21st-ranked South Alabama 3-0 in front of 2,105 loud and rowdy fans by coming through at the plate in two key situations and getting a dominant pitching performance from ace Jaclyn Traina.
