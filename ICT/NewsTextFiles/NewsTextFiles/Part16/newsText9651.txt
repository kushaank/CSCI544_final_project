
CAPE TOWN - A 42-year old man accused of raping a four-year old girl near Kuruman in the Northern Cape was sent for mental evaluation on Monday.

The suspect had apparently sexually assaulted the little girl at his house near the Tsineng village earlier this month.

He will return to the Mothibistad Magistrates Court on Tuesday for a formal bail application.

According to reports, he had lured the girl to his house with biscuits before sexually assaulting her.
