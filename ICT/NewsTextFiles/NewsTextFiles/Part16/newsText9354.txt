
A fire at the La Quinta Inn on South Colorado Boulevard forced guests to evacuate early Monday morning.

About 42 firefighters attacked the fire that started just after 3 a.m. at 1975 S. Colorado Blvd., The Denver Fire Department confirmed.

The fire started in a unit and spread to the roof, crews told 7NEWS Reporter Lindsay Watts.

The fire appeared to be out at 4:15 a.m., Watts reported.
