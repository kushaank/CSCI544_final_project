
Ukraine has become one of the topics at a meeting between Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov and El Salvador’s Parliament Speaker Sigfrido Reyes, the Russian Foreign Ministry says. "The high-ranking officials exchanged views on major global problems, including the events in Ukraine," the ministry says.

Lavrov and Reyes discussed prospects for the development of cooperation between Russia and El Salvador in different fields. They noted the importance of strengthening inter-parliamentary relations within the bilateral political dialogue.

During the talks, Lavrov and Reyes confirmed the commitment to strengthening cooperation between Russia and Latin America, including "with the use of potential of the region’s major integration associations", the ministry says.
