
FIERY WRECK: The pilot of this microlight walked away unharmed.

A pilot has survived his microlight aircraft crashing this afternoon.

The crash occurred at Masterton's aerodrome runway in Solway, just before 2.30pm, police said.

The plane was "completely destroyed by fire", a Fire Service spokesman said.

"The pilot has walked away with minor injuries, thankfully. It's a lucky one."

The male pilot in his 50s was treated by paramedics at the aerodrome but not taken to hospital.
