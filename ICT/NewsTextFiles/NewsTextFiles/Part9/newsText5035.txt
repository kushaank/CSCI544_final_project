
VII Education Week will be held in Ufa with participation of the members of the SCO, which includes Kyrgyzstan. The event "Education without Limits" will be held for the first time. Rectors of the SCO universities, representatives of the Russian  Academy of Sciences, scientists, education and culture workers will attend the event.

Organizers of the international forum: the Ministry of Education and Science of the Russian Federation, federal agency on compatriots' issues living abroad, the Government of Bashkortostan, Bashkir State  Pedagogical University named after M.Akmulla.

Delegates will pay special attention to the development of international cooperation in the sphere of education and science in the framework of economic and pedagogical trends, as well as the system of training of highly qualified personnel for the national economies of the SCO countries, ensuring the formation of the Eurasian educational space. Participants will also discuss the implementation of a joint research programs and projects.

Recall that the presidency of the Shanghai Cooperation Organization will switch to Russia in autumn 2014. In 2015, international summits of the SCO heads of states and BRICS governments will be held in Ufa.
