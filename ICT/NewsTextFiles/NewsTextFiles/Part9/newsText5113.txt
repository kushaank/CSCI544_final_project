
Director-General Roberto Azevêdo, in a lecture at the Institute for Global Economics in Seoul on 16 May 2014, thanked Korea for playing “a crucial role in delivering the Bali package”. For the post-Bali work of the WTO, he said that “as a middle grounder in the Doha negotiations, Korea can help to bridge the gap between developed and developing country members”. During his visit to Korea, the Director-General met with President Park Geun-hye.
