
People’s Mayor of Slavyansk Vyacheslav Ponomaryov told Interfax on Monday night that Ukrainian army open fire practically at the same time every day and shoot from archers and trench mortars. The mayor said that Kramatorsk and the suburb of Slavyansk Semyonovka came under fire on Monday.

Ponomaryov said that the rebels accepted the battle as a result of which two people were injured in Semyonovka. Both of them were sent to hospital.

Earlier, the press-service of the self-declared Donetsk People’s Republic reported a fight in the vicinity of the Uspenka village which is only four kilometres away from the Russian border.
