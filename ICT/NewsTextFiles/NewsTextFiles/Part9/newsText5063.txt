
GALWAY, IRELAND--May 16, 2014 --Written by Martin Lynch, European News Editor for Industrial Info (Galway, Ireland) Ireland has started the process of changing its energy mix from an over reliance on imported gas and coal to more renewables, interconnections and a smarter electricity grid.

The government's new Green Paper on Energy kicks off a 10-week consultation process that will drastically change the country's energy future.
