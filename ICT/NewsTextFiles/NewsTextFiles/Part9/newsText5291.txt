
Kyrgyzstan considers the most profitable offers on Manas International Airport OJSC, the Minister of Transport and Communications Kalykbek Sultanov said today to journalists.

According to him, "there are a lot of talks about the airport, but so far only offers are received." "From the Russian, Chinese, Korean, a lot of companies. And we consider them, which are profitable and convenient for us. Now there are talks that we signed an agreement," the Minister said.

Recall, Rosneft was going to acquire 50 percent stake in the airport, but later it was reported that the company refused to bargain.

"The issue of Rosneft is open," Kalykbek Sultanov said.
