
Ensour said the government will offer facilities and incentives for investments at industrial estates, mainly in governorates to achieve sustainable development, provide job opportunities for the youth and transfer technology.

He instructed the JIEC to accelerate the process of studying and designing new industrial estates that will be established in order to float tenders for this purpose.

Director General of the corporation Luay Sahwil presented a briefing on the corporation's plans, accomplishments and future plans.

The meeting was held as part of the efforts to implement the Royal directives to establish industrial estates in all governorates across the Kingdom.
