
AT LEAST 36 people - eight soldiers and 28 militants - were killed and 25 soldiers were wounded during armed clashes between the military and Mouvement National pour la Liberation de l'Azawad (MNLA) militants near the regional governor's office - which was seized by the militants - in the city of Kidal in Mali's Kidal region on 17 May, Al-Jazeera, BBC and Reuters reported. Four civil servants and 24 soldiers were also taken hostage by the group.
