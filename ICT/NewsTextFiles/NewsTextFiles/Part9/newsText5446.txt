
The Granby Police Department tells us at about 7:30 a.m. this morning they responded to a report of a pedestrian being struck by a vehicle.

Police say RaNae Powell, 72 of Granby, was walking southbound along the northbound side of South Main Street when a southbound vehicle crossed into the northbound lane and struck Powell from behind.

Powell was pronounced dead at the scene by emergency responders.  Authorities believe Powell was killed on impact.

The incident is still under investigation.  At this stage in the investigation, authorities say charges are not being sought against the driver.
