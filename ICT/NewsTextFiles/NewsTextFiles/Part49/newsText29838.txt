
SIOUX CITY, Iowa (KTIV) Members of the 185th Air Refueling Wing in Sioux City are honored as top in the nation for the work, in the kitchen.

In March, they were judged on the quality of the food service and hospitality.

Over the weekend, they were awarded the Air Guard's Kenneth Disney Award of Excellence.  It was part of the the National Restaurant Associations Hotel-Motel show in Chicago.

This recognizes them as the best dining facility in the Air National Guard.
