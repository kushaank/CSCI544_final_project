
Asia was the major rice exporting destination for Vietnam rice so far in May 2014, with about 82,471 tons or about 46% of total rice exports. The Americas was the second largest destination, accounting for of about 63,014 tons of rice exports (equivalent to about 35% of total rice exports in May 2014). Vietnam also exported 27,358 tons of rice to the Africa, about 6,477 tons of rice to Europe, about 1,444 tons of rice to the Middle East, and about 134 tons of rice to Australia so far in May 2014.
