
New Delhi: Narendra Modi has thanked world leaders for their good  wishes over Bharatiya Janata Party's landslide victory in Lok Sabha  elections and underscored India's strong ties with countries like the  US, Japan, Russia and Nepal.

In his tweets, he also thanked the prime ministers of New Zealand and Fiji for their good wishes.
