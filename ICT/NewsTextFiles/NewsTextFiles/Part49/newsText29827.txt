
One person was killed and four others injured Sunday night in a two-vehicle crash northwest of Salisbury, the N.C. Highway Patrol says.

The crash was reported shortly after 9:30 p.m. on Woodleaf Road at U.S. 601. That is a short distance north of U.S. 70, near the Salisbury city limits.

Troopers said a car carrying two people and an SUV carrying five people slammed into each other. Troopers said they are still trying to determine exactly what caused the two vehicles to crash.
