
RENO, Nev. (MyNews4.com & KRNV) -- With summer just around the corner, Bowers Mansion is gearing up for the tourist season.

Volunteers headed down to Washoe Valley  to get the Wells Fargo Bowers Mansion Park in tip top shape for summer visitors. The beautification project had volunteers planting flowers and performing critical fuel reductions to make the park safer. According to volunteers, the clean-up project is all about making the park a safe place and keeping the community together.
