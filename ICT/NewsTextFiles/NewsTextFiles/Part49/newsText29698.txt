
A former news director at KBTX was found dead at his home Sunday afternoon, according to College Station police.

Officers responding to a 2:30 p.m. call from a family member found Mike George deceased.

George served as news director of KBTX Media from 2008 to 2013. More recently he was developing business opportunities in Bryan-College Station. He continued to contribute at KBTX, officials there said.

George, an Emmy-winning journalist, began his news career at KBTX as an anchor, reporter and executive producer in 1986. He has worked in news stations in Austin, San Antonio, Atlanta and Las Vegas, according to a KBTX news release written when he was hired this last time.

Throughout his career, George won more than two dozen awards for his work, including Emmys and Edward R. Murrow awards.

He graduated from Texas State University and served on the board of Texas Associated Press Broadcasters. He was 51, according to police.
