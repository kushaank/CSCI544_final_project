
MOSCOW: Russian Foreign Minister Sergei Lavrov has called on the Organization for Security and Cooperation in Europe and the United Nations to ensure an "open and impartial" probe by Kiev into deaths that occurred in the Ukrainian city of Odessa this month, the Foreign Ministry said on Monday.
