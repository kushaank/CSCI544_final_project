
Oleg Sidyakin and Marat Saichenko should be released immediately, the press service for the Russian embassy in Kyiv said in a statement released on May 19.

 The Russian Foreign Ministry said it demanded Kyiv stop the tendency to  detain Russian reporters over accusations with no proof and release them  unconditionally, the situation is under control of Russian Foreign  Minister Sergei Lavrov.
