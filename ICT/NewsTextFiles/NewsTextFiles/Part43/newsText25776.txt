
Portugal's industrial producer prices declined for the ninth straight month in April, but at a slower pace than in the previous month, figures from Statistics Portugal showed Monday.

The producer price index fell 0.6 percent year-on-year in April, after a 1.5 percent drop in the previous month, revised from 1.4 percent. Producer prices have been falling since August last year.

The producer price index in the manufacturing industry dropped 1.3 percent annually in April, following a 2.4 percent fall in March.

On a monthly basis, producer prices rose 0.1 percent in April, after a 0.2 percent fall in the previous month. It was the first decline in three months.
