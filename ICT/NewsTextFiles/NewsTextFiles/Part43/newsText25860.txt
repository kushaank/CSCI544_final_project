
(MENAFN - Qatar News Agency) Ha may 19 (qna) - the advisory council held on monday morning its regular weekly meeting of its 42nd regular session under the chairmanship of its speaker he mohammed bin mubarak al khulaifi.

at the outset of the meeting he the secretary-general of the advisory council fahad bin mubarak al khayareen read the agenda which was then agreed upon. then the council endorsed the minutes of its previous session.
