
ISLAMABAD: Analyst Maqbul Elahi Malik said that incumbent government has taken enormous steps to improve Railways as it is backbone of the economy.

He said that Minister for Railways had turned Railways into a profitable department by overcoming corruption, Radio Pakistan reported.

He said that Prime Minister Nawaz Sharif personally was monitoring performance of Minister for Railways who had introduced innovative measures in Railways.

He said that cargo service by trains would be a profitable venture because Pakistan Railways would cater cargo requirements of China, Afghanistan and Central Asian Republics.

He said that revival of metro train services would be an important step of the government because it would reduce traffic burden from our roads as metro trains run within major cities and inter cities had cast positive impacts on environment and were attractions for tourists in developed countries.
