
ISLAMABAD: Pakistan and Sweden possess great potential for boosting up existing bilateral trade ties between the two countries particularly in different areas of agriculture and agricultural research.

Federal Minister for Ministry of National Food Security and Research (MNFSR) Sikandar Hayat Khan Bosan in a meeting with trade representatives and businessmen of a Swedish trade delegation along with Ambassador Jonas Hafstrom special adviser to the Minister for Trade of Sweden said Pakistan was an attractive destination for investment in the field of agriculture.

He invited Swedish entrepreneurs and businessmen to explore agriculture market potential for the joint ventures with Pakistani business community and expressed hope that this would open up new avenues of development for both countries.
