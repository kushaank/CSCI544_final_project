
ACCORDING to the pro-opposition Syrian Observatory for Human Rights, four civilians were killed during a suicide attack by an Ajnad al-Sham militant near a security checkpoint in the Masaken al-Haras area of the city of Qudsaya in Syria's Rif Dimashq governorate on 14 May. Casualty figures provided by the Syrian Observatory for Human Rights cannot be independently verified but have been assessed to be the most reliable.
