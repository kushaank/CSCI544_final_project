(TRNS) — Russian President Vladimir Putin on Monday said that he had ordered the removal of Russian troops from the Ukrainian border and ordered them to report back to their respective bases.

Russia reportedly had more than 40,000 troops placed close to the Ukraine border in what Putin has characterized as military exercises.

The move comes ahead of Sunday’s elections in Ukraine, but NATO has maintained that there isn’t any evidence of troop movement, despite Russia’s statement to the contrary.

The Russian government has repeatedly said that the outcome of the March 25 elections will be invalid due to what it says is interference from the West.
