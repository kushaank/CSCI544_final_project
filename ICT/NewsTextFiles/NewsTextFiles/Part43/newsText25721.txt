
Zamboanga City – Combined elements of the military and the police arrested last Saturday (May 17) an Abu Sayyaf member suspected of planning bombing activities here.

Sr. Supt. Angelito Casimiro, Police Office Acting Director of this city, identified the suspect as Nujir Ahidji, also known as Abu Jomar, a native of Jolo Sulu and temporarily residing in Upper Calarian here.

According to reports, Jomar was arrested by virtue of a search warrant issued by RTC Executive Judge Hon. Gregorio dela Peña against the suspect and a certain Usradz Hassan.
