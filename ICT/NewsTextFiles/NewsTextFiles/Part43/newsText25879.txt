
The figure given by the Observatory for Human Rights says that it includes rebels, civilians and Syrian military members. Fighters from both parties are also included, those who fight for President Bashar al-Assad, militiamen and foreign fighters who support Assad’s cause.

The activist network on Syrian grounds serve as the observatory’s basis for their information tally. The United Nations however, have halted their Syrian death tally since the verification of information has been difficult. Their last count being 100,00 last July.
