GRAND TRAVERSE COUNTY, MI – Police arrested five suspects linked to heroin and the production of methamphetamine.

Sheriff’s deputies responded early Saturday, May 17, to a motel in East Bay Township and arrested a fugitive.

The arrest led to search warrants being served at two other motel rooms, and the arrests of five: a 17-year-old Detroit man, and two men, ages 25 and 26, and two women, 23 and 31, all of the Grand Traverse County area.

Police then used a search warrant to uncover a methamphetamine lab in a storage unit in Garfield Township.

The Sheriff’s Department worked with Traverse Narcotics Team, or TNT, on the investigation. A state police meth response team also responded.

Police said the suspects faced multiple charges related to heroin and methamphetamine.

To report illegal drug activity, call the TNT tip line at 231-338-0868. Anyone with information regarding this case can contact detective Lt. Dan King at 231-492-5492.
