
Chennai: Another political drama is unfolding in Tamil Nadu over resignations after the Lok Sabha election results. The Chennai Police has arrested 11 DMK cadre for allegedly attacking media personnel outside party leader MK Stalin's house.

This came after DMK cadre allegedly attacked media personnel for calling MK Stalin's resignation a political stunt. While the DMK has apologised, the party has denied that any of its workers were involved in the attack.

This after DMK treasurer and chief Karunanidhi's son MK Stalin first offered to resign and then withdrew his offer on Sunday. DMK workers were protesting outside Stalin's house when the attack reportedly took place.
