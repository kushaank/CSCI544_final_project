
SIALKOT,  The Traffic Police have arrested an a person Asad Imran for issuing the fake international driving licenses to local people after extorting heavy amounts from them here.

DSP Traffic Sialkot Muhammad Waseem Akhtar told the newsmen that police have registered a case against the accused and recovered three fake international driving licenses from him.

Police have sent the accused behind the bars and further investigations are underway in this regard.
