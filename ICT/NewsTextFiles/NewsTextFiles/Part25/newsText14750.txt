
Taipei, May 19 (CNA) A woman was washed away in a river that was welling up as torrential rains hit Nantou County, central Taiwan, Monday evening.

As of 8:00 p.m., police and fire fighters were still searching for the woman, who along with her husband were stranded in the river in Beigang Village, Guoxing Township of Nantou County.  The man was safe.
