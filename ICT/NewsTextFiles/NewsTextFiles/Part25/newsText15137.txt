GRESHAM, Ore. -- A California man was stabbed to death in a Gresham apartment Sunday night, police said.

Police were called just after 3 a.m. to the report of a stabbing in the 17900 block of East Burnside Street.

Officers at the scene found a 32-year-old man with a stab wound in need of medical attention, said Detective John Rasmussen of the Gresham Police Department.

Emergency medical technicians arrived but were unable to save the man's life,  Rasmussen said.

Investigators said all involved parties have been accounted for, but asked that anyone with information about the stabbing call Gresham police at 503-618-2719.
