
A teenager died after he was tasered by police following a fight with hospital security staff.

Baltimore City Police are investigating the death of the teen, who fell into a coma after the incident at the Good Samaritan Hospital in the Maryland, America, city.

Cops attempted to sedate the unnamed 19-year-old after he was involved in a physical fight with at least five staff while he was being treated for emotional distress.

A hospital spokesperson said: "There are sometimes circumstances that threaten the safety of our staff, which necessitate police intervention."

According to police reports, he was given unknown amounts of medication before they arrived on scene.

He died on Wednesday, however police didn't know the full extent of his injuries until days later.

Deputy Commissioner Jerry Rodriguez said: "The person was breathing when the officers left the hospital.

"It was not learned that the individual was in a coma and was possibly brain dead until several days after this incident.
