BROOMFIELD, Colo. - Broomfield Police have issued a missing person alert for an at-risk teenager with developmental disabilities.

Police said Erin Rachelle Huss, 19, ran away from a group home Sunday night.

Huss is approximately 5'6" tall, weighs about 125 pounds and has brown eyes and short brown hair.

She was last seen wearing a grey tank top, and black and white stripped sweater with dark grey pants.

If you see Huss, call Detective Dale Hammell at 303-464-5733 or the Broomfield Police Department at 303-438-6400.
