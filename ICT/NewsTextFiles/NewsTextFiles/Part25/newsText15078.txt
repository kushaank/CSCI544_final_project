Dover, Del.

The Delaware State Police joined forces with 14 other states in a “Drive to Save Lives” enforcement initiative for eight hours on May 17, which netted more than 1,000 traffic violations on Delaware roadways.

All 15 states that line Interstate 95 from Maine to Florida participated in the intensified enforcement initiative, led and coordinated by the Delaware State Police.
