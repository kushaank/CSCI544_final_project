
A man and a woman wound up dead outside a Delaware community clubhouse.

Delaware State Police say troopers are investigating the deaths of a man and woman found in the parking lot of a community clubhouse in Sussex County.

Police say troopers found 63-year-old Edward Watkins Sr. and his 53-year-old wife, Robin Watkins. Both were pronounced dead at the scene.

A handgun was found at the scene.

Investigators say the two lived just over the state line in Bishopville, Maryland.
