
Dothan police arrested a Panama City, Fla., man over the weekend on a drug trafficking charge.

Court records show Dothan police investigators arrested Russell Logan Walker, 33, of Panama City, Fla., on Saturday and charged him with felony trafficking a controlled substance.

Records show police charged Walker with trafficking more than 2 ounces of synthetic marijuana, also known as spice.

Walker was released from custody at the Houston County Jail after he posted $30,000 bail.
