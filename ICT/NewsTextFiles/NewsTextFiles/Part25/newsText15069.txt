
GRANITE CITY, Ill. (KMOX) - Illinois State Police have joined the probe into the murder of a 43-year-old Pontoon Beach man who apparently was shot in the head while riding his motorcycle.

A Madison County Sheriff’s deputy discovered what appeared to be a motorcycle crash around 3:30 a.m. Sunday in a rural area near Granite City.

The deputy found the body of Travis Mayes next to his motorcycle. He had been shot in the head.

A caller to 911 provided information about the shooting. A 22-year-old Pontoon Beach man has been arrested and is being held pending charges.

Illinois State Police Captain James Morrisey said the men “know each other,” but he couldn’t give details.
