ST. LOUIS • A man was found shot to death about 2:40 p.m. Monday in the 5500 block of Delmar Boulevard, police say.

Lemoryne Borders, 41, had been shot multiple times and was pronounced dead in a common area near a stairwell inside the Clara Court Apartments, police say.

Borders lived in north St. Louis County near Chambers and Bellefontaine roads..

Capt. John Hayden said detectives believe Borders was at the apartment building to pick up a relative when he was shot.

He had been shot in the head, hand, both shoulders and back. He was found in the interior entry way of the apartment building.

Police say other people inside the building at the time of the shooting gave conflicting accounts about what happened.
