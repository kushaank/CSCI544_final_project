
A 3-year-old Northwest Side girl died from multiple injuries suffered from child abuse and police are questioning a person of interest in her killing, authorities said.

The girl, identified as Ashley Mendoza, died from multiple injuries from child abuse in a homicide, the Cook County medical examiner's office determined following an autopsy this afternoon. Officers found the girl dead inside her home in the 3300 block of North Natchez Avenue about 8:30 p.m. Sunday, police said. She was pronounced dead on the scene, according to the medical examiner's office.

A police supervisor on the scene declined to comment.
