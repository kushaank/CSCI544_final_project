
JAJARKOT, MAY 19 -   Police have seized 323 pieces of socket bombs in Jajarkot district.

Chief District Officer, Chakrapani Pandey, said that that the socket bombs were confiscated from the house of Jay Bahadur Thapa of Afgan of Suwanauli-2.

Thapa found the bombs from the house while constructing a new house after demolishing the old one, added Pandey.
