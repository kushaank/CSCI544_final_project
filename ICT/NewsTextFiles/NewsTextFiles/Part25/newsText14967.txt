
A woman has died following a head-on car crash in Waikato.

The crash occured on State Highway 3 in Ohaupo at around 5.50pm. The woman's car crossed the centre line into the path of two cars and a truck travelling in the opposite direction.

She received fatal injuries, while another person was airlifted to Waikato Hospital suffering from minor to moderate injuries.

Police say emergency services are still working to clear the scene and people driving between Hamilton and Te Awamutu tonight should take alternative routes or expect major delays.

The crash is still under investigation by the Waikato Serious Crash Unit and Police say it is too early to say what contributed to the incident.
