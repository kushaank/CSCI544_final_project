TERRE HAUTE, Ind. (WTHI) – A Terre Haute woman could soon join her husband in prison after submitting a guilty plea.

Jennifer Mahoney pleaded guilty to a felony charge of Sexual Misconduct with a Minor.

She and her husband, Matthew Mahoney were arrested in April 2013 in connection to sexual activity with a 14-year-old girl.

Matthew Mahoney is already serving a 10 year sentence.

Vigo County Prosecutor Terre Modesitt told News 10 that the plea agreement caps Jennifer’s sentence at six years.

Attorneys will argue sentencing on June 10th.
