
Russia assisted relief materials worth of AFN 20 million for flood affected in Northern provinces of Afghanistan. The relief materials were handed-over by Russian Ambassador to H.E. Wais Ahmad Barmak, Deputy of National Emergency Committee and Minister of Rural Rehabilitation and Development.

Relief materials are include: 5.5 ton milk, 5.5 ton fish conserve, 3 ton sugar, 1500 blankets, three large-size tents and kitchen kits. The materials will be distributed on those who have been affected by the recent flash floods and landslides.
