All active, retired and honorably-discharged members of the United States Armed Forces will receive free admission to the park. All military members will be required to have a valid ID, including active duty ID, retired personnel ID, VA ID or a copy of DD-214 discharge papers and a photo ID.

Military members can also receive free admission into Soak City, which opens its gates May 24.
