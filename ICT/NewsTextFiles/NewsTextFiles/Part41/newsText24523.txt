
Broomfield-based Ball Corp. plans to build a beverage can plant in Myanmar, officials for the packaging and aerospace company announced Monday.

The one-line plant is under a long-term contract to supply Coca-Cola Pinya Beverages, officials said, adding that the facility also will supply other local, regional and multi-national customers.

The Myanmar plant is expected to begin production in mid-2015.

Financial terms were not immediately available.
