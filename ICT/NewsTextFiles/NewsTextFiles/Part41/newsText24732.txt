
Vodafone Germany's discount brand Otelo has launched new smartphone tariffs to replace existing tariffs, teltarif.de reports. The new tariffs offer inclusive units and data allowances for smartphone users. Targeted at normal users, the Otelo Smartphone 300 pre-paid option offers 300 units for calls and texts in Germany, an Otelo community flat for calls and texts, plus 300 MB high-speed data for EUR 8.99 per month. The starter pack costs EUR 9.95, with the first month free. The Otelo Allnet Basic XS plan offers 250 free units and 250 MB high-speed data for EUR 9.09 per month, and the Otelo Allnet Basic S plan offers 500 units and 250 MB data for EUR 14.99 per month. Both these tariffs are offered with minimum 24-month contracts.
