
Makedonski Telekom and T-Mobile have selected the project that will be the Macedonian representative at the finals of the Deutsche Telekom Innovation Contest 2014 in Krakow, Poland. This year, 39 countries entered the contest with 326 ideas. After the selection in the semi-finals, twelve finalists will compete in Krakow on 27 June. The Solar Irrigation Pump project developed by students Dimitar Minovski and Kristijan Gjorgjevski was selected as the winner from ten Macedonian finalists.
