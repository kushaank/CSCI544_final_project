
In the first major ceasefire violation since the 2014 Lok Sabha results were announced, one army soldier was killed while two others injured when they were attacked by guerrilla gunmen at the Jammu border.

On Sunday, two soldiers in Akhnoor area of Jammu district had stepped on a landmine that triggered an explosion injuring both of them. The patrol injured on Monday had gone to evacuate the soldiers injured the previous day, and were attacked by gunmen from the other side.

The army had foiled another infiltration bid by militants from across the LOC on Sunday in Jammu and Kashmir's Poonch district. A high alert has been sounded in the area.
