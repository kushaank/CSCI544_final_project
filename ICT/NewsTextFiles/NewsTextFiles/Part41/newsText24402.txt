
The Dontesk region self-defense forces have managed to defend two roadblocks as a result of overnight clashes between federalization supporters and the National Guard in Kramatorsk, the local militia headquarters told Interfax on Monday.

"The attack on our roadblocks by national guard squads has been repelled. At this moment, the national guard is in the village of Semyonovka near Kramatorsk," a source said.

The fighting in Kramatorsk continued until this morning, he said. The attack on the town ended at 6:30 a.m. local time.

There have been no reports yet about fatalities and injuries.
