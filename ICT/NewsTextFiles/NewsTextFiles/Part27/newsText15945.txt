
A man was shot in the leg in Baltimore Monday afternoon, police said.

The victim was in the 2000 block of Sinclair Lane in the Clifton Park neighborhood when a man approached him, shot him in the leg and ran off, police said. Officers responded at 1:18 p.m., and the man was taken to an area hospital for treatment.

No suspect identity or motive was known, police said. The shooting is under investigating, and anyone with information may call Northeast District detectives at 410-396-2444.
