
New Delhi: Delhi's Lt Governor Najeeb Jung on Monday directed the Municipal Corporations to complete the desilting work by May end, before the monsoon starts. Jung also urged the corporation officials to initiate strict criminal proceedings against those dumping waste and debris at unauthorized sites within the capital city.

He instructed the Municipal Corporations to carry out the desilting work of drains less than 4 feet in all unauthorized colonies, irrespective of the agency dealing with the development works there.

Najeeb Jung said that suitable land would be allocated by DDA for dumping sites very soon.

The Lt Governor also instructed Delhi Metro Rail Corporation (DMRC) not to leave debris at their construction site and to improve their traffic management at sites. He also instructed BSES and TPDDL to ensure that no digging is done during the monsoon period.

Jung took a review meeting in this regard with senior officers of the Delhi Government on Monday. The officers briefed the Lt Governor on their preparedness for the forthcoming monsoon and the desilting of drains carried out under their respective jurisdiction.
