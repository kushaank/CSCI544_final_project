
Kabul (BNA) The senior military authorities to Afghanistan, Pakistan and ISAF are going to hold meeting on Monday, this was stated by defense ministry spokesman General Zahir Azimi on Sunday.

In talks with BNA, Azimi said that 37th tripartite meeting at the level of chief of army staffs is scheduled to be held in Kabul in Monday with the objective to bring harmony in the war on terror and enhance security cooperation.

General Shir Mohammad Karimi, his Pakistani counterpart Gen. Sharif Rahel and the commander of NATO-led ISAF troops in Afghanistan Gen. Dunford attend the tripartite meeting.
