
NEW DELHI: All four AAP MPs, who were elected from Punjab in the recently concluded Lok Sabha election, met party chief Arvind Kejriwal on Monday to discuss the party's strategy in the state, said a party leader.

The newly-elected MPs, actor-comedian Bhagwant Mann (Sangrur), Sadhu Singh (Faridkot), Harinder Singh Khalsa (Fatehgarh Sahib) and Dharam Vira Gandhi (Patiala), met Kejriwal at his residence here to discuss the party's next course of action in Punjab, said the leader.

They also discussed the Aam Aadmi Party's strategy for Punjab's assembly elections scheduled for 2017 and also the upcoming local body election, said the leader.
