
EAST LANSING (WKZO) -- Drug arrests at Michigan State University are shooting up. The "Lansing State Journal" reports arrests from 2010 to 2012 were up 158-percent. While university officials tell the newspaper there isn't a drug problem on campus, arrests went from 101 to 261. MSU police add a lot of the arrests have involved non-students, and they haven't stepped up their enforcement.
