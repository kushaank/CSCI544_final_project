
KATHMANDU, MAY 19 - Indian prime minister to be Narendra Modi has assured that he will stress and focus on strengthening good bilateral relations between Nepal and India. Modi made such remarks during a telephone conversation with senior UCPN (Maoist) leader Baburam Bhattarai on Sunday. Bhattari made a call on Modi and congratulated him for the victory of BJP and Modi during the election.

While extending his best wishes to Modi for his successful tenure as PM, Bhattarai hoped that the Nepal- India relations will further strengthen under the leadership, said a statement issued by Bhattarai’s aide Bishowdeep Pandey.
