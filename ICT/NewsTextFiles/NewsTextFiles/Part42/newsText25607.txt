
Islamabad ,Group Chairman of Meinhardt Group (Singapore) Mr. Shahzad Naseem called on Prime Minister Muhammad Nawaz Sharif at Islamabad on Monday. Secretary Railways Ms. Praveen Agha, Chairman CDA Mr. Maroof Afzal and Chairman NHA Shahid Ashraf Tarar also attended the meeting.

The Prime Minister apprised the group about the various avenues of investment in Pakistan. Pakistan provides investment opportunities in all sectors of economies including industry, infrastructure, communications and energy. Maximum facilitation has been provided for investors, PM told.

Mr. Shahzad Naseem, the Group Chairman Meinhardt, appreciated the efforts of the government to revive the economy of Pakistan adding that investment friendly policies of the present government has made Pakistan the most attractive destinations for the foreign investors.
