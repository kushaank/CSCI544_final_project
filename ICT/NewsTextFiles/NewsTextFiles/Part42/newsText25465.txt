
AUCKLAND - Following a rigorous process, the University of Auckland has appointed JWT as its advertising agency partner.

The University of Auckland joins other great New Zealand brands at JWT, with the likes of Contact Energy, Trade Me Jobs, Sacred Hill Wines and Z Energy choosing JWT as their agency partner in recent years.
