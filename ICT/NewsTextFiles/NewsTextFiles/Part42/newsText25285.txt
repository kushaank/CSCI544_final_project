
The Nigeria Governors’ Forum (NGF) on Monday called on all Nigerians to support the Federal Government in the search for the kidnapped school girls.

This is contained in a statement released in Abuja on Monday and signed by the Administrator of the Forum, Osaro Onaiwu.

“Be temperate in your utterances and remain focused on the ultimate goal which is the peaceful rescue of the girls,’’ the statement quotes the forum as saying.

It called on the security agencies not to relent in their efforts to rescuing the missing girls.

It commended the security agencies for their sacrifices at ensuring that Nigeria remained one indivisible nation.

The News Agency of Nigeria (NAN) recalls that more than 200 girls, who were sitting for WAEC, were abducted on April 14 from the Government Girls Secondary School in Chibok, Borno.
