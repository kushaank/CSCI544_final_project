Take the kids to Bounce U to burn off some of that late-afternoon energy, and help raise money for Make a Wish Foundation in the process. During this one-day event, Bounce U will donate all discounted open bounce revenue to the foundation serving children with terminal illnesses and their families.

DETAILS >> 3 p.m. Wednesday, May 21. Bounce U, 1166 S. Gilbert Road, Gilbert. $5 per bouncer for a 1-hour session; this including raffles, vendors, treats, bouncing, and face painting. Registration, socks and pre-payment are required. (480) 632-9663 or BounceU.com .
