
IRANIAN actress Leila Hatami has landed herself in hot water over a kiss on the cheek in Cannes.

Iran's Deputy Culture Minister Hossein Noushabadihas been quoted by the website of state broadcaster IRIB, saying the actress had stepped "out of line".

He said: "Those who attend intentional events should take heed of the credibility and chastity of Iranians, so that a bad image of Iranian women will not be demonstrated to the world.

"Iranian woman is the symbol of chastity and innocence."
