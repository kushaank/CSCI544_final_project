
The three-member bench headed by Justice Jawwad S. Khawaja comprising Justice Gulzar Ahmed and Justice Mushir Alam resumed hearing of the case regarding wireless spectrum auction for the 3rd Generation Telecommunication Services and Transparent Licensing of 3G services in Pakistan.

During the course of proceedings, Chairman Pakistan Telecommunication Authority (PTA) Dr. Ismail Shah informed the court that the government has auctioned 3G and 4G spectrum on April 23.

He said that four 3G licenses and one 4G license had been auctioned and the government earned $111 billion while the Federal Board of Revenue received 10 per cent tax from the companies.

The court disposed of the case on the report of Chairman PTA.
