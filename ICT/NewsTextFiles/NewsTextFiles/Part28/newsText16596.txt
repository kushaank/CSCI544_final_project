
SANA'A, May 19 (Saba)- Undersecretary of State for Political Affairs Dr. Hameed al-Awadhi discussed with Deputy German ambassador to Yemen bilateral cooperation between the two countries and ways of enhancing it.

The meeting dealt with the role that could be played by Germany within the European Union to ease the ban on air cargo imposed on Yemen by the Union.

They also discussed the cooperation in the field of training and rehabilitation of Yemeni diplomats in Germany, as well as the situation of Yemeni students studying in Germany.
