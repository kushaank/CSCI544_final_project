
A Spring Hill High School student is being questioned after a threat was posted on Facebook on Monday regarding a gun at the high school.

The issue was immediately brought to the attention of school officials, and no weapon was found, according to a USD 230 news release.

Law enforcement officials ensured that students and staff were safe and began questioning the student who allegedly posted the threat, according to the release.
