About 1,715 students around the state of Idaho rejoiced Saturday as they earned a combined 1,805 degrees from the University of Idaho.

This year the university celebrated its 125th anniversary, its 119th commencement ceremony and the first of new university President Chuck Staben in the ASUI-Kibbie Activity Center.
