
Scottsbluff Public Schools has been awarded a preschool expansion grant to help increase Early Childhood Education opportunities for the district.

On May 9, the Nebraska Department of Education approved SBPS to receive a $125,000 grant with $25,000 of that amount to be used for startup funds. Those funds will include building accommodations that may be needed. With this grant, the district will be including an additional full-day class session for 4-year-old children in the district next fall to serve a total of 120 students. As of May 19, the district has registered 103 students for the 2014-15 school year.
