Hundreds of students participated in the Hanover High School's mock election on May 9.

The inaugural event was geared at introducing students to local government and teaching the importance of participating in all elections.

Organized by senior student Kristen Daudelin with the help of Hanover Town Clerk Catherine Harder-Bernier, the mock election mirrored that held one day later by the town. Students cast ballots for their picks for town offices.
