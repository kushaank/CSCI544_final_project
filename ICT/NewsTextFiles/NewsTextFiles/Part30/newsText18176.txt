
A single-vehicle accident west of West Jersey claimed the life of a 29-year-old Toulon man early Sunday morning.

Stark County Sheriff Jimmie Dison pronounced Jason P. Jackson dead at the scene  on the West Jersey Blacktop (300N) near 100E.

Authorities responded to a call from the OnStar vehicle security system in Jackson’s pickup truck at 2:04 a.m.

An autopsy was scheduled in Peoria on Sunday.

The accident remains under investigation.

The Stark County Ambulance and the Toulon Fire Department assisted at the scene.
