
Two suspected drug pushers were arrested in anti-illegal  drugs operations in Dumaguete City, Negros Oriental, Saturday.

Roland Buenavista Valencia, “alias Tatay”, of Barangay  Daro, was nabbed at around 12:35 p.m. in a buy-bust operation in the barangay by  operatives of the Provincial Anti-Illegal Drugs Special Operations Task Group, led  by Insp. Ryan Jay Orapa, the Dumaguete  police, and the local Philippine Drug Enforcement Agency.

Orapa said Valencia  sold a sachet of suspected shabu, worth around P2,000, to an undercover  agent. The P500 in marked bills used in the transaction were also recovered from him.

Gerardo Alcares Patagoc, “alias Gerard”, of Barangay  Looc, also in Dumaguete, was nabbed in a buy-bust operation in Barangay  Tabuc-tubig, at around 3:30 p.m., by the PAIDSOTG, Dumaguete police and the PDEA.  Confiscated from him were a sachet of suspected shabu, worth about P6,000, and a  P500 marked bill, Orapa said.

Valencia and Patagoc are now detained, while charges for  violation of Republic Act 9165, or the Comprehensive Dangerous Drugs Act of  2002, are being readied for filing against them. The two are in the police’s watch list of alleged drug  personalities.
