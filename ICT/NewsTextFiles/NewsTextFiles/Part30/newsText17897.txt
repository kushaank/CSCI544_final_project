
The Income and Tax Ministry of Ukraine has made a final decision not to introduce a tax on the turnover of companies, First Deputy Income and Tax Minister of Ukraine Ihor Bilous has said.

"I want to remove the issue of the tax on turnover of companies. We won't introduce it, as we're going to Europe," he said at a meeting with businessmen in Kyiv on Monday.
