SHELBY COUNTY, Alabama - Seven Republican candidates are seeking the party's nomination in the June 3 primary for Alabama House District 43.

The seat's district spans an area in north-central Shelby County covering parts of Helena, Pelham and Indian Springs, as well as a section of the Birmingham area and Hoover in south Jefferson County.

Seats in the state House each have four-year terms. The seat's compensation is based on the state's median annual household income, which was $41,574 in 2012. Legislators can also be reimbursed for documented expenses.
