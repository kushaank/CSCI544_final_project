
An investigation is underway after two government officials were murdered in Mmabatho.  The two officials worked at the Department of Economic Development, Environment, Conservation and Tourism and the Department of Human Settlement.  Three people were left seriously injured during the shooting.

On behalf of the Premier, Kgwele added that security companies are employed at a high cost to the state to ensure that such incidents do not take place. The Premier will be demanding answers and taking drastic actions.
