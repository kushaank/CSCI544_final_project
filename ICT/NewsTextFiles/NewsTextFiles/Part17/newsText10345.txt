
It's the latest in a shake up of Government support for the renewable sector, after plans to scrap the Clean Energy Finance Corporation and a review of the Renewable Energy Target.

Director of West Wind in Gisborne Victoria, Tobi Geiger, says uncertainty is affecting confidence and investment in the renewable sector.

Director if the deregulation unit at the Institute of Public Affairs, Dr Alan Moran, argues the renewable energy sector has had government support for long enough and now needs to compete with other energy sources without assistance.

Kane Thornton of the Clean Energy Council says most Australians support a shift towards clean energy and the Federal Government should play a role in supporting that transition.
