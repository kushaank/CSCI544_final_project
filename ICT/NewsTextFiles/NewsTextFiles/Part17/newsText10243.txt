
YEREVAN, MAY 19, ARMENPRESS. The Government of Armenia plans to increase Armenia’s competitiveness in the region in the IT sector. By the adoption of the package of the draft laws on the Amendments to the law  of the Republic of Armenia  “On State Support of the Information Technology Sector", "On profit tax" and "On income tax", the Government will contribute to the formation of as many as 30-60 new companies a year. This is listed in the program of the new Government of the Republic of Armenia for 2014-2017, the issue of the approval of which is included in the agenda of the special session of the Executive Body on May 19.

As reports “Armenpress”, it is scheduled to install wireless (WiFi) internet access points in the border villages of Armenia, in the result of which in 2015 we will have internet access in more than 340 high mountainous frontier and rural communities.
