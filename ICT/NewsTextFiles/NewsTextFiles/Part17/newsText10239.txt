
The Armenian military ‘broke its ceasefire with Azerbaijan again last night,’ the Azerbaijani Defense Ministry stated on May 19, Ilkin Izzet writes for Trend .

Armenian soldiers opened fire at Azerbaijani Armed Forces’ positions near the Paravakar Village of Armenia’s Ijevan regions, the ministry said.

At the same time, Azerbaijan’s positions came under fire from forces located near the villages of Sarijali, Kengerli, Yusifjanli of  the Aghdam and Goyarkh of Terter region.

Azerbaijani soldiers were also shot at from positions located in the ‘nameless heights’ of the Khojavend and Fizuli regions.
