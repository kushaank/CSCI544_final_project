
STEPANAKERT. – President of the Nagorno-Karabakh Republic Bako Sahakyan received famous writer and scenarist Kim Bakshi.

President Sahakyan expressed gratitude to Kim Bakshi for creating valuable works about Artsakh and Armenia, our nation’s history and culture underlining its importance both for the Armenian people and the outer world.

NKR culture and youth affairs minister Narine Aghabalyan participated in the meeting.
