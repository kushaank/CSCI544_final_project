
YEREVAN, MAY 19, ARMENPRESS. Today, the President of the Republic of Armenia Serzh Sargsyan sent letters of condolence to the President of the Presidency of Bosnia and Herzegovina Bakir Izetbegović and the President of Serbia Tomislav Nikolić on the occasion of devastating floods in Bosnia and Herzegovina and Serbia which caused dozens of human losses and enormous damage giving great enormous ravage.

The Department of Mass Media and Public Relations of   the President’s Staff of the Republic of Armenia informed Armenpress that   Serzh Sargsyan expressed his deepest condolences and sympathies to the   Presidents Bakir Izetbegović and Tomislav Nikolić, families of the dead   people, friendly people of Bosnia and Herzegovina and Serbia. The President   of the Republic of Armenia wished them patience, strength and stamina to   overcome this difficult moment.

Serzh Sargsyan expressed confidence that the measures taken by the authorities of Bosnia and Herzegovina and Serbia will allow these countries to overcome the devastating severe incident.
