
On May 15, 2014 Ambassador Extraordinary and Plenipotentiary of Belarus to Argentina Mr.Victor Kozintsev met with First Deputy Minister of Foreign Affairs of Argentina Mr.Eduardo Antonio Zuain.

During the meeting the parties discussed current issues of Belarus-Argentina cooperation in different spheres. The sides considered the organization of visits exchange at different levels, the development of the legal framework of bilateral relations, promotion of reciprocal travels of the citizens, strengthening contacts between the countries in the scientific, technological and other spheres.
