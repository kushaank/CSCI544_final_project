
JOHANNESBURG - President Jacob Zuma has called on the African Union to bring stability to the continent, adding that he was worried about the activities of armed rebels.

Zuma’s calls came in the wake of recent incidents involving the abduction of 200 girls in Nigeria and the Kenya bomb attacks that left ten people dead last week.
