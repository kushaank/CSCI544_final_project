
The Carl Zeiss Vision Care business group is holding its annual conference for the Eastern Europe region in Malta this week.

Some 30 delegates from 14 countries in the region, including Malta, will attend the conference, now in its seventh edition, on 21 and 22 May, 2014.

Boris Dejonckheere, ZEISS Sales and Marketing Director, will be addressing the conference, which is being held at the Excelsior Hotel, Floriana.

The convention promises to be both stimulating and productive as expected from Carl Zeiss Vision Care, with the main topics covering performance and growth, marketing and new product development.
