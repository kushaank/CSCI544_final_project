The Council for the Development of French in Louisiana has a history of promoting francophone studies that goes back nearly half a century. Here, in 1972, CODOFIL leader Jimmy Domengeaux introduces the first teachers from France and Canada to arrive in Acadiana. Four decades later, French immersion programs have become common in the region.
