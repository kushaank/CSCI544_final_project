
Geneva, May 19 - President Giorgio Napolitano said Monday that the European Union must provide Italy with greater help in coping with a massive wave of migrants arriving from North Africa. "Today we are faced with the absolute need to achieve a concrete, operative model of cooperation with the European Union," Napolitano said.
