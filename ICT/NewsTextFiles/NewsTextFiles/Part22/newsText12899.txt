
MONTREAL – A 28-year-old man is fighting for his life in hospital after being shot on Saint-Hubert street near Mont-Royal avenue.

Residents reportedly heard gunshots around 7:50 p.m. Sunday night.

Witnesses told police they saw a man sitting in his car covered in blood.

Montreal police found the man in his car. He was shot in the upper body at least once.

“We think it may have been related to narcotics,” said Constable Danny Richer.

The man remains in critical condition.

No arrests have been made.
