One woman was killed and another seriously injured in an early morning shooting Sunday near the Northeast Canton Community Center.

An autopsy is planned for Kailee Brown, 30, today, said Harry Campbell, chief investigator of the Stark County Coroner's Office.

Police were called at 2:54 a.m. Sunday to a parking lot at 2600 25th St. NE where someone reported shots had been fired, the news release said.

She was taken by Canton firefighter/paramedics to Mercy Medical Center, where she was pronounced dead at 3:28 a.m., Campbell confirmed.

The news release said that a short time after Brown had been discovered in the vehicle, Alexis Anderson came to Mercy Medical Center with gunshot wounds from the same incident. Anderson, 22, of Detroit, was listed in serious condition when admitted Sunday.

Detectives are asking anyone with information about the shooting to call them at 330-489-3144 or call the city's anonymous 411 tip line.
