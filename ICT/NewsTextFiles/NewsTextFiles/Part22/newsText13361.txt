
"Kyrgyzstan and China entered into cooperation almost on all key aspects," the President of the KR Almazbek Atambayev said during the state visit to China on May 18.

"China is close and trusted neighbor of Kyrgyzstan. The history of our relations goes back to antiquity. We are united not only by common mountains and rivers, but also the harmony of indigenous and national interests. For 22 years since the establishment of diplomatic relations we have successfully developed mutually beneficial partnership, addressed issues of borders, and established full political trust and solid legal and contractual framework are created. Cooperation has been established in almost all the key aspects," the Head of the State said.

Almazbek Atambayev thanked China "for its constant political and moral support, as well as a huge help in the development." "It is not stipulated for some political interests, it is truly disinterested," the President said.

The official meeting with the President of China Xi Jinping was held in state residence in Shanghai. Almazbek Atambayev has arrived in China with a state visit, which will last till May 21. In its framework the President of the Republic will also participate in the IV Summit of the Conference on Interaction and Confidence-building Measures in Asia (CICMA).
