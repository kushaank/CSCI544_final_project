
Shanghai, May 19 / Kabar /. Kyrgyzstan and China signed seven bilateral agreements.

- Joint Declaration of the KR and China to further deepen the strategic partnership. The document was signed by President Almazbek Atambayev and President Xi Jinping.

- Protocol on the exchange of instruments of ratification of the Treaty on extradition of sentenced persons, signed by Minister of Foreign Affairs Erlan Abdyldaev and Minister of Foreign Affairs of China Wang Yi.

- Programme of Cooperation between the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Kyrgyz Republic and the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of China in 2014-2015. The document was also signed between the Ministers of Foreign Affairs of the Kyrgyz Republic and China.

- Agreement on cooperation between the State Drug Control Service of the Kyrgyz Republic and the Ministry of Public Security of China on the issues of cross-border cooperation, signed by Kyrgyz Ambassador to China Kanykei Baktygulova and Deputy Minister of Public Security Liu Yan Pin.

- Memorandum of Understanding between the Ministry of Economy of the Kyrgyz Republic and the Ministry of Commerce of China concerning the project of reconstruction and rehabilitation of Bishkek roads through grant assistance of the Government of China. The document was signed by Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Kyrgyz Republic Erlan Abdyldaev and Minister of Commerce of China Gao Huchen.

- Memorandum on cooperation in the field of inspection and quarantine between the State Inspectorate for veterinary and phytosanitary security under the Government of the KR and the Directorate General of Quality Supervision Inspection and Quarantine of the PRC. The document was signed between Kyrgyz Ambassador to China Kanykei Baktygulova and Deputy Bureau of China Wei Chuan Zhuan.
