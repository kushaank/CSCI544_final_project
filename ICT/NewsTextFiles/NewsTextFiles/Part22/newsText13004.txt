
A Ridgefield High School science research student has won an all expenses paid trip to the Large Hadron Collider facility in Geneva, Switzerland, according to a release from the Connecticut Science and Engineering Fair.

Senior Isabelle Goldstein earned the prize for her research topic, “Partitioning Gamma-Ray Sources in Fermi Large Area Telescope Observations for Spatial and Spectral Analysis,” at the Intel International Science and Engineering Fair in Los Angeles. All five Connecticut winners were women this year.
