
The Airbus A400M new generation airlifter landed today for the first time in Mexico, where the aircraft will show its performance in a series of demonstration flights.

Flown by the experimental test pilot, Ignacio "Nacho" Lombo, the aircraft landed at Mexico DF International Airport at 1:50pm (local time).

"I'm very proud to bring the A400M to Mexico to show how this aircraft is transforming the world of heavy transport. It will be a honour to demonstrate its capabilities to meet the challenges of this country," said Nacho Lombo. The A400M will stay in Mexico until 23 May.
