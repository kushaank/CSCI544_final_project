
BioWare has released a fresh batch of Dragon Age: Inquisition screenshots.

The latest images show off a location called The Western Approach. "Arid, corrupt, and dangerous... and those are its best qualities," BioWare said on Twitter. "Tell us what threats you think lurk in the sand." Also revealed in the screenshots is the Adamant Fortress.

Publisher EA has confirmed a Dragon Age Inquisition release date of October 7 in North America and October 10 in Europe.
