
NEW YORK (MarketWatch) -- Syria's air defense chief, Lt. Gen. Hussein Ishaq, was killed in clashes between rebels and government forces near the capital, the Associated Press reported Sunday. He died Saturday from injuries sustained during the fighting, making him one of just a few top military officials to die in Syria's civil war, the report said.
