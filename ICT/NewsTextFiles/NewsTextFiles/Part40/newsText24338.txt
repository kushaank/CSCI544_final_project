The Salvation Army in Jackson presented Lloyd G. Ganton with the prestigious "Others" Award at its annual Community Celebration at the Cascades Manor House.

Lloyd has worked tirelessly supporting the Jackson Salvation Army as its chair of the Advisory Board for six years. During that time he spearheaded the new building committee. He has been an active committee member of Brad's Lunch Bunch helping to raise the more than $200,000 that has been donated to the Salvation Army Food Bank.

Lloyd is the CEO of Lloyd Ganton Retirement Centers in Jackson, Spring Arbor and Brookyn and he is involved in many other civic and community activities.
