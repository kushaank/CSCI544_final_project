
Wildlife officials say if you spot a coyote while out walking, you should keep your distance.

BOULDER, Colo. - Warning signs have been posted on the University of Colorado's east campus in Boulder after a dog and its owner were attacked by a coyote.

The Boulder Daily Camera newspaper reports the man was walking his dog in a wooded area Thursday evening when the coyote attacked.

The man was bitten on his arm but was able to pull his dog out of harm's way and run the coyote off by waving a stick.
