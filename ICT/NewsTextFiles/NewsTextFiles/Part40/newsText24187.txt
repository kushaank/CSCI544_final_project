
WESTON (WAOW) – A motorcyclist was taken to hospital after hitting a car that turned in front of him Monday, an investigator said.

The crash happened about 8:15 a.m. at the intersection of Camp Phillips Road and Weston Avenue, according to the Everest Metro Police Department.

The motorcyclist, who was not wearing a helmet, suffered non-life-threatening injuries, a lieutenant said.

The female driver of car was northbound on Camp Phillips Roads when she turned in front of the southbound motorcyclist, the lieutenant said.
