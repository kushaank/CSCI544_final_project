
A 19-year-old Juárez woman gave birth to a boy Monday afternoon at city hall in downtown Juárez.

According to officials, María Luicía Guadalupe Velázquez was complaining of having labor pains while she was at the city clerk's office.

City Hall guards alerted Juárez Health Director Hugo Staines Orozco, who was at a meeting, about the woman's condition.

Staines Orozco, a pediatrician, delivered the baby in one of the offices.
