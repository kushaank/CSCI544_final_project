
MOSCOW, May 19./ITAR-TASS/. Russian President Vladimir Putin will on Tuesday discuss with UN Secretary General Ban Ki-moon topical world issues, including the Ukrainian crisis, Kremlin spokesman Dmitry Peskov told ITAR-TASS on Monday.

“Yes, a separate meeting of the president and the UN secretary general is scheduled,” he confirmed, saying the sides are expected “to exchange opinions on all topical world issues, including on the situation in Ukraine”.
