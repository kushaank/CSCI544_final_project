
VARANASI: Uttar Pradesh minister  Satairam Yadav (61), his gunner and driver were killed when a train rammed into their SUV at an unmanned crossing in UP's Jaunpur district on Monday.

"The driver of the SUV failed to notice the Jaunpur-Audihar passenger train at an unmanned railway crossing at Chaukiadham. All three died on the spot," a police office said. Following the mishap, an angry mob protested at the spot of the accident.

The body of Yadav, a senior SP leader enjoying the status of a minister of state, was moved to the district hospital and from there to Police Lines for a guard of honour. It was later cremated with full state honours at Ramghat along the Gomti.
