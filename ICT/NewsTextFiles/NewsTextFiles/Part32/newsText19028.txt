
SAN FRANCISCO, May 19, 2014 /PRNewswire/ -- Digital Realty Trust , Inc. ( DLR ), a leading global provider of data center and colocation solutions, today announced that Matt Miszewski, Digital Realty senior vice president of marketing and sales, will be speaking at the Uptime Institute Symposium taking place May 20-22, 2014 at the Hyatt Regency Santa Clara Convention Center in Santa Clara, California.

The keynote, on Wednesday, May 21, 2014 at 3:30 p.m. in the Mission City Ballroom, is entitled "The Cloud: Where IT and Business Strategies Converge." The session will highlight how the data center will play an increasingly significant role for both cloud service providers and for the enterprise.

In addition, Kevin Dalton, Digital Realty vice president of engineering, will be participating on a panel discussion entitled, "Applying LEED in the Data Center" on Wednesday, May 21 at 11:05 a.m. in Grand Ballroom D.

For the latest updates on Digital Realty at the event, be sure to follow @digitalrealty on Twitter, or join the conversation by using #uptime14 in all event tweets.
