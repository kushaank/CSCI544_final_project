
Deming Mayor Benny Jasso welcomed guests and special guest New Mexico Governor Susana Martinez during Saturday's Deming Motor Vehicles Division Appreciation Day at Voiers Park on North Country Club Road. Seated in center is Taxation and Revenue Department Cabinet Secretary Demesia Padilla. The staff of the Deming Motor Vehicles Division were recognized by the Governor and a small reception was held at the park pavilion. Sponsors for the food and refreshments was the Lou Sisbarro Superstore car deladership in Deming.
