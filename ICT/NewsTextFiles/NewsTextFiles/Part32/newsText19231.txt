
Azerbaijani President Ilham Aliyev on May 17 signed an order on extra measures to speed up social and economic development of the country's Mingachevir city.

The order allocates 3 million manats for the continuation of the redevelopment work in the city.

Under the presidential Order, the funds were allocated to the Mingachevir Executive Authorities from the President`s Reserve Fund for 2014.
