
Manama, May19(BNA)Minister of State for Information Affairs and Government's Official Spokesperson Sameera Ebrahim bin Rajab today received BMI Bank chief executive Jamal Al Hazeem.

The minister commended the financial institutions for their crucial contribution in  support of the national economy.

She affirmed Bahrain's determination to continue providing financial institutions with all support and facilities so as to attract banking investments to Bahrain.
