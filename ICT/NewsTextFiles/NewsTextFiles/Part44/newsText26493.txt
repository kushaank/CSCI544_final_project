
ARBIL/ Aswat al-Iraq: Zummar Peshmarga forces denied the news of preventing Syrian refugees coming to the region in the past few days.

Commander General Hashim Sabti confirmed that the security situation in Zummar area is "very good", where no security problems or incidents were registered.

He pointed out that the areas controlled by the Iraqi army are not the responsibility of the Peshmarga forces.

Charity sites reported that Peshmarga forces prevented the passage of the Syrian refugees in Zummar area.

Zummar, 60 km west of Mosul city, is one of the disputed areas between Baghdad and Arbil, which was covered by Article 140 of the Iraqi constitution.
