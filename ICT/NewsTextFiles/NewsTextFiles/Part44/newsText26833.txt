
The National Bank of Ukraine (NBU) has again prolonged the term for the requirement of the obligatory sale of 50% of currency income, a well-informed source in the sector has told Interfax-Ukraine.

"The obligatory sale of currency income is prolonged until August 20, 2014. The instrument proved its stabilization efficiency," he said.

As reported, the obligatory sale of currency income was introduced in Ukraine in November 2012 and it was prolonged several times.
