
ABBOTABAD: An Anti-Terrorism Court in Abbottabad on Monday sent three suspects accused of gang-raping a college student on a 10-day physical remand, Express News reported.

On May 13, the student was duped by her friend into going with her in a car where two men, including a seminary teacher, had allegedly raped her in Mansehra and then left her stranded in the middle of a road near Ghazikot Township.

The police had later arrested the two alleged rapists along with the driver and the victim’s friend.

Following protests against the incident, police had added section seven of the Anti-Terrorist Act.

According to police, the men were involved in several other cases of molestation and abuse of college girls. They would make videos of the abuse to blackmail the girls later.

One suspect having beared is not “seminary teacher” he is shopkeeper of Mansehra City.
