The Ukrainian Central Elections Commission has registered another 532 official monitors in the May 25 presidential election. In all, 2,191 official election observers have been registered as of yet.The official website of the Central Elections Commission said Ukraine would be visited by observers from 19 international organizations and 18 foreign states.

The commission sent ballot papers to district elections commissions deployed in foreign countries on May 17. In all, 442, 436 ballot papers were delivered to 114 polling stations outside of Ukraine.
