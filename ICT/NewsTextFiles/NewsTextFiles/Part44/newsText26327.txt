
Today at 7:30 AM, the Russian Emergencies Ministry sent another Il-76 aircraft with humanitarian aid for flood-affected people on board to the Republic of Serbia. The aircraft will deliver 38 tons of humanitarian aid and another group of rescuers in Belgrade.

The humanitarian cargo includes 10 pumping units, 1 mobile power station, 30 boats "Solar", 2,500 blankets, canned meat and fish, sugar, baby food and other essentials.
