
Rwanda has in thw works plans to build a $30 million solar power plant in the Kayonza district of eastern Rwanda. The construction of the 10 megawatt plant is part of a deal with Goldsol II, a consortium made up of three companies: Rwanda-based 3E Power Solar, Malta's Gesto Energy Africa and TMM Renewables headquartered in South Africa.
