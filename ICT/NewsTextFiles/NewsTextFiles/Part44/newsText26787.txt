
There is a fight going on in the village of Uspenka, Donetsk region of Ukraine, the press service for the Donetsk People's Republic has reported.

Uspenka is a village in the Amvrosiyevsky district of the Donetsk region. It has a population of some 1,500 people. It is located on the river Krynka, four kilometers from the Russian border.

"Residents of the village of Uspenka are fighting armed people. There is no information on casualties," the press service for the Donetsk People's Republic told Interfax.
