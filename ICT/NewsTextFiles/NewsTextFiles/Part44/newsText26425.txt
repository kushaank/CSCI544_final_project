
Former Prime Minister and JDS chief HD Deve Gowda on Tuesday congratulated PM designate Narendra Modi for his achievement in the Lok Sabha poll and said, he has won a massive mandate.

Modi must show his ability and intelligence to ensure the development of the entire nation to the expectations of the people of the country, Gowda told reporters here. In the run-up to the elections, Gowda had said: “He (Modi) can never reach his numbers. If he achieves it, then I will take political sanyas”, challenging Modi and his team to prove their claims. Gowda’s party secured two Lok Sabha seats including his from Hassan.

“I am a born fighter throughtout my political life…..I have not lost my heart by the results of the Parliamentary elections…,”, he said.

To a question, he said, “I don’t want to describe the present situation of the Congress but regional parties have also shown good results…you can’t rule out the regional parties,” he said.
