
Baku – APA . After the meeting in an expanded format, the signing ceremony of Azerbaijani-Vietnamese documents was held.

“The Agreement on trade, economic, scientific and technological cooperation between the Government of the Republic of Azerbaijan and the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam” was signed by Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Azerbaijan Elmar Mammadyarov and Foreign Minister of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam Pham Binh Minh.

“The Convention on the elimination of double taxation of incomes and property and prevention of tax evasion between the Government of the Republic of Azerbaijan and the Government of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam” was signed by Tax Minister of the Republic of Azerbaijan Fazil Mammadov and Finance Minister of the Socialist Republic of Vietnam Dinh Tien Dung.
