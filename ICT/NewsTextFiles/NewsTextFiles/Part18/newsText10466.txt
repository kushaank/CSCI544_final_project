
A worker has been found dead at the Tropicana gold project on Sunday night.

Macmahon CEO Ross Warren said the company was focussed on providing counselling support to the family and workers on site.

“We are very saddened by the news,” he said.

Macmahon is currently engaged in a 10-year contract for services at Tropicana, which is a joint venture between AngloGold Ashanti and Iendependnce Group.
