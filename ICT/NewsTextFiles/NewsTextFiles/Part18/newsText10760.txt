
There’re a range of important events within the state visit (historic visit) of the President of Azerbaijan to Vietnam.

The due note was made on Twitter by deputy chief of the Presidential Administration of Azerbaijan, head of the foreign relations department Novruz Mammadov.

 “A grand official welcoming ceremony was held in the course of state visit. We visited the Ho Chi Minh Mausoleum.

“President Ilham Aliyev had a touching and memorable meeting with over 300 Vietnamese alumni of Azerbaijani universities”, N.Mammadov said.
