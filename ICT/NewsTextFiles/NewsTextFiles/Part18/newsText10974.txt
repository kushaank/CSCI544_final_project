
Power generator, water pumps, tents and blankets were taken in to the flooded districts. Due to severe showers some areas in Serbia got completely flooded. The roads are blocked, the water supply systems are damaged, local industry is not operational. 16 people were killed and dozens of thousands were evacuated.

Dragomir Karic, the honorary council of the Republic of Belarus in Belgrade: "I would like to thank the Belarusian President Alexander Lukashenko and the people of Belarus for the rendered help. It was provided promptly and efficiently. It proves the fraternal relations between our nations, who can count on each other".

2 MI-8 emergency gunships and 4 crews of rescuers were sent to Serbia at the order of President Alexander Lukashenko.
