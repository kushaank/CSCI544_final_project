
President of the Republic of Azerbaijan Ilham Aliyev has arrived in the Socialist Republic of Vietnam on a state visit.

A guard of honor was arranged for President Ilham Aliyev at Noi Bai international airport in Hanoi.

President of Azerbaijan Ilham Aliyev and his wife Mehriban Aliyeva were met by senior state and government officials of Vietnam.

Then a brief protocol talk took place.
