
BNP Chairperson Khaleda Zia will attend a protest rally organised by party-backed lawyers on May 24.

Jatiyatabadi  Ainjibi Forum, a BNP-backed lawyers’ body, has planned the rally to protest the  murder of fellow lawyer Chandan Kumar Sarkar in Narayanganj and spate of  abductions across the country.

The forum’s secretary general Mahbub Uddin  Khokon said on Monday Khaleda Zia would be present as the chief guest at the  rally on the Supreme Court premises.
