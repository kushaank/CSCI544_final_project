
Baku, Fineko/abc.az.  Azerbaijan’s Ministry of Finances has renewed the license of Gunay Sigorta insurance company.

According to the Ministry, the license was renewed after the company brought its total capital to the minimum level required by the insurance legislation (AZN 5 million).

The license of Gunay Sigorta (№042483), issued on August 14, 2008, was withdrawn by the Ministry of Finances on February 24, 2014 due to non-compliance of the company’s total capital to the minimum requirements.
