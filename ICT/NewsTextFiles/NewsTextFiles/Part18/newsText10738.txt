
The OSCE mission conducted a monitoring on troops contact line in the Tartar-Agdara highway of Azerbaijan`s Tartar region on May 19 in accordance with the mandate of special envoy of the OSCE Chairman-in-Office.

Azerbaijan`s Defense Ministry reported the monitoring has been held on the Azerbaijani side by field assistants of the Personal Representative of the OSCE Chairman-in-Office Jiri Aberle and Peter Svedberg.

From the opposite area, the monitoring has been conducted by field assistants of the Personal Representative of the OSCE Chairman-in-Office Yevgeny Sharov and Hristo Hristov.
