
Manama, May19(BNA)Minister of State for Defence Affairs Lieutenant-General Dr. Shaikh Mohammed bin Abdulla Al-Khalifa today received Kuwaiti Military Judicial Authority President Major General Abdulwahab Al-Salahi, who is leading a delegation on his visit to Bahrain.

The minister welcomed Major-General Al-Salahi, hailing strong bilateral relations bonding the Kingdom of Bahrain and the State of Kuwait. He paid tribute to the leaders of the two countries for bolstering joint cooperation, including military relations.
