
The Azerbaijani Ministry of Emergency Situations delivered humanitarian aid to Serbia for flood relief operations.

Two planes with humanitarian aid were sent to Serbia on May 19. The assistance was rendered upon Azerbaijani President Ilham Aliyev's instructions, department head of the Ministry of Emergency Situations Amal Teymurov told media on May 19.

"Another plane with humanitarian aid will go to Bosnia and Herzegovina this evening," he added.

Dozens of people were killed and thousands were left without houses as a result of the floods in Serbia and Bosnia and Herzegovina.
