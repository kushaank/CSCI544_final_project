Vice President of the European Commission Siim Kallas is to visit Kyiv on May 20 and during his visit a first tranche of EUR 100 million will be provided to Ukraine, a representative of the European Commission Pia Ahrenkilde Hansen has said.

She said at a briefing in Brussels that during the visit Kallas plans to meet Ukrainian Prime Minister Arseniy Yatseniuk and Finance Minister Oleksandr Shlapak."The discussion will focus on the program of macro-financial aid to Ukraine, under which a first tranche of EUR 100 million will be provided," she said.Hansen said that the second tranche of EUR 1.6 billion would be provided when Ukraine ratifies a memorandum of understanding on the program.
