
Chairperson of the Ranaviru Seva Authority Mrs Padma Wettewa said that relatives of the martyred war heroes will participate tomorrow's event. The national war heroes' commemoration month organized jointly by Ministry of Defence and Urban Development and Ranaviru Seva Authority commenced on the 2nd of this month after the first Ranaviru flag was pinned on the President Mahinda Rajapaksa during a brief ceremony at Temple Trees.
