
As many as 43 percent of the total defence budget goes to the Pakistan Army, 22 percent to the Pakistan Navy, whereas 10 percent goes to the Pakistan Air Force (PAF), according to Quddus. “The armed forces send their recommendations to the Defence Ministry, which passes them on to Finance Ministry, in line with preparation of defence budget every year,” he said.

The defence authorities have sought more than Rs 372 billion for the Pakistan Army, Rs 157 billion for the Pakistan Air Force (PAF) and Rs 75 billion for the Pakistan Navy in the upcoming defence budget (operational) compared to Rs 310 billion for the military, Rs 131 billion for the PAF and Rs 63 billion for the Pakistan Navy in the ongoing fiscal year’s defence budget, Daily Times has learnt.
