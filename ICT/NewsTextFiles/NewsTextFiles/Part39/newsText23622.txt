
Post Luxembourg has launched CCP Mobile, a free app for iOS and Android smartphones and tablets that gives P&T bank customers access to their CCP internet accounts when on the go. The app allows customers to check their bank balance, carry out bank transfers, browse recent transactions and top up the 'Easy' prepaid VISA card from Post Finance. CCP Mobile is available in Luxenbourgish, French, German and English.
