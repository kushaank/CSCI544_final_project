
The Sri Lanka army says that the army is ready to face any threat against the country and its national security, State radio reported today.

Army Commander Daya Rathnayaka said the security forces have the revolving strength to face any threat with President Mahinda Rajapakse dedicated to protect the sovereignty and establish the security of the country.

The army chief said that intelligent units of the security forces were strengthened under the guidance of the Defence Secretary and accordingly the security forces could defeat attempts by the LTTE to regroup.
