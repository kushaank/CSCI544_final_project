
YEREVAN, MAY 19, ARMENPRESS. At the National Assembly the four-day session is under way. As reports "Armenpress", 18 issues on the amendments to the draft decision are included in the agenda of the fifth session of the Parliament. In the draft decision of the agenda of the upcoming sittings 117 issues, 20 international agreements are included. The draft decisions on the agenda of the four-day sittings of May 19-22 of the National Assembly will be approved at the May 19 parliamentary session.

At the start of the sitting the issues discussed on May 16, will be put on voting. In the opinion of the Minister of Science and Education of the Republic of Armenia Armen Ashotyan, as a result, the adoption of the package will allow 3-5 thousand children a year to receive free education .
