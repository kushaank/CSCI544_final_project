
YEREVAN, MAY 19, ARMENPRESS. On May 19 the sitting of the NA Standing Committee on Foreign Relations moderated by Artak Zakaryan was held. The Information and Public Relations Department of the National Assembly of the Republic of Armenia informed “Armenpress” that NA deputy Armen Rustamyan withdrew the NA draft statement “On Azerbaijan’s Responsibility of Turning the Nagorno Karabakh Problem into Armed Conflict, Distorting its Essence and Impeding the Peaceful Negotiation Process,“ authored by him in order to revise it.
