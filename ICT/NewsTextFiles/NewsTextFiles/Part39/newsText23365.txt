
New Delhi: Even though Congress President Sonia Gandhi and Vice President Rahul Gandhi offered to resign taking responsibility for the party's debacle in 2014 Lok Sabha elections, in typical Congress style it was outrightly rejected by the leaders. The offer made by Sonia and Rahul to resign was unanimously rejected,' said party leader and Amritsar MP Amarinder Singh.
