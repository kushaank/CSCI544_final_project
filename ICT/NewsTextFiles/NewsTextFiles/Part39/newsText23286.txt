
WASHINGTON, May 19, 2014 /PRNewswire-USNewswire/ -- The 2014 National Town Meeting on Demand Response and Smart Grid is taking place on Monday - Wednesday, May 19-21, in Washington, DC, at the Ronald Reagan Center on 14th St. NW.  It is the 11th annual event.

Close to 400 delegates are expected to attend the non-profit event of the Association for Demand Response and Smart Grid, representing a diverse group of technology companies, utilities, government bodies and organizations.

One highlight of the event is Tuesday, May 20, when the entire day features CEOs, Commissioners, and other notable speakers in facilitated roundtable format discussing topics with each other and the audience.

Another highlight of the Town Meeting is the frequent electronic voting by attendees on various questions. In addition, the "Griddies" - the National Town Meeting Award for Excellence in Communication - will be awarded by attendees to the best examples of creative work that explain the importance of smart energy practices and technologies.

Four State Utility Commissioners from around the country will be receiving leadership awards on the morning of May 20.

A complete agenda and list of speakers is available at www.smartgridtownmeeting.com .
