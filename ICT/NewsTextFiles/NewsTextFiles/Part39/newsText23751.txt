
YEREVAN, MAY 19, ARMENPRESS. The song state ensemble “We Are Our Mountains” of Artsakh has recently come back from a tour in the Russian Federation, which were held by the invitation of the representatives of the local Armenian community.

The Director of the ensemble Slava Apresyan noted in a conversation with Armenpress that they received the invitation by the Armenian community in Samara and the ensemble gave concerts in Talyati, Saratov, Ulyanovsk, Kazan and other cities in Russia in April-May. According to the director, in the result of such invitations the staff leaves for Russia once or twice a year and each time holds 7-8 concerts in different cities.

The Director of the ensemble also mentioned that it is already several times the representatives of the Azerbaijani community have tried to hinder the organization of the concert during the tours, but due to the efforts made by the NKR and local authorities, as well as the interference of the Chairman of the Armenian Union in Russia Ara Abrahamyan all such attempts were failed.
