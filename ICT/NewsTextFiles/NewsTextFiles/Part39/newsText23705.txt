
Sell Your Car for Cash, a cash-for-cars services based in Suffolk County, New York, announces the launch of its new website, which features responsive design and a customer quoting feature to facilitate easy sales for mobile users.

Sell Your Car for Cash is a cash-for-cars firm servicing all of New York state (including both Nassau and Suffolk counties and all five New York City boroughs), as well as Northern New Jersey, Eastern Pennsylvania and Southern Connecticut. The company buys cars of any year, make or model, with any amount of mileage and in any condition, including motorcycles, snowmobiles, boats, and all-terrain vehicles.

The new site also includes improved social media integration and a frequently-updated blog with repair tips, advice on when to sell your car and what factors to consider when doing so.
