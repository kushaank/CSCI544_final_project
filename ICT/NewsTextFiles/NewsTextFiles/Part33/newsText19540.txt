
Pedro Agramunt (Spain, EPP/CD) and Joseph Debono Grech (Malta, SOC), co-rapporteurs of the Parliamentary Assembly of the Council of Europe (PACE) on the honouring of obligations and commitments by Azerbaijan, will be making a fact-finding visit to that country on May 19-21, 2014, PACE office in Baku told Trend on May 19.

In Baku, talks are scheduled with the President of the Republic, the Ministers of Justice and Internal Affairs and the Prosecutor General.

They are also due to meet representatives of the political parties, the President of the Supreme Court and the Azerbaijani delegation to PACE.
