
YEREVAN, MAY 19, ARMENPRESS. The Minister of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Armenia Edward Nalbandian and the Minister of Defense of the Republic of Armenia Seyran Ohanyan will pay a visit to Brussels on May 20. The aim of the visit is to participate in the session of the North Atlantic Council to be held in “28+Armenia” format. The Press, Information, and Public Relations Department of the Ministry of Foreign Affairs of the Republic of Armenia informed “Armenpress” about this.
