
Day Pitney's Women Working Together (WWT) hosted its Annual Leadership Luncheon and Panel Discussion at The Bond Ballroom in Hartford on May 12.

Guest speakers included: Deirdre Daly, United States Attorney for the District of Connecticut; Lonnie Reed, Connecticut State Representative; Robin Smith, vice president & general counsel, LEGO Systems, Inc.; and Pamela Trotman Reid, president of the University of Saint Joseph.

Topics discussed at the event were: Pathways to Leadership, Characteristics of Strong Leaders and Leadership Challenges.

Each year, WWT provides mutual support, professional growth and community service opportunities to women attorneys at the firm and to clients. WWT creates an atmosphere of camaraderie, enthusiasm and laughter for women attorneys that enriches the law firm experience and provides opportunities to meet and network with our clients on a professional, yet personal, level.
