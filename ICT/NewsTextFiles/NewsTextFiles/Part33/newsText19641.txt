Police are investigating a shooting Saturday night near the intersection of Colonial Avenue and West 22nd Street.

WVEC reported that the victim was a valet at Norfolk Chop House restaurant who discovered a man inside a  customer's unlocked car. The man pulled a gun on the valet, who ran and  was shot in the back, the owner of the restaurant told WVEC.

He was taken to the hospital, where he spoke to investigators Sunday morning after being treated for non-life-threatening injuries.

Polce on Monday released surveillance video images of the suspect, described as a black male, between 5 feet 7 inches and 5 feet 10 inches, in his late teens to early 20s. Anyone with information is asked to call the Norfolk Crime Line at 1-888-LOCK-U-UP (1-888-562-5887).
