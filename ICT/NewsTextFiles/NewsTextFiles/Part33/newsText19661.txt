ST. LOUIS COUNTY • Authorities on Monday released surveillance images of a weekend bank robbery in an effort to identify the bandit.

A man entered a First Bank branch at 4090 North Highway 67 about 10 a.m. Saturday, police said. He gave a teller a note demanding money. He indicated he had a weapon but did not show one, according to authorities.

Authorities aren't saying how much the robber left with. No one was injured.

Authorities described the robber as a black man wearing a fedora or fishing-style hat, sunglasses and a dark, multi-color wind breaker. He was described as about 5 feet 9 to 5 feet 10, about 180 pounds and between 35 and 45 years old. Authorities said he had facial hair.

St. Louis County Police asked anyone with information to call 314-889-2341 or CrimeStoppers at 1-866-371-8477.
