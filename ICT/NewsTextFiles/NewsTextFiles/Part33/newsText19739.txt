
Andrew Schaeffer rejoices after receiving his diploma for a Bachelor of Arts in Sociology from Randolph College during the commencement ceremony in Lynchburg, Va. on Sunday.

Randolph College President Bradley Bateman closed out his first year as president of the historic college Sunday morning by bidding adieu to the small but tight-knit class of 2014 in a tradition-filled ceremony on the grassy Dell behind campus.

“Graduates of the class of 2014, you will always be very special to me because you were my first graduating class,” Bateman said Saturday morning after personally handing out 20 master’s and 109 bachelor’s degrees.
