
YEREVAN, MAY 19, ARMENPRESS.  On the Armenian Government's initiative an All-Armenian Investment Fund will be set up, which will be the best example of comparable institutions. This is marked in the program for 2014-2017 of the new Government of the Republic of Armenia, the issue of the approval of which is included in the agenda of the special sitting of the Executive Board convened on May 19. According to the program, the Government in collaboration with the Central Bank will also contribute to the creation of investment banks.

As reports “Armenpress”, to raise the country’s prestige in the field of investment, the Government of Armenia will organize international investment conferences, other events for the international recognition and positive image building for separate regions and sectors, as well as promotion campaigns of the prestigious financial and business information means.

At the regional and economic cooperation platforms, due to more availability for Armenia in the markets of the Customs Union and Common Economic Space, the country's investment attractiveness will be actively introduced, for the potential investors informative and educational events will be held.
