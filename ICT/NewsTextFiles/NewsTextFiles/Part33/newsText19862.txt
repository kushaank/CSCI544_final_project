
Imphal, May 19 2014 : As part of its measures to reform units and branches, All Manipur Students' Union (AMSU) reconstituted AMSU Kakching Khunou Branch during a meeting held at the union's head office on May 16 .

The meeting also elected Thingam Ramesh Singh as president, Angom Angousana Meitei as vice-president and Angom Jotin Singh as general secretary of the newly constituted branch.
