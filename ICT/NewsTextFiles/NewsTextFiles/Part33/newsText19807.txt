
KINGSTON, Jamaica -- A 27-year-old woman is dead after a knife was brought into play during an altercation between her and another woman at Brooks Avenue, Kingston 20, Sunday evening,

The dead woman is Kimberly King, of a Brooks Avenue address in Duhaney Park. The woman accused of inflicted the deadly wounds has not yet been apprehended by the police.

Police report that about 6:45 pm, King and the other woman was involved in an altercation when a knife was used to inflict multiple stab wounds to King. The police were alerted and King was assisted to hospital where she was pronounced dead.

The police say they are now probing the circumstances surrounding the killing and are appealing to anyone with information to contact the Duhaney Park Police at 933-4280, police 119 emergency number or the nearest police station.
