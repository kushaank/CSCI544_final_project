
New Delhi: YSR Congress chief Jagan Mohan Reddy will meet Prime Minister designate Narendra Modi in Delhi on Monday, a day ahead of the crucial BJP Parliamentary Party meet.

Jagan is expected to meet Modi along with his Members of Parliament. He is also likely to meet other senior BJP leaders. The meeting is crucial as Jagan's party has won six Lok Sabha seats in Andhra Pradesh.

Jagan had in the past said that he is willing to do business with Modi, however, there had been no alliance between the party pre-election.
