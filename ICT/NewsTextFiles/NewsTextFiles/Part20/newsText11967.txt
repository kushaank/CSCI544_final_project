
Traders of Jammu and Kashmir and Pakistan-occupied Kashmir (PoK) will meet here tomorrow to discuss problems faced by them during cross-LoC commercial activities.

The traders will meet at Chakan-Da-Bagh crossing point on the LoC in Poonch to discuss problems confronting them, officials said.

Some 35-40 traders from Jammu and Kashmir, led by Jammu Chamber of Commerce president Y B Sharma, will attend the meeting, they said.

Government officials from the two sides would also participate in the meeting.
