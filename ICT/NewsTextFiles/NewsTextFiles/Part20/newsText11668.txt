
BANGALORE: The Bangalore Water Supply and Sewerage Board (BWSSB) will supply water through tankers to households facing water shortage. A tanker of Cauvery water will cost `360.

According to a press release, the service will be started on a trial basis from May 26 for a month to make use of 30 water tankers that the board has acquired recently.

“To avail the service, customers have to call BWSSB sub-division office in their area. They have to give their address and RR Number. Within hours, the water tanker will reach their house and money has to be paid to the driver,” a BWSSB official said.
