
Commissioner of Greater Visakhapatnam Municipal Corporation (GVMC) M.V. Satyaranarayana inspected various wings at the GVMC Main Office on Monday. He inspected the work being done by the various wings like Town Planning, Public Health, Finance, Accounts, Audit, IT, UCD, ‘Saukaryam’, Registration of Births and Deaths, Printing Press and the Call Centre.

On coming to know that some employees in the Town Planning Wing were working in the same wing for the past eight years, he directed the officials concerned to transfer them to other wings.

Additional Commissioner S.S. Varma accompanied the Commissioner during the inspection.
