
The US Department of Defense announced on Monday the death of a US soldier who was supporting Operation Enduring Freedom.

Spec. Adrian M. Perkins, 19, of Pine Valley, California, died on May 17 in Jordan's capital Amman from a non-combat related injury, the Pentagon said.

He was assigned to 1st Battalion, 67th Armor Regiment, 2nd Brigade Combat Team, 4th Infantry Division, Fort Carson, Colorado.
