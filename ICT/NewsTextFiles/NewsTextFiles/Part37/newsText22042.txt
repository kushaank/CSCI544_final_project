
The Department of Defense announced Monday (5/19/14) the death of a Fort Carson soldier who was supporting Operation Enduring Freedom.

Spc. Adrian M. Perkins, 19, died May 17 in Amman, Jordan from a non-combat related injury.

Perkins was from Pine Valley, California. He was assigned to Fort Carson's 1st Battalion, 67th Armor Regiment, 2nd Brigade Combat Team, 4th Infantry Division.
