
Manama, May19(BNA) Industry and Commerce Minister Dr. Hassan Fakhro today received  MP Adel Al Maawda and discussed key economic issues and investments which benefit the country and citizens.

Dr. Fakhro  stressed the government and ministry's efforts to strengthen the economic sector. MP Al-Maawda commended the efforts undertaken by the Industry and Commerce Minister Dr. Hassan Fakhro  to bolster  the national economy.
