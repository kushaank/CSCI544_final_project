
Talks continued Sunday night between hospital officials and the pharmacists union, which represents 1,430 pharmacists working for Kaiser facilities in Southern California, the Los Angeles Daily News reported.

Monday, Kaiser Permanente announced that all of its Southern California pharmacies in would remain open during normal operating hours.

If a strike should occur, patients would be advised to order prescriptions online or visit local drugstores, several of which have arranged deals with Kaiser.
