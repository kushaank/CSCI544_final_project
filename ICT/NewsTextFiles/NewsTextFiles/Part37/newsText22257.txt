
Lawyers make their case.  Former Gov. Bob McDonnell and his wife, Maureen, were back in federal court this morning for what is considered a key hearing in their corruption case. The couple's lawyers want the judge to dismiss the 11 charges against each of the McDonnells and to separate their trials. The judge says he'll rule tomorrow.

Riverrock draws record crowd.  More than 100,000 people attended the annual sports and lifestyle fest on the James River over the weekend, organizers say. Find photo galleries, videos and complete coverage  here .

New England Patriots may invade Richmond. The Washington Redskins and New England Patriots are in talks to hold joint practices at training camp this year before a preseason game between the teams. Most likely the joint practices would be held Aug. 4-5, although no agreement has been reached.
