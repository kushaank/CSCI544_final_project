
ALLAHABAD: Around 80% lawyers exercised their right to vote in  Allahabad High Court Bar Association ( HCBA) election on Monday.

As many as 6,106 lawyers cast their votes out of 7,665 voters. The polling passed off peacefully amidst tight security arrangements. It began at 9 am and continued till 5 pm.

A large number of candidates and their supporters had set up camps on GT Road adjacent to the high court playground. The large number of candidates and their supporters were seen distributing pamphlets.

According to returning officers, as many as 280 booths were set up. The counting will begin at 12 noon on Tuesday.
