
FORT CARSON, Colo. (AP) – A Fort Carson soldier from California has died while serving in Jordan.

The Department of Defense says that 19-year-old Spec. Adrian M. Perkins of Pine Valley died Saturday in Amman of a non-combat related injury.

He served with the 2nd Brigade Combat Team in the 4th Infantry Division at Fort Carson near Colorado Springs.
