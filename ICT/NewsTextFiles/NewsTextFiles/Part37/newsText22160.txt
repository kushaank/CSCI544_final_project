
Zain Kuwait has awarded a contract to Japan's NEC and local IT integrator, Metco for upgrades to its mobile network backhaul systems.

Metco will useNEC's iPasolink microwave system to upgrade Zain Kuwait's backhaul transmission network to meet growing mobile broadband demand. The  project is expected to be completed by the end of 2014.

Metco, which has a strategic relationship with Zain for more than 25 years  ago, also indicated that the new Zain Kuwait microwave network will be one of  the most extensive in the world.
