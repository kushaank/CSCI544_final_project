
KINGSTON, Jamaica -- Opposition Spokesman on Finance, Audley Shaw, is slated to address a symposium by Institute of Internal Auditors (IIA) Jamaica Chapter today, Monday, May 19.

The symposium, presented by the IIA in association with the Institute of Chartered Accountants of Jamaica, Financial Services Commission (FSC), UHY Dawgen Chartered Accountants, and ACCA Caribbean, under the theme: ‘The Pulse of Our Profession’, is scheduled for 5:30 pm at the Knutsford Court Hotel in Kingston.

Panellists in the symposium include IIA President, David Hall; Deputy Commissioner, Government of Jamaica, Audit Commission, Collin Greenland; Head of ACCA Caribbean, Brenda Lee; Vice President & Chief Auditor, Bank of Nova Scotia, Jim Brown; and Janet Johnson-Haughton, Senior Director, FSC.
