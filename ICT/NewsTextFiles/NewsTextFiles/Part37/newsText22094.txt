
A new book delves into the four sons of Fred Koch and the influence their oil industry leader and anti-communist father had on them.

Vanity Fair reports "Sons of Wichita: The Saga of the Koch Brothers" examines the lives not only of Koch Industries Inc. leaders Charles and David Koch , but also their less politically involved brothers, Bill and Freddie. It's written by Daniel Schulman , a senior editor at Mother Jones magazine.

The book is set to be released on Tuesday.
