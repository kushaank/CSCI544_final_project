
KARACHI: Four people were killed on Monday in three separate incidents of firing in various parts of the metropolis.

According to police, two people were shot dead by unidentified assailants in Mithadar area. They were identified as Shaukat Hussain Shirazi (30) and Qaisar Hussain (35).

In another incident, the owner of a marble factory was kiled when unidentified armed men fired at his car in Mianwali Colony of Manghopir. He was identified as Farhan Afzal Khan (30).

Another person, identified as Mohsin Ali (40), was gunned down in Dakkhana area of Liaquatabad.

The police have started investigation into these incidents.
