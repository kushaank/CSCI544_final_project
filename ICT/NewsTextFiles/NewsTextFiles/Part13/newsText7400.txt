
On Saturday, May 17, 2014 at 9:55 p.m. Officers responded to the 6400 block of Panama Lane concerning an injury collision involving a pedestrian.

Investigation revealed Maninder Singh 24, of Bakersfield was crossing southbound, outside a marked crosswalk, when he was struck by a gray Lexus, which was being driven eastbound on Panama Lane by Ruben Robles 46, also of Bakersfield.

Singh sustained moderate injuries as a result of the collision and was transported to a local hospital for treatment.

Robles and his adult female passenger were not injured during the collision and were released at the scene.

Alcohol does not appear to be a factor in this collision.

Anyone with information regarding this collision is encouraged to call the Bakersfield Police Department at (661) 327-7111.
