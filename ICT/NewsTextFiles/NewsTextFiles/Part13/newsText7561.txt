
A cleric, along with his accomplices, allegedly raped a 15-year-old girl on Monday in Noorpur area within the limits of Kasur’s Saddar police station.

According to police, the girl was taken to the cleric for an ‘exorcism’ by her parents as they believed she was possessed by a ghost.

The cleric asked the girl’s parents to leave her alone with him for the exorcism and then raped her along with his accomplices.

The victim was shifted to hospital while the cleric has been arrested.
