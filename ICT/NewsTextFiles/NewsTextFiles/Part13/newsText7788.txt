
Thailand will be one of the lucky countries to witness B.A.P‘s world tour, B.A.P Live on Earth 2014.

Details for the Bangkok Attack was released today. They will be performing  in the country on June 28 at the Royal Paragon Hall 2-3. Previously, the group had successful concerts in South Korea, Europe, United States of America, Australia and Taiwan. With their large following in Thailand, the show is expected to draw thousands of fans.

Meanwhile, there will also be upcoming performances in Singapore and Japan.
