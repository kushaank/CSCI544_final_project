
The first staff meeting of the “Poppy-2014” operation was held today, 19 May, in Tajikistan’s Drug Control Agency.

According to the Drug-Control Agency, the meeting was chaired by the First Deputy Head of the Agency, the Head of Operations, Major-General Vaysiddin Azamatov.

Organizational issues on conducting “Poppy-2014” were  reviewed, as well as collaboration between ministries and other relevant structures.

According to him, Ministry of Interior, National Security Committee, and Customs Services representatives participated in the meeting.
