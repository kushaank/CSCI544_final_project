
Placing the Saudi growth story in the context of GCC growth, Mahendran said there were several reasons to remain invested in GCC equities. These included improving economic and business indicators across the region, sustainable earnings growth and positive repercussions across the region from the MSCI inclusion of Qatar and the UAE. He added that the improving volumes, liquidity and foreign ownership limits, were expected to generate a spate of IPOs to provide greater depth and breadth to the GCC markets.
