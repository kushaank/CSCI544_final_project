
HEBRON, May19, 2014, (WAFA) – Israeli forces Monday arrested four Palestinians, including two teenagers, from the West Bank cities of Hebron and Bethlehem, as well as summoned another for interrogation, according to security sources.

Sources said that forces arrested two Palestinians from the Hebron area.

Forces also raided several   neighborhoods   in the town of Dura to the south of Hebron and set up several checkpoints at the entrances of Hebron city.

Meanwhile, forces arrested two teenagers, aged 17 and 18 from Aida refugee camp, north of Bethlehem, after breaking into and searching their families’ houses.

Forces also handed a Palestinian from the camp summon to appear before the Israeli intelligence.
