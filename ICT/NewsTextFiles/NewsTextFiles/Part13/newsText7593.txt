Indian food and culture till take over the Romanian Village Museum in Bucharest between May 23 and 25 at the Namaste India, the Indian Culture Festival. The festival will include events organized daily, from 10 to 17,00 or 18,00.

The start of the festival will be at 12 on Friday, May 23. The three-day event will include film screenings, reading sessions, concerts and dance sessions, discussions, crafting sessions, language lessons, yoga sessions, as well as workshops on cooking Indian food.

Entry tickets will be RON 30 for the whole festival, and RON 10 a day.
