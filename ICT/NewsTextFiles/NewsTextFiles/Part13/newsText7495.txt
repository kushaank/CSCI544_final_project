
New Delhi : BJP leader Sushma Swaraj Monday met India's to-be prime minister Narendra Modi in Gujarat Bhavan here.

The meeting is to take further the discussions on government formation.

Bharatiya Janata Party president Rajnath Singh too is slated to meet several party leaders.
