
COTABATO CITY, Philippines --- Two men riding a motorcycle together shot dead Lanao del Sur assistant provincial prosecutor Simpal Alawi Sr. in Marawi City on Monday.

The victim, who was also a part-time instructor at the law school of the Mindanao State University in Marawi City, was on his way to office on board his vehicle when the suspects overtook the victim's vehicle and opened fire with .45 caliber handguns.

Alawi died instantly from gunshot wounds.
