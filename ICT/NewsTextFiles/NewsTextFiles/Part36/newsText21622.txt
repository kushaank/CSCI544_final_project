
Euromedic Ireland has announced the opening of a new 1.5m medical scanning clinic in Dublin city centre.

Over 100 people will be employed at the 30,000 sq ft Meath Primary Care when the centre is fully operational in July 2014.  It will also house 10 GPs, physiotherapy, occupational therapy, dental clinic, audiology, wound clinic, pharmacy, and an extensive consultant suite that will include orthopaedic andophthalmology services.
