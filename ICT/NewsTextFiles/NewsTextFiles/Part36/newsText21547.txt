
Ahmedabad: The BJP Legislature Party in Gujarat will meet here on  May 21 to elect its new leader to replace Chief Minister Narendra Modi,  who is all set to be the country's next Prime Minister.

Modi became the chief minister for the first time on October 7, 2001 and won three consecutive terms. He is also scheduled to address a rally on May 20 in Maninagar, his Assembly constituency.
