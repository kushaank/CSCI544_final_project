
LINCOLNWOOD, Ill., May 19, 2014 /PRNewswire-iReach/ -- The newest addition to the Porsche model lineup — the 2015 Porsche Macan — is set to arrive at Chicagoland luxury dealership Loeber Motors in the coming days. While the dealership isn't promoting the crossover's arrival with live tigers like locations in other parts of the country, Loeber Motors is offering model comparisons and various financial tools to help its customers know what to expect from the all-new Porsche Macan.

The 2015 Porsche Macan will be available in a base Macan S configuration and a few performance-focused Turbo trims. In its base offering, the 2015 Porsche Macan is fitted with a 3.0-liter V-6 engine rated for 340 horsepower and 339 pound-feet of torque. Mated to a seven-speed automated manual transmission, the all-new crossover is capable of accelerating from 0-60 miles per hour in just over five seconds, according to Porsche.

The performance-heavy Macan Turbo will host a 3.6-liter V-6 that generates 400 horsepower and 406 pound-feet of torque. A seven-speed transmission sends power to all four wheels and Porsche boasts a 0-60 time of 4.6 seconds.

Expected to compete for the top spot in the performance compact crossover segment, the 2015 Porsche Macan's highly anticipated arrival at Loeber Motors is one of several exciting new happenings at the dealership.

The dealership also is hosting a Mercedes-Benz Open Haus event Saturday, May 17, which overlaps with a Mercedes-Benz Certified Pre-Owned event. With outstanding limited-time savings available on the lot, luxury car shoppers can trade their existing models for above Black Book value and get in a new model for a great value.
