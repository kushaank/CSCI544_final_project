WATERVILLE — The First Choice Pregnancy Center will host a  two-mile-walk at 9 a.m. Saturday, May 31, starting at Head of Falls. Families, friends, youth and co-workers can participate together and be a visible voice in our community that states: “Together For Life”.

Registration begins at 8:30 a.m. Complimentary food, music, and prizes, food and music, will follow the walk, from 10:30 a.m.-2 p.m.; weather permitting. The walk portion will continue rain or shine.
