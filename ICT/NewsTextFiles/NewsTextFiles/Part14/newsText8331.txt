Valedictorian Donald Lee Knapp, son of Donald and Patricia Knapp, and salutatorian Savanah Michelle McLendon, daughter of Ralph and Sheila McLendon, spoke at the ceremony, along with Top 10 Senior Paige Spivey.

Knapp and McLendon both plan to major in chemistry at the University of South Alabama.

The class officers were Ian Hughes, president; Hannah Turner, vice president; Summer O'Field, secretary; and Kimberly Fredrick, treasurer.

The class of 2014 is donating a digital messaging system to the school to provide announcements, updates and status of school events.

The graduates have earned more than $6 million in scholarships.

The Mobile County school system is streaming graduation ceremonies online at  mcpss.tv  and on the air with the school system's  new 24-hour TV channel,  available on Comcast channel 15 and on AT&T U-verse On Demand channel 99.
