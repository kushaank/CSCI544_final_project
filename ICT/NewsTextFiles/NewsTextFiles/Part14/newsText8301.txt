
Wisconsin Pork Association, in conjunction with the National Pork Board, will host the We Care trailer at two upcoming events in Wisconsin.

May 31: Green County Dairy Breakfast, Monticello. Breakfast will be served from 6 to 10 a.m. Volunteers are needed from 5:30 to 11 a.m. Volunteers will receive paid mileage and tickets to the breakfast.

June 14: Beer, Bacon and Cheese Festival, New Glarus. The event runs from 1 to 5 p.m. Volunteers are needed from 11 a.m. to 5:30 p.m. Volunteers will receive admission bracelets for samples from the various vendors, as well as mileage.
