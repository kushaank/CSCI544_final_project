
MADISON COUNTY, Ala. - A Madison County Sheriff's Office employee is on leave without pay after his arrest Sunday evening for domestic violence.

Madison County Sheriff Blake Dorning said Christopher Anthony Causey, 34, of Hazel Green, was arrested while he was at work in the Madison County Metro Jail. He is charged with third-degree domestic violence, which is a misdemeanor.

Dorning said the charge comes from an alleged altercation with his spouse.

Causey is set to be released on a $300 bond after a mandatory 12-hour holding period in the jail.
