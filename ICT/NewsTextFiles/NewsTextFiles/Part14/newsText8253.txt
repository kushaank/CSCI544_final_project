TUSCALOOSA, Alabama -- The Tuscaloosa chapter of BamaCarry , the state's largest group of gun rights activists, will host a political candidate's forum in Northport Tuesday evening.

The public forum, which will be held at the Northport Civic Center on McFarland Boulevard, is scheduled to run from 6:30 to 8 p.m. Tuesday.
