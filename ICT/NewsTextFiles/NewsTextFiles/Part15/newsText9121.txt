GRANDVILLE, MI -- A 27-year-old man is accused of hiding in a gym's ceiling and peeping on a woman as she used a tanning bed.

Grandville Police were called to Planet Fitness, 3845 Rivertown Parkway, on May 12 on a man reportedly watching through ceiling tiles as a female was tanning.

Cody Ryan Postmus, 27, was arrested Saturday, May 17, and arraigned Monday on a count of surveilling an unclothed person, which is a two-year felony.

Bond was set at 10 percent of $25,000, according to jail records. Postmus is currently in custody awaiting a probable cause hearing scheduled for June 2.

State Police records show Postmus in 2009 was convicted of misdemeanor obscene disorderly conduct. He pleaded guilty to the count in Ottawa County, records show.
