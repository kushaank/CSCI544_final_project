
The Never Forgotten Honor Flight took off earlier this morning, once again transporting a group of Northwoods veteran’s to our nation’s capital.

On the flight leaving today, 88 veteran’s will participate. That includes 8 from World War Two, 79 from Korea and 1 from Vietnam. This brings the overall total of participants to 1,500 since the first flight in 2010.

Today’s trip left at 7 this morning and is scheduled to return to Central Wisconsin Airport at 10pm. Two additional trips have been scheduled for September and October.

North Central Wisconsin’s Never Forgotten Honor Flight is one of six hubs based in Wisconsin and 77 overall in the United States.
