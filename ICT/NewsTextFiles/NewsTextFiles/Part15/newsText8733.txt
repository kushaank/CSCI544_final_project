
LOUISVILLE, Ky. (WDRB) -- A woman charged with abusing and neglecting 20 poodles in a disgusting Paoli, Ind. home is in custody and will be heading back to Indiana.

Laura King faced a judge this morning in Louisville where she is a veterinary technician.

The dogs were found covered in feces and maggots in an abandoned house owned by King. She's charged with 20 counts of animal abuse and neglect.

The rescued poodles are in the care of the Orange County Humane Society.
