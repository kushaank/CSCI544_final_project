
The Supreme Court Monday granted three weeks' interim bail to Tehelka founder-editor Tarun Tejpal so that he could perform last rites of his mother, who passed away in Goa Sunday.

The apex court bench of Justice BS Chauhan and Justice AK Sikri  passed the orders granting Tejpal interim bail after holding the hearing on his  plea in their chamber.
