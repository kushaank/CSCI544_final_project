
PLOVER, Wis. (AP) -- Police have released more details about the fatal shooting at the home of an off-duty Portage County sheriff's deputy over the weekend.

Plover police say Tony Gischia returned home with another person early Saturday when they were confronted by a masked intruder. They say the intruder was shot and killed during a scuffle.

A police statement released Monday doesn't say who fired the shot. Police referred questions to the Wisconsin Department of Justice, where a spokeswoman says the investigation is ongoing and she can't immediately comment further.

The alleged intruder was identified as 30-year-old Charles Jameson of Stevens Point. Police say Gischia and the other individual both knew Jameson.
