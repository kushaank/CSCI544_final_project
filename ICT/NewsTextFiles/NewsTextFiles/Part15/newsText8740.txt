
The sixth annual Outdoor Youth Expo will be held from 10 a.m.-2:30 p.m. on June 7 at the Lakeshore Conservation Club, 9911 Cass County Road 77 across the road from Mount Ski Gull.

The event is free to the public and includes duck and goose calling, blue bird and wood duck house building, trap shooting, archery, fishing stations, .22 and black powder shooting, a BB gun gallery, DNR and youth programs and fire extinguisher training.
