CHEEKTOWAGA, NY - Westchester County Executive Rob Astorino, the Republican who wants the top job in Albany, paid a visit to Western New York Saturday.

Astorino was at the Private Leonard VFW Post in Cheektowaga for an Erie County Republican Committee-sponsored roundtable discussion as part of his campaign for governor.

We asked if he's facing an uphill battle here, considering Erie County's GOP chair wanted to draft Donald Trump for the gubernatorial race.
