
PLEASANT HILL (CBS SF) – A Pleasant Hill man reported missing Friday has been located in Nevada in good health.

Police put out a missing person notice for Jason Adams , 31, after he was last seen at Friday at work and never returned home from work.

Around 2 a.m., officers in Reno, Nevada spotted Adams’ vehicle in a parking lot.

They made contact with Adams and determined he was in good health, according to Pleasant Hill police.
