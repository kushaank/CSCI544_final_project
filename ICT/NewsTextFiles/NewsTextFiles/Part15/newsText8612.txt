
There are new fears that a potential deadly virus is spreading here in they United States.  Doctors are now very concerned by the way this latest case was most likely transported and spread.  MERS stands for Middle Eastern Respiratory Syndrome.  There have been three cases in the US.  The first two were infected in Saudi Arabia and then traveled to the United States.  The latest case was reported in Illinois and doctors say it was likely spread with just a handshake.  In the latest case, authorities say the person infected had two business meetings with a person who recently returned from Saudi Arabia .  The Center for Disease Control says the only physical contract between the two was a handshake.
