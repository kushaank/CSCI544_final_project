
STORM LAKE, Iowa | Three men were arrested following an assault that occurred in a residence at 1026 Lake Avenue around 3 a.m. Monday.

One man attacked the resident of the home during an argument, then left with two friends, police said.

Braeden Bailey, 19, Rembrandt, Iowa; Bidong Yak, 19, Storm Lake; and Dante Putnam, 19, Storm Lake, were booked into Buena Vista County Jail on simple misdemeanor charges.

Police also learned that Putnam was wanted out of O'Brien County, Iowa, on an assault causing bodily harm serious misdemeanor charge.
