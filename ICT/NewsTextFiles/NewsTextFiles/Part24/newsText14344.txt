
A man has been arrested on suspicion of murdering a woman who was found at a house in Barrow, Cumbria, on Sunday.

A spokeswoman for Cumbria Constabulary said: "At 9.42am today the North West Ambulance Service notified the police of a sudden death of a woman, in her 20s, who had been found in a property on Napier Street, Barrow.

"The death is being treated as unexplained and the circumstances are under investigation. Police have arrested a man, aged 68, on suspicion of murder and he is assisting detectives with their inquiries."
