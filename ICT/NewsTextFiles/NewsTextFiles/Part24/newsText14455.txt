
CAMPBELLSVILLE, Ky. (WKYT) - The Campbellsville Police Department is searching for Dustin Carter, 23, who they believe was involved in a hit and run.

Carter was last seen wearing a white t-shirt with blue jeans.

Police say he was involved in a hit and run accident, striking two pedestrians.

It all happened on South Central Avenue in Campbellsville on Monday.

Anyone who has seen Carter is asked to call Campbellsville Police at (270) 465-4121.
