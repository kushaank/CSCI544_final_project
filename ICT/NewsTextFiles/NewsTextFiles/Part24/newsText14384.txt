
HOUSTON (May 19, 2014) Police in Houston were looking for three suspects Monday after two men were killed at a home during a robbery.

The victims were identified Monday as Tyreal Breaux, 26, and Horace Jackson, 60.

They were both shot to death.

Officers found them dead on Sunday after responding to reports of gunfire.

Investigators initially said some cash was stolen and drugs were confiscated from the residence.

Police did not immediately release further details on the three men being sought or what was taken.
