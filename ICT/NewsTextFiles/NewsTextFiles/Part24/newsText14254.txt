
A 25-year-old man has died after the tractor he was driving overturned and crushed him.

Police, fire and ambulance services were called to the scene of the overturned tractor near Houghton Farm at Northmostown near Sidmouth at around 12:50pm today.

The man from the Sidmouth area was declared dead at the scene. His next-of-kin have been informed.

The nine tonne tractor had been towing a trailer of a similar weight when it overturned after leaving a field in the area.

Police officers, including forensic collision investigators, are still at the scene of the tragedy and Pinn Lane remains closed at this time.

Police are appealing for witnesses of the incident to contact them on 101 and quote log number 330 of the 19/5/14.
