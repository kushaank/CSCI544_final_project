
PHIDIM: Locals constructed a new police post building and handed it over to the police post at Jorpokhari VDC in Panchthar on Monday. Villagers of Jorpokhari bazaar area and its surroundings donated 15 ropani land to the police post for the building. Jorpokhari Police Post was operating in a rented house for the past 25 years. The villagers had formed a committee that raised more that five lakh rupees for the building. A new three-room building was constructed in three years. The rooms have been used as office, residence, toilet-cum-bathroom. Kamal Tolangi in-charge of the police post said they had the opportunity to be in touch with locals.
