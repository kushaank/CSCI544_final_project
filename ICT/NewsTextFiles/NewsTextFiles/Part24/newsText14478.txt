
Islamabad : A girl who married 6 months back on her own will was shot dead along with her father-in-law in Jhangi Sydan area. Shahzad town police said accused Shahid, maternal uncle of the girl killed Nadia and her father-in-law and escaped from the scene.

Deceased Nadia had married to Waseem 6 months back on her own will. Police have registered a case against the accused and started further investigation.
