
A 29-year-old Hamilton man wanted in connection with an alleged abduction and sexual assault of a child is in police custody.

John Adam Greene turned himself in on Monday and was arrested by Hamilton Police.

Greene is facing several charges including abduction, aggravated sexual assault, sexual interference and threatening death.

Hamilton police reached out to the public over the weekend to assist in the search.

Police said Greene was considered to be dangerous and warned he may be armed.
