
Phoenix police said a security guard was being interviewed after a shooting at a Phoenix bar in which one person was wounded early Monday morning.

Phoenix police said the shooting happened about 2:30 a.m. at The Funny Farm Cocktails, 4217 W. Thomas Rd.

Police detained the security guard and were interviewing witnesses. The victim was taken to a hospital and was expected to survive, said Phoenix Officer James Holmes.

The investigation was continuing.
