
POLICE have released CCTV footage of cars being damaged in a Darlington street.

Officers are keen to speak to the man captured in the images after wing mirrors were vandalised on 12 cars parked in Hollyhurst Road, near Darlington Memorial Hospital.

The incident happened in the early hours of Friday, April 25.

The suspect is described as being in his late teens, wearing a Berghaus-style jacket.

He had fair hair and was wearing a blue coat.

Anyone with information is asked to call Acting Sergeant Catherine Iley, on 101.
