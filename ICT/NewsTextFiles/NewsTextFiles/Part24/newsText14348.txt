ST. LOUIS • The Major Case Squad of St. Louis has been activated to investigate a shooting death in Madison that launched a police chase from Illinois into St. Louis on Monday afternoon.

Police said the incident started just before 2 p.m. Madison police received several 911 calls saying that shots had been fired and a man was down at Washington Avenue and Third Street in Madison.

Police arrived and found Kenneth C. Deal, 43, of the 600 block of Meredocia Avenue in Venice, lying in the street.  He was taken to a hospital and pronounced dead shortly afterwards.

Witnesses pointed out a suspect vehicle to police.  Madison police attempted to stop the vehicle, which refused to stop.

Police gave chase through Madison, Brooklyn and Venice and headed across the Stan Musial Veterans Memorial Bridge into St. Louis. The vehicle eluded police in St. Louis.

During the chase through Madison and Brooklyn, somebody in the vehicle shot at Madison and Brooklyn squad cars, but they were not hit.

Police searcged neighborhoods around Hyde Park by car and helicopter Monday afternoon.

Anyone has information about this incident is asked to call the Major Case Squad at 1-618-876-7182.
