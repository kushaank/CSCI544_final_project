
Police have appealed for witnesses to a road rage incident on the A96 near Inverness.

The driver of a blue Vauxhall Astra was assaulted by a man who was about 19 years old and 5ft 10in tall.

He was believed to have been driving a grey Peugeot 205 and part of its registration was N204, or similar.

The attack happened at about 10:00 on Saturday close to the Inverness Airport roundabout on the A96. Police said the road was busy at the time.
