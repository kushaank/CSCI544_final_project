
Officer David Howley, of the Oldham County Police Department, died of a heart attack hours after returning home from work on May 14.

His death is classified as "line of duty" under the provisions of the Hometown Heroes Act. Under this act, if a person suffers a heart attack or stroke (that later proves fatal) within 24-hours of stressful or strenuous duty, it is presumed to have occurred in the line of duty.
