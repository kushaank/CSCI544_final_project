
Rio Rancho police are asking for the public’s assistance in locating a missing woman.

Cynthia Mofley, 67, was last seen Sunday morning in the area of Enchanted Hills and Sprint Blvd. driving a red Hyundai Elantra with a New Mexico license plate of MBZ-004.

Mofley is 5’7”, weighs 170 lbs and has mid-length red and grey hair and brown eyes.

Mofley is on dialysis and needs regular medication.

If you know of Cynthia Mofley’s whereabouts, you are asked to contact the Rio Rancho Police Department at 505-891-7226.
