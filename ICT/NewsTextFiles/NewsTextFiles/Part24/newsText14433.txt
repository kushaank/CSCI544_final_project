
TUCSON (AP) — Police are searching for a man suspected of shooting another man at a Tucson home before fleeing in his car.

Two other adults in the home were unharmed, police said.

According to police, the suspect showed up at the house and got into an argument with the victim at the front door. The suspect then fired through the door, hitting the victim.

The men were acquainted, but police are still working to confirm the suspect’s identity.
