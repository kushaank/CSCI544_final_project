
The police search for a man missing from Morven, GA. is in its second day, and they are asking for help from the public in finding him.

According to a Mattie's Call, officials are searching for Collin Jankus. He is 45 years old and weighs approximately 180 lbs. He is described as a 6'2" black male with brown eyes and short black hair.

He was last seen at the Uplift Center of Brooks County at 7752 Hwy 133 in Morven.
