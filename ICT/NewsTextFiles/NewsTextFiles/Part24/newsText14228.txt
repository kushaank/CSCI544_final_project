
Monday 2.15pm: Police are appealing for help to track down a missing man, believed to be in the Peterborough area.

Thames Valley Police are trying to locate a 43-year Andrew Lever - better known as Andy - reported missing on Saturday, May 17 from Portway, Banbury and believed to have travelled to the Cambridgeshire area.

He was last seen by family on the evening of Saturday 17 May and is thought to have driven to the Cambridgeshire area in his blue Ford Ka, with a registration plate beginning BW03. He may have been headed towards the Chatteris, or Peterborough area of Cambridgeshire.

He is white, around 5ft 10ins tall, with short brown hair. He has two tattoos, a Japanese symbol on one arm and black and red design on the other. He was last seen wearing a grey t-shirt and black three-quarter length jeans.

Police are concerned for his welfare due to his state of mind when he went missing.

If you have any information that can assist police to locate this missing person, please call the 24-hour non-emergency number 101 and ask to speak to Det Insp Claire Thorngate of Thames Valley Police, quoting URN 1894 17/5.
