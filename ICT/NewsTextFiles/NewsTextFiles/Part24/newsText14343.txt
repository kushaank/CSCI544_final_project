
Police say 48-year-old Graham Adolphus Yearwood and 12-year-old Destany Boyce, who were reported missing, have been traced and they are safe.

Yearwood of Checker Hall, St Lucy went missing on Saturday while Boyce of Acacia Drive, Country View Estates, Christ Church left home sometime between 10:15 p.m. last night, and 12:15 a.m. this morning.
