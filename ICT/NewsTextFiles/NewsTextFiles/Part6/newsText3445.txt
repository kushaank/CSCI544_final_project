
Omaha, Neb. (KMTV) - The Omaha Police Department's Homicide Unit issued a warrant for the arrest of Anthony Burries, for first degree murder. Police arrested Burries Monday night in Odessa, Missouri.

Police went to Tina Hoult's apartment near 98th and Mockingbird early Sunday  morning for a welfare check. They found her murdered. Police have not said how Hoult died, or if she knew Burries.

The Nebraska Department of Corrections website lists Burries as a convicted felon.  Most recently he served time for domestic assault. He was released from prison in November.
