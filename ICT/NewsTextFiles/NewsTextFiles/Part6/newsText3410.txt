
While Officer Curtis Thompson with the Edmond (Oklahoma) Police Department watches, Barbara Buffington of Tulsa practices her sudden stops and starts during a motorcycle safe driving clinic held Saturday, May 3, in Grove.

For several hours on May 3, motorcyclists of all ages gathered at Wolf Creek Park to brush up on their driving skills.

It was all part of the annual Civilian Motorcycle School, led by Sgt. Acey Hooper and officers from the Edmond, Oklahoma Police Department, and hosted by the Grove Police Department.
