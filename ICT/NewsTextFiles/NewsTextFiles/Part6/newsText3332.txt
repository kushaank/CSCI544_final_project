
Jasmine Cameron, 19, of LaGrange was arrested early Saturday morning at the LaGrange Police Department on aggravated assault charges.

Police responded to West Georgia Medical Center at approximately 2:06 a.m. where they spoke with Sebrina Ford, 25, of LaGrange who reported Cameron stabbed her in the chest with a knife at Just Right Sandwich Shop located in the 700 block of Johnson Street.

According to officials, Cameron allegedly stabbed Ford over Cameron’s boyfriend who Ford previously dated.

Ford’s injuries were reportedly minor.

Police called Cameron and asked her to come to the LPD for an interview where they arrested her.

The case is still under investigation.
