
A Pleasant Hill man missing since Friday has been found in Nevada and determined to be in good health, police said Monday.

Police found Jason Adams, 31, inside his off-white 1972 International Harvester pickup truck in a Reno parking lot around 2 a.m. Monday, Pleasant Hill Sgt. Scott Vermillion said.

Adams' family reported him missing on Friday, telling police that he left San Ramon about 9 a.m. to run an errand at a home improvement store in the Pleasanton-Dublin-San Ramon area.

He never returned that night, police said.

Police did not say why Adams disappeared but said he was in good health. The truck he was driving had a long bed in it, police said.
