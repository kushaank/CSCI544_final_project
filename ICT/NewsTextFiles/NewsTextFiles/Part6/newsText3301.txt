
KARACHI,  Majlis-e-Wahdatul Muslimeen (MWM) on Monday announced it would hold nationwide protest on Friday against the killing of Sherazi Imambargah caretaker in Karachi.

A MWM spokesperson said that Shaukat Hussain was caretaker of Sheerazi Imamgarah and was killed in Methadar today. He said that MWM condemned the murder of Shaukat Hussain and his guest Qaiser Hussain.
