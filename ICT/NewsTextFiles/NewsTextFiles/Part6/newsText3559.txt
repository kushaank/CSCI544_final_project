
WEST POINT, Miss. (WTVA) -- A 65-year-old man was arrested Saturday following a shooting that left one injured in West Point.

Police say John Brown was charged with aggravated assault after a man was shot in the leg on Critz Street.

Police are not releasing the victim's name at this time.

Witnesses told police it appeared an altercation between Brown and the man led to the shooting.

The victim was treated and released from the North Mississippi Medical Center in West Point for his injuries.

Brown is currently being held at the Clay County Detention Center on a $10,00 bond.
