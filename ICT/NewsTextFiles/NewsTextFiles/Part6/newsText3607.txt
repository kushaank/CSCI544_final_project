The Virginia State Police Bureau of Criminal Investigation’s Police Shooting Investigative Team out of the Wytheville Field Office is investigating the incident.

According to a news release, the Pearisburg police officer initiated a traffic stop about 3 p.m. During the course of the stop, the driver was shot. He was taken to a nearby hospital where he died a short time later.

The Giles County Sheriff’s Office is also reportedly investigating the incident. Officials did not release the names of the officer and driver. No other details were available.
