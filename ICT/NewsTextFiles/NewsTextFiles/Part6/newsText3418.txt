
The air ambulance landed on Tooting Bec Common after a building collapsed yesterday.

Part of a building, under renovation in Louisville Road, crumpled at about 5pm.

The Metropolitan Police Service also attended the incident and both Louisville Road and Drakefield Road were closed while the area was made safe.

The London Ambulance Service were called but confirmed nobody was injured during the collapse.
