
EDWARDS, Miss. - Authorities say an elderly woman and her granddaughter were found dead inside a home on Jackson Avenue in Edwards.

Police Chief Louis Johnson said it appears that they died from natural causes. Chief Johnson said the person that does yard work at the elderly woman's address couldn't get in contact with her so he got in touch someone else who knew her and she called the police.

Police went to the home and found the bodies and began a death investigation.

No other details have been released.
