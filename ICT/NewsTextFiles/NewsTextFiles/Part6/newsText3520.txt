
Karachi- A forensic report of weapons used in the killing of Suleman Lashari has been obtained.

The accused used three different weapons and fired 26 bullets altogether, while the complainant and his security guards fired 10 shots. Police further said that the accused, Salman Abro fired 12 bullets from the SMG of a policeman, while the killed policeman fired seven shots from his rifle.

Another arrested policeman, Maqbool, fired 7 bullets from his SMG.

The police, on the basis of mobile phone record, claimed that the slain Suleman Lashari and Salman Abro were not friends. Salman Abro, along with a police personnel allegedly broke into the residence of Suleman Lashari and shot him dead. A policeman was also killed while Abro sustained injuries in retaliatory fire.
