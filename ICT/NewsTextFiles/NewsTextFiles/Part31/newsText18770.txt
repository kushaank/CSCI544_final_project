
ISLAMABAD: Central Leader of Pakistan Muslim League Nawaz (PML-N) Siddiqul Farooq on Monday said the government launched various welfare projects to provide relief to the masses.

He pointed out that continuity of democracy was imperative for the economic uplift and development of the country.

Talking to a private TV channel, he said that PML-N government had rendered unprecedented sacrifices for the restoration of democracy in the country.

He said that government was alleviating poverty through different fruitful programmes including Prime Minister's Youth Business loan scheme.

He said the government was making efforts to reduce power loadshedding, adding that projects had been launched to generate electricity from solar, wind, coal and hydro.

He said the elections 2013 were held in free, fair and transparent manner.
