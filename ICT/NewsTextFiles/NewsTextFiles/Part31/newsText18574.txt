BIRMINGHAM, Alabama -- The Birmingham Jefferson County Transit Authority will offer free transportation to its May board meeting in Hoover.

The meeting is scheduled for noon Wednesday, May 28, at Hoover City Hall.

A MAX bus will depart Central Station on Morris Avenue at 11 a.m. that day. The bus will return for Central Station immediately after the meeting, according to BJCTA.

Seating is limited and reservations are required.

To make reservations, contact BJCTA Transportation Manager Ernestine Cobb at (205) 521-0109.
