FLORENCE, Alabama -- Two Sheffield men are in the Lauderdale County Detention Center in connection to an overnight pharmacy burglary, according to Florence police.

Police say Marquis McCray, 25, and Darnell Gilbert, 24, both of Sheffield, broke into Florence Pharmacy on Veterans Drive early Monday. Police responded to an alarm at the business shortly after 4:30 a.m. A K-9 unit led officers to the suspects in a nearby wooded area. Police recovered pills they believe were stolen from the pharmacy.
