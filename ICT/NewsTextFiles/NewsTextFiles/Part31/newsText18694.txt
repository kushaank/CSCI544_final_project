
The Volta Regional Minister, Helen Ntoso.

The Volta Regional Minister, Helen Ntoso has commended the Clergy for their commitment to the socio-economic development of the country.

She urged them to continue partnering government in its developmental efforts.

She said this in Ho at a ceremony to mark the Golden Jubilee of Priesthood of the Catholic Bishop of the Ho Diocese, Most Rev Francis Lodonu.

Madam Ntoso commended Bishop Lodonu for his selfless and dedicated service to his people and to God throughout his 50 years of priesthood.
