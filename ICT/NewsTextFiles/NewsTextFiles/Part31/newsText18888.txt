
ISLAMABAD: Minister for Petroleum and Natural Resources Shahid Khaqan Abbasi said on Monday that positive criticism on government's policies and performance is part of the democracy.

Talking to a private TV channel, he said there were no differences among the party members and if found, would be resolved.

He said the government would remove the reservations of Irfan Ullah Marwat, Member Sindh Assembly soon.

The minister said the government was making efforts to resolve the issues of the common man and launched a number of people's welfare projects.

Shahid Khaqan said the government was making efforts to streamline the system and for this, all available resources were being utilized to develop the country.

He said the PML-N rendered unprecedented sacrifices for restoration of democracy in the country.
