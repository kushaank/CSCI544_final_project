
NeoTurks is India's premier digital ad-tech company that is building innovative data and ad-serving platforms to help clients reach out to premium audiences in India, Asia Pacific, Australia and Middle East through its subsidiaries Lyxel – a data and demand side platform; Spidio – a video ad-tech platform and 1 Flamingo – a social media innovator. NeoTurks was founded by Dev Batra (IIM Lucknow, NIT Surat, Serial Entrepreneur) and Nishith Srivastava (Lucknow University, Serial Entrepreneur). In a very short time, NeoTurks has added several marquee clients across all of its service offerings and currently in the process of raising its Series A investments.
