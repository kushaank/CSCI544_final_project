
Geneva, May19(BNA)Health Minister Sadiq bin Abdelkarim Al-Shehabi is leading Bahrain's delegation to the 67th annual meeting of the World Health Organisation (WHO), which opened today and will run until May26.

The delegation includes Assistant Undersecretary for Primary Healthcare and Public Health Dr. Mariam Adhbi Al-Jalahma, Public Health director Dr. Mariam Al-Mulla Hermas, Financial director Mohammed Al-Attawi,  Infection Control Consultant Dr. Safa and Public Relations Officer Abdulla Makli.

The delegation will attend the meetings of the Arab and GCC and No-aligned Movement (NAM)health ministers on the sidelines of the WHO annual session.
