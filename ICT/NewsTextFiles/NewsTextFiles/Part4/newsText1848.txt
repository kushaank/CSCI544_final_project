A man who was shot by police in Manhattan over the weekend is now facing charges.

Michael Schultz is charged with attempted assault on a police officer with a deadly weapon and criminal weapons possession.

Police answered a call of a disorderly group gathered outside the Washington Houses in East Harlem yesterday morning.

The NYPD says the officers were questioning Schultz when he ran away.

They chased him and opened fire after they say he pointed a gun at them.

Schultz was hit multiple times and was taken to the hospital in critical condition.

A handgun was recovered at the scene.

No one else was injured in the shooting.
