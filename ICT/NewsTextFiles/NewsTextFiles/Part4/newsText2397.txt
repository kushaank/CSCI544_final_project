
“There is a need to develop fundamental documents and conventions on IDPs”.

Azerbaijani Deputy PM, Chairman of the State Committee for Work with Refugees and IDPs, Ali Hasanov has today received the UN Special Rapporteur on the human rights of internally displaced persons, Chaloka Beyani.

The meeting was attended by Assistant to the UN Special Rapporteur on the human rights of internally displaced persons Marie-Eve Friedrich, Head of representation of UN High Commissioner for Refugees (UNHCR) in Azerbaijan Dag Sigurdson and Head of representation of UN High Commissioner for Human Rights in Azerbaijan Kamran Bagirov.

Hasanov expressed his confidence that Chaloka Beyani’s visit to the region would produce results.

Touching upon the Nagorno-Karabakh conflict, Hasanov noted that Azerbaijan-Armenia conflict has 200-year history.
