Ten GE transportation locomotives started a lengthy trip to Africa today.The locomotives have been stored at Erie Sand and Gravel since April.

BBC Chartering is the shipping company that will bring these ten locomotives plus another forty to Mozambique. Officials from Erie Sand and Gravel say this is their busiest shipping season in the last twenty years.
