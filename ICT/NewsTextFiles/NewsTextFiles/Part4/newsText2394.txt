
“What will OSCE Minsk Group Co-Chairs do in Baku?” Azerbaijani media wonders.

The mediators are expected to meet with president and foreign ministry, but they had left for a visit to Vietnam on Monday together with deputy chief of the presidential administration Novruz Mamedov, the agency said. On Tuesday Azerbaijani leader will arrive in China.
