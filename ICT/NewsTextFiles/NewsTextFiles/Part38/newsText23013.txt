
At least 10 Zimbabweans perished on Monday when a minibus hit a tree near the capital Harare, a police spokesperson said.Paul Nyathi said the deceased were travelling from Harare to Chitungwiza when the minibus veered off the road and smashed into the tree.

The driver and six passengers perished on the spot while three others died on their way to hospital.

Eyewitnesses said the accident was caused by a tyre burst that forced the driver to lose control of the vehicle.
