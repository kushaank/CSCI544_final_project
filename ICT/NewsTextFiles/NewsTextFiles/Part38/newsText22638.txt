
An article saying President Barack Obama has announced plans to run for a third presidential term is fake.

According to a disclaimer, National Report doesn’t post real news stories.

“National Report is a news and political satire web publication, which may or may not use real names, often in semi-real or mostly fictitious ways. All news articles contained within National Report are fiction, and presumably fake news. Any resemblance to the truth is purely coincidental,” it states. The disclaimer was taken down, but it’s accessible via Archive.org.

The satirical article about Obama’s third term had thousands of “likes” and shares on Facebook. A number of people apparently believed it.
