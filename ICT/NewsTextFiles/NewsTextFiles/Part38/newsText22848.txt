
Police just over the border in Enfield, CT, are looking for an armed robbery suspect Monday night that was last seen heading into Western Massachusetts.

Enfield police tell CBS 3 that Northern Lights Smoke Shop on Rt. 5 in Enfield was robbed at gunpoint at about 9 p.m.

Employees there described the suspect as a white man wearing a black mask.

Just minutes later and right down the street, Steve's Boston Seafood was robbed by a suspect matching the same description.

Cash was taken from both businesses.

The driver was last seen driving a green Jeep towards Longmeadow.

Anyone with information is asked to call the Enfield Police Department at 860-763-8911.
