
LAMJUNG, MAY 12 -   Construction work on the Upper Marsyangdi A Hydropower Project in Lamjung district has come to a halt from Monday due to the strike of trade union workers.

The project work was halted after the trade union workers took to agitation to press for their three-point demands. The workers resorted to the strike after the talks held until late into the night on Sunday ended inconclusively.

The workers had halted work at the project prior to this.
