
LEBANON — Lebanon Toastmasters invite the public to celebrate their first anniversary with them with an official chartering ceremony tonight.

The event will be 5:45 to 7:15 p.m. tonight at the Elks Lodge, 633 Park St.

Impromptu “Table Topics” speeches are scheduled, with prizes for guests. Club leaders will give presentations. Raffles and refreshments will be a part of the evening.

For more information, see the group’s Facebook page at Facebook.com/LebanonOregonToastmasters/ .
