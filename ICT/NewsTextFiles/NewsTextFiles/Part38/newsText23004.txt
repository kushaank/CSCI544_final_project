
WASHINGTON, May 18 (Xinhua) -- The United States on Sunday offered its condolences to Laos over a plane crash that killed 16 people including cabinet ministers.

"We are deeply saddened to hear the news of a plane crash in Laos, which claimed the lives of government officials and others on board the flight," State Department spokeswoman Jennifer Psaki said in a statement.

A deputy prime minister who was also the minister of defense, the minister of public security and the mayor of Vientiane, Laos' capital, were among the dead.

"We extend our deepest condolences to the government of Laos," Psaki said.

The plane crashed on Saturday morning in Laos' northeastern province of Xieng Khouang.
