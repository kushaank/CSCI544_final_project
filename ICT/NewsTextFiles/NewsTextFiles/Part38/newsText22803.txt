
SEOUL, May 19 (Yonhap) -- Hyundai Motor Co., South Korea's largest carmaker, ranked No. 1 in terms of customer loyalty engagement in the United States, a brand consultancy firm index monitored in Seoul showed Monday.

According to Brand Keys based in New York, Hyundai came in first alongside Ford in the 2014 loyalty index.

Mercedes-Benz, Lexus and Jeep came in a joint second, with Subaru and BMW ranking third. Toyota came in fourth and GM made the top five list.

Brand Keys said its index is focused on predictive consumer behavior on brand equity that can help determine loyalty to a particular brand in the future.

It has been conducting such research since 1997. For the 2014 year, it evaluated 555 brands and surveyed 32,000 U.S. consumers.
