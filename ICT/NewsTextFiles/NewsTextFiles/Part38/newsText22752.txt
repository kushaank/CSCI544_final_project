
An accidental insulator explosion at a subway south of Seoul has injured 11 people. An investigation is underway into the exact cause of the blast.

SEOUL, May 19 (UPI) -- Eleven people were injured Monday by an explosion on a subway train south of Seoul.

The blast occurred at 6:56 p.m., wounding 11 people waiting on the platform of Geumjeong Station in Gunpo.

An official with Korea Railroad said that while the investigation is ongoing into the cause of the explosion, it appears to have been accidental.

KORAIL, the official said, suspects "the accident occurred as an insulator connecting the train to the electric supply equipment suddenly exploded."
