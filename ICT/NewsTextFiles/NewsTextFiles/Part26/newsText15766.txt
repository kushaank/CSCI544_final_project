
Evolve Bank & Trust has announced that Al Bright Jr. has accepted a position on the bank's board of directors.

Bright is a law partner in the Memphis office of Waller Lansden Dortch & Davis, where he practices business law. He is also the current chairman of board for the Economic Development Growth Engine for Memphis and Shelby County (EDGE), and its related entities, which include the Industrial Development Board of Memphis and Shelby County.

Memphis-based Evolve Bank & Trust currently operates six full-service branches and loan production offices in Arkansas and Tennessee and more than 30 loan production offices across the country.
