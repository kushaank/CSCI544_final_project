
Lee County voters, particularly those in Supervisor Districts 2 and 3, will have a chance to meet and question candidates in three contested primary races. The Chamber of Commerce’s Government Affairs Committee and the Daily Democrat are sponsoring two candidate forums — one for democratic nomination for Lee County Treasurer and one for the democratic nominations for District 2 and District 3 seats on the Lee County Board of Supervisors. Both forums, slated for Wednesday, May 21, and Thursday, May 22, will be at 6 p.m. at the Palm’s restaurant in Fort Madison. Representatives from the Chamber of Commerce and Fort Madison Partners will moderate the forums.
