
The police are seeking the public's assistance in locating the relatives of 35-year-old Kurt Diame Ellington of New Haven, Duhaney Park, Kingston 20.

Reports from the Duhaney Park police are that Ellington was shot and killed by unknown assailants about 10:55 p.m., on Monday, May 5, on Bunyan Crescent in the community. However, the body remains unclaimed.

From information received, Ellington has two aunts known only as 'Gloria' and 'Ms Taylor', and two cousins, known as 'Chris' and 'Robert', who all reside in the United States.

Anyone with information that can assist the police in locating the relatives of Kurt Ellington is being asked to contact the Duhaney Park police at 933-4280 or police 119 emergency number.
