
GAINESVILLE, Fla., May 19, 2014 /PRNewswire/ -- Gainesville attorneys, Avera & Smith, LLP, announce the receipt of a $664,328.42 verdict, awarded in the Circuit Court of Alachua County, Florida on May 6, 2014.  Lance Avera, partner at Avera & Smith, represented Plaintiff, L'Tonya Johnson, for injuries sustained in a motor vehicle crash with Defendant, Jeremy Wilson, who was working for Defendant Southeastern Building Services, Inc. when the crash occurred.
