Flaum Management Co., developer of the proposed $300 million E23 casino and resort in Albany, New York, has partnered with the Chickasaw Nation of Oklahoma to manage the casino.

"Global Gaming Solutions, a commercial gaming business of the Chickasaw Nation, is participating in the ... process regarding the development of the E23 project. We look forward to sharing specific project details with the mayor's office, the common council, county leaders and all local residents in the very near future," said John Elliott , chief executive officer of Global Gaming Solutions LLC.

A spokesman for Flaum Management of Rochester said information would be released soon.

Albany Mayor Kathy Sheehan said met with representatives of Global Gaming Solutions. She also met with representatives of Och-Ziff Capital Management Group about the Albany casino. It's her understanding Och-Ziff, a private equity fund in Manhattan with $43.5 billion of assets under management, would provide financing for the project.

An entity called NYS Funding LLC by Och-Ziff Real Estate paid the state's $1 million mandatory license application fee.
