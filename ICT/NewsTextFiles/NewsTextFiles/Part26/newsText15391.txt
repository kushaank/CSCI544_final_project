
A security guard was on Sunday night beheaded by an armed gang that attacked Gatunduri Day Secondary school in Embu West before making away with computers.

The 55 year old man was decapitated using a machete.

His colleague is currently recuperating at Embu Level Five Hospital where he was rushed after the night attack.

Area Chief Jacob Kamongo says the gangsters raided the school at around 3am, before attacking the two and stealing 15 computers and other items from the school lab.

Elsewhere, 45 year old watchman was also hacked to death after a seven man gang attacked Gikuuri Day Secondary School stealing books and other items.

Residents in the area now want security tightened to prevent such incidents in the future.
